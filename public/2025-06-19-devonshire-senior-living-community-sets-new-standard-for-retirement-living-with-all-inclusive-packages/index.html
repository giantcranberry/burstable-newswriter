Devonshire Senior Living Community Sets New Standard for Retirement Living with All-Inclusive Packages

Newswriter Business and Technology News • June 19, 2025
Read Original Article →

Summary

Sue and Paul Brands found their ideal retirement living at Devonshire, an Erickson Senior Living community in Palm Beach Gardens, Fla., attracted by its predictable costs, comprehensive amenities, and the peace of mind it offers. The couple highlights the community's all-inclusive monthly service package, which covers utilities, maintenance, access to amenities, and more, as a key factor in their decision. This package aligns with the desires of over 90% of seniors for predictable monthly expenses, as per a survey by Erickson Senior Living. Additionally, Devonshire residents enjoy unique perks like membership at the PGA National, enhancing their retirement lifestyle. The community's recent updates and the assurance of a 'Home for Life' policy, ensuring no resident is displaced due to financial hardship, further underscore the value and security Devonshire offers to its residents.

Full Article

The Devonshire senior living community at PGA National in Palm Beach Gardens, Fla., is redefining retirement living by offering an all-inclusive monthly service package that covers everything from utilities to access to world-class amenities. This model not only provides residents like Sue and Paul Brands with a predictable cost structure but also liberates them from the unforeseen expenses associated with homeownership, such as maintenance and rising insurance costs.

A recent survey by Erickson Senior Living underscores the importance of predictable monthly expenses for seniors, with over 90% prioritizing this aspect in their retirement planning. Devonshire's approach directly addresses this need, offering a blend of financial stability and luxury living. Residents enjoy membership at the prestigious PGA National, which includes golf courses, tennis courts, and pickleball courts, adding significant value to their lifestyle.

Executive Director Brad Andrus highlights the community's ongoing commitment to facility upgrades and maintenance, ensuring a high-quality living environment. Recent renovations to the clubhouse and residence buildings exemplify this dedication, fostering a modern and comfortable atmosphere for residents. The active and engaged lifestyle promoted by Devonshire is evident in the wide range of activities available, from fitness centers to educational lectures and social events.

Perhaps most notably, Devonshire's Home for Life program offers residents unparalleled peace of mind by guaranteeing that no one will face eviction due to financial hardships. Additionally, the option for a 90% refundable entrance fee provides further financial security for residents and their beneficiaries. For many, like Sue and Paul, Devonshire represents not just a residence but a secure and enriching way to enjoy retirement.
