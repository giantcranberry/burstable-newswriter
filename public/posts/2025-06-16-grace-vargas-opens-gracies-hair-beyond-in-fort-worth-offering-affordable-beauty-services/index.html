Grace Vargas Opens Gracie's Hair & Beyond in Fort Worth, Offering Affordable Beauty Services

Building Texas Show News • June 16, 2025
Read Original Article →

Summary

Grace Vargas, a licensed cosmetologist, has opened a new salon called Gracie's Hair & Beyond in Fort Worth, Texas. She offers a wide range of beauty services at affordable prices, aiming to make beauty accessible to all.

Full Article

Grace Vargas, a dedicated licensed cosmetologist, has recently opened Gracie's Hair & Beyond at the Salon and Spa Galleria Oakmont Boulevard in Fort Worth, Texas. This new establishment marks a significant addition to the local beauty industry, offering a wide range of professional hair and beauty services designed to meet the diverse needs of the community. Vargas's journey into the beauty industry was inspired by her family's involvement in the field, shaping her commitment to delivering services that are not only high in quality but also affordable.

With her training from the Ogle Beauty School of Hair, Skin and Nails, Vargas brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to her salon. Services offered include precision haircuts, a variety of color treatments, special event styling, and makeup application. Vargas's salon stands out for its dedication to creating personalized looks that enhance each client's unique style and personality, ensuring that everyone leaves feeling confident and beautiful.

To commemorate the grand opening, Gracie's Hair & Beyond is introducing promotional discounts, offering clients the opportunity to save between $10 to $20 on services. The salon's operating hours are from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m., providing flexibility for clients. Additionally, the location boasts free parking and is handicap accessible, further emphasizing Vargas's commitment to making beauty services accessible to all.

Vargas's vision extends beyond the technical aspects of beauty services; she aims to foster a welcoming environment where clients can feel valued and empowered. By maintaining affordable pricing, Gracie's Hair & Beyond ensures that professional hair and makeup services are within reach for a wider audience, potentially setting a new standard for inclusivity in the beauty industry. This initiative not only benefits the local community by providing access to quality beauty services but also contributes to the economic vitality of Fort Worth by supporting small businesses and creating jobs.
