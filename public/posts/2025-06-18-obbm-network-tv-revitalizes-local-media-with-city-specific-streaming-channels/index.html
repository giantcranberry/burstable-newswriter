OBBM Network TV Revitalizes Local Media with City-Specific Streaming Channels

Building Texas Show News • June 18, 2025
Read Original Article →

Summary

Television viewing is on the upswing after a 4-year decline, driven by changes in viewer habits and the desire for 'comfort' programming. The Trusted News Initiative's influence on traditional TV content has led to a void in thought leadership, which OBBM Network TV aims to fill with local, wholesome entertainment and government information across 330 U.S. cities. The network's expansion into local markets aims to support local economies and provide viewers with content that applies to their location.

Full Article

The television industry is witnessing a significant transformation as OBBM Network TV introduces city-specific streaming channels across the United States. This initiative responds to the shifting viewing habits of audiences who are increasingly looking for content that reflects their local communities and diverse viewpoints, moving away from traditional media sources.

Recent trends indicate a resurgence in television viewership, attributed to a desire for 'comfort' programming and changing lifestyle preferences. OBBM Network TV is capitalizing on this shift by addressing a critical gap in the market: the demand for alternative perspectives, especially among conservative viewers, who feel underserved by the homogenized content of mainstream media.

Larry Kortkamp of OBBM Network TV emphasizes the network's mission to provide a platform that strengthens conservative voices without resorting to divisiveness. The network's strategy focuses on fostering local confidence by delivering content that covers local government, businesses, and community events, thereby rebuilding trust in media at the grassroots level.

Starting in Q1 2024 with 18 local channels in Dallas-Fort Worth, OBBM Network TV has expanded to Florida's Space Coast, Houston, and Austin, with plans to reach 330 cities nationwide. The network's offerings include local news, civics education, family-oriented entertainment, and conservative commentary, all while supporting local economies through targeted advertising.

Available on popular streaming platforms like Roku, AppleTV, and Amazon Fire TV Stick, OBBM Network TV's channels blend local and national programming. This innovative approach not only caters to the preferences of conservative viewers but also has the potential to redefine media consumption and engagement in communities across the country.

The implications of OBBM Network TV's expansion are profound. By prioritizing local content and diverse viewpoints, the network could inspire a broader movement towards media localization and content diversity. This model may prompt other media entities to explore similar strategies, leading to a more decentralized and varied media ecosystem that better serves the needs and interests of all Americans.
