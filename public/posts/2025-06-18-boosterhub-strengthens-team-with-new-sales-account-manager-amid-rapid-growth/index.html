BoosterHub Strengthens Team with New Sales Account Manager Amid Rapid Growth

Building Texas Show News • June 18, 2025
Read Original Article →

Summary

BoosterHub, a software app for high school booster clubs, has appointed Aidan Gildea as their new Sales Account Manager. With a background in fostering client relationships and experience in managing club lacrosse programs, Gildea is set to make a significant impact on the BoosterHub team. The company expects to double in size in 2025.

Full Article

BoosterHub, a pioneering software application aimed at simplifying operations for high school booster clubs, has recently announced the addition of Aidan Gildea to its team as the new Sales Account Manager. This move comes at a time when the company is witnessing unprecedented growth, with more than 200 booster clubs adopting its platform in 2024 alone and projections to double this number by 2025. Gildea's appointment is a strategic step towards bolstering the company's sales efforts and enhancing its service offerings to booster clubs across the nation.

Gildea's unique background, including his experience as a member of the Southwestern University Lacrosse team and his involvement in managing club lacrosse programs, equips him with a profound understanding of the challenges and needs of booster clubs. His firsthand experience with the pivotal role these clubs play in supporting student-athletes and fine arts programs positions him as an invaluable asset to BoosterHub. 'I am thrilled to join the BoosterHub team and contribute to empowering booster clubs with efficient software tools. As a former athlete, I understand how vital these clubs are in providing support to student-athletes and artists alike,' Gildea remarked on his new role.

Robin Eissler, Founder and CEO of BoosterHub, expressed excitement about Gildea's joining, stating, 'We are excited to have Aidan join our team. It will help us meet our goal of saving parent volunteers time and maximizing their impact on their programs.' BoosterHub's platform is designed to streamline essential booster club functions such as communication, fundraising, financial management, and e-commerce, all through a user-friendly interface. This not only saves valuable time for volunteers but also allows them to allocate more resources towards enriching extracurricular programs.

The timing of Gildea's appointment is particularly noteworthy as high schools nationwide grapple with budget limitations, making the support from booster clubs more crucial than ever. BoosterHub's rapid expansion and its focus on recruiting professionals with relevant experience underscore the company's dedication to meeting the evolving needs of these volunteer-driven organizations. This development highlights the increasing demand for specialized software solutions in the education sector and the potential of technology to transform the operational efficiency of booster clubs.

Furthermore, Gildea's appointment reflects the importance of having team members who possess a deep, personal understanding of the sector they serve. His background is expected to enhance BoosterHub's ability to connect with and address the specific challenges faced by booster clubs. As BoosterHub continues to grow and refine its platform, the implications for high school extracurricular programs are significant. By facilitating more effective management of booster clubs, the company is playing a pivotal role in supporting a wide array of student activities, thereby enriching the educational experience for students across the country.
