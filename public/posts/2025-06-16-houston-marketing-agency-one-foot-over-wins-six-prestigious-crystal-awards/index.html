Houston Marketing Agency One Foot Over Wins Six Prestigious Crystal Awards

Building Texas Show News • June 16, 2025
Read Original Article →

Summary

One Foot Over, a Houston-based B2B marketing agency, received six awards at the 2025 American Marketing Association Houston Crystal Awards for their exceptional work in marketing and communications. The awards highlight One Foot Over's commitment to helping complex B2B brands connect with their target audiences and drive business impact.

Full Article

One Foot Over, a Houston-based B2B marketing agency, has recently been celebrated for its exceptional contributions to the marketing industry by securing six Crystal Awards from the American Marketing Association (AMA) Houston chapter. This recognition underscores the agency's ability to deliver strategic, creative, and effective marketing solutions that drive significant business outcomes for their clients.

The awards were distributed across various categories, highlighting the agency's versatility and expertise in areas such as website design, landing page creation, and comprehensive marketing campaigns. Notably, One Foot Over's work for Cemex U.S. earned them four awards, including accolades in B2B Marketing Campaign, New Market Segment, Account-Based Marketing, and Social Responsibility Campaign. These achievements reflect the agency's commitment to not only meeting but exceeding the marketing needs of their clients in sectors like technology, industrial manufacturing, and sustainability.

Stephen Brent May, Co-Founder of One Foot Over, shared that the awards are a testament to the team's dedication and the tangible impact of their work on clients' businesses. This sentiment was echoed by AMA Houston President Jill Manty, who praised the winning entries for their demonstration of marketing's power to achieve strategic and measurable results.

The recognition of One Foot Over at the Crystal Awards is significant not only for the agency but also for the broader marketing industry in Texas. It highlights the importance of strategic, results-driven marketing in today's competitive business environment and sets a benchmark for excellence that other agencies and professionals can aspire to. For businesses in Texas and beyond, the success of One Foot Over serves as a reminder of the value of partnering with marketing agencies that prioritize creativity, strategy, and measurable impact in their campaigns.
