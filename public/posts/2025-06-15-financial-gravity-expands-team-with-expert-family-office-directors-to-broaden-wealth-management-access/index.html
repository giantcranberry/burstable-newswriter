Financial Gravity Expands Team with Expert Family Office Directors to Broaden Wealth Management Access

Building Texas Show News • June 15, 2025
Read Original Article →

Summary

Financial Gravity welcomes Lauren Fischer and David Fischer as new Family Office Directors. Lauren brings over a decade of finance experience, while David is a passionate Financial Planner. Together, they aim to provide holistic financial planning solutions for clients. As Family Office Directors, they will work with Financial Gravity to prescribe advanced tax solutions and ensure clients' financial success.

Full Article

Financial Gravity Companies, Inc. has taken a significant step forward in its mission to democratize family office services by welcoming Lauren and Dave Fischer as new Family Office Directors. This strategic addition underscores the company's commitment to expanding its multi-family office service offerings, making advanced tax solutions and comprehensive wealth management strategies accessible to a broader audience.

Lauren Fischer, with her extensive background in financial planning and investment management, including roles at SALI Fund Services and Artemis Capital Management, brings a wealth of knowledge and the prestigious Chartered Alternative Investment Analyst certification to the team. Her recent achievement of the CERTIFIED FINANCIAL PLANNER™ designation further solidifies her expertise in the field. Dave Fischer, equally accomplished and also a CERTIFIED FINANCIAL PLANNER™, shares a deep commitment to client-focused financial planning, honed through his experiences at 49 Financial and Grace Capital Management.

Together, as co-founders of Team Fischer Financial, Lauren and Dave Fischer are set to leverage Financial Gravity's innovative platform to offer holistic financial solutions. Their approach integrates various financial disciplines, including investments, tax planning, retirement, estate management, and philanthropy, providing families with a comprehensive and efficient wealth management experience.

Scott Winters, CEO of Financial Gravity, emphasized the strategic importance of the Fischers' addition to the team. Their expertise not only aligns with the company's mission but also enhances its capability to deliver sophisticated financial services traditionally reserved for high-net-worth individuals to a wider demographic. This move represents a pivotal shift in the financial services industry, from a focus on traditional tax compliance to a more integrated, client-centered approach.

The implications of this expansion are significant for both the industry and families seeking comprehensive financial planning. By breaking down barriers to access advanced wealth management techniques, Financial Gravity is setting a new standard for financial services, promising a more inclusive and holistic approach to financial planning for families across Texas and beyond.
