Boerne's Das GreenHaus Incubator Wins Prestigious 2024 Community Economic Development Award

Building Texas Show News • June 18, 2025
Read Original Article →

Summary

The Boerne Kendall County Economic Development Corporation (BKCEDC) has been awarded the 2024 Community Economic Development Award (CEDA) from the Southern Economic Development Council (SEDC) for its role in launching Das GreenHaus, Boerne’s first business incubator. The award marks a significant achievement for BKCEDC and recognizes the collaborative efforts of the organization, the Boerne Kendall County Angel Network (BKCAN), and community leaders. Das GreenHaus, a 501(c)(3) public-private nonprofit, has made a substantial impact in just over a year, empowering aspiring entrepreneurs and fostering small business growth in the Texas Hill Country.

Full Article

The Boerne Kendall County Economic Development Corporation (BKCEDC) has achieved a significant milestone with its Das GreenHaus business incubator being honored with the 2024 Community Economic Development Award (CEDA) by the Southern Economic Development Council (SEDC). This accolade recognizes the incubator's exceptional contribution to nurturing startups and entrepreneurs in the Texas Hill Country, marking a pivotal moment for local economic development efforts.

Since its inception in June 2023, Das GreenHaus has emerged as a vital resource for over 50 founders, offering them a comprehensive Idea-to-Market curriculum, mentorship, and access to essential resources. The incubator's success stories, including the Waste-to-Energy climate technology company LOCOAL's decision to establish its headquarters in the region, underscore its role in attracting innovative businesses and fostering economic diversification.

The award not only celebrates the achievements of BKCEDC and its partners but also shines a light on the effectiveness of public-private partnerships in driving sustainable economic growth. By engaging with the community through educational programs and networking events, Das GreenHaus is building a robust entrepreneurial ecosystem that promises long-term benefits for Kendall County and beyond.

This recognition serves as a testament to the potential of targeted economic development strategies in smaller communities. It demonstrates how tailored support and collaboration can transform rural and suburban areas into vibrant hubs of innovation and entrepreneurship. The success of Das GreenHaus offers valuable insights for other regions aiming to stimulate their local economies through similar initiatives.

As Das GreenHaus continues to expand its impact, its contributions to the Texas Hill Country's economic resilience and diversification are expected to grow. The incubator's focus on fostering a culture of innovation not only attracts talent and investment but also positions the region for sustained economic success in the years to come.
