Andalusia Whiskey Co. Wins World's Best Small Batch Single Malt, Elevating Texas Distilleries on Global Stage

Building Texas Show News • June 16, 2025
Read Original Article →

Summary

Andalusia Whiskey Co., a distillery in Blanco, Texas, has been awarded World’s Best Small Batch Single Malt at the 2025 World Whiskies Awards for their Stryker Single Malt finished in Madeira casks. The award recognizes the rise of American Single Malt on the global stage, with Texas leading the movement.

The Stryker expression, known for its bold Texas character, impressed judges with its unique depth and complexity after aging in casks from Haak Winery. Co-Founders Ty Phelps and Tommy Erwin expressed pride in the recognition, highlighting the distillery's commitment to Texas-grown whiskey.

As American Single Malt gains traction internationally, Andalusia Whiskey Co.'s win signifies a new chapter in American whiskey, showcasing the innovation and passion driving the craft distilling industry.

Full Article

The recent accolade bestowed upon Andalusia Whiskey Co., a grain-to-glass distillery nestled in the Texas Hill Country, has not only spotlighted the distillery's exceptional craftsmanship but also underscored the burgeoning reputation of Texas distilleries on the international whiskey stage. Winning the World's Best Small Batch Single Malt at the 2025 World Whiskies Awards for their Stryker Single Malt, finished in Madeira casks from Haak Winery, Andalusia has set a new benchmark for quality and innovation in the whiskey industry.

This victory is a testament to the distillery's unwavering commitment to grain-to-glass production, where every step of the process, from milling the grain to aging the spirit under the vast Texas sky, is meticulously carried out on-site. The Stryker Single Malt, with its rich smoked malt and distinctive finish from local Madeira casks, embodies the essence of Texas terroir, offering a flavor profile that is as unique as it is complex.

Beyond the personal achievement for Andalusia Whiskey Co., this award signifies a pivotal moment for the American Single Malt category. As co-founder Ty Phelps pointed out, the recognition is a collective win for all Texas distilleries, showcasing the potential for excellence when innovation, passion, and a deep connection to place converge. This international acclaim not only elevates Andalusia's stature but also shines a global spotlight on the creativity and skill of American craft distillers, particularly those in Texas.

The timing of this award could not be more auspicious, as the American Single Malt category is gaining momentum, transitioning from a niche market to a recognized and respected segment of the global whiskey industry. Texas distilleries, with their distinctive approaches and commitment to quality, are at the forefront of this evolution. Andalusia Whiskey Co.'s achievement is a clear indicator of the sophistication and quality that Texas and American craft distilleries bring to the table, promising exciting developments for whiskey enthusiasts worldwide.
