Empowered Essence Salon Opens in Georgetown, Offering Personalized Hair Care and Empowerment

Building Texas Show News • June 16, 2025
Read Original Article →

Summary

Kristinia, the visionary behind Empowered Essence, brings 15 years of hairstyling expertise to Hill Country Salon Suites in Georgetown, TX. Her focus on all hair textures and personalized care reflects a commitment to enhancing natural beauty and empowering clients. Empowered Essence is more than just a hair salon; it's a sanctuary where clients of all ages leave feeling confident and refreshed.

Full Article

Empowered Essence, a newly opened hair salon at Hill Country Salon Suites in Georgetown, Texas, is setting a new standard for personalized hair care and client empowerment. Founded by Kristinia, a hairstylist with 15 years of experience across New Orleans, East Texas, and Austin, the salon specializes in catering to all hair textures with a focus on transformative beauty treatments that enhance confidence and self-assurance.

The salon's comprehensive service menu includes haircuts for children and adults, master coloring, hair extensions, Brazilian blow-dry treatments, curly perms, wash and blowout services, customized facials, and scalp treatments. Each service is performed with meticulous attention to detail, underscoring Kristinia's dedication to client satisfaction and personalized care.

Located within Hill Country Salon Suites, Empowered Essence benefits from being part of a facility that offers twenty-six affordable luxury salon spaces designed for entrepreneurial professionals. Melanie, a representative of Hill Country Salon Suites, highlights the facility's supportive environment, treating tenants as family and committed to their success.

Operating Tuesday through Saturday, Empowered Essence provides flexible scheduling to meet the diverse needs of its clients. The salon's philosophy revolves around celebrating individual beauty and empowering clients through high-quality, personalized hair care services. This approach not only meets the growing demand for customized beauty solutions but also contributes to the local economy by supporting small businesses and fostering entrepreneurship in the beauty industry.

The opening of Empowered Essence is significant for the Georgetown community and the broader Texas economy, as it represents the growing trend of businesses that prioritize personalized services and client empowerment. By focusing on individual needs and fostering a supportive environment, Empowered Essence is poised to make a lasting impact on the beauty industry and the lives of its clients.
