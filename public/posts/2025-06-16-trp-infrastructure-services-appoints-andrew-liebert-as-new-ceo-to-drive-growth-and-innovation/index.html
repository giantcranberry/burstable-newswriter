TRP Infrastructure Services Appoints Andrew Liebert as New CEO to Drive Growth and Innovation

Building Texas Show News • June 16, 2025
Read Original Article →

Summary

TRP Infrastructure Services, a leader in roadway marking and traffic control solutions, has announced the appointment of Andrew Liebert as its new Chief Executive Officer. Liebert, with over two decades of experience in the highway construction and traffic safety industries, is set to lead TRP's strategic growth initiatives. This transition sees co-founder Rod Pekurney moving to a Senior Advisor role, ensuring continuity and leveraging his deep understanding of the company's culture and operations. TRP, a portfolio company of Arlington Capital Partners, is poised for expansion under Liebert's leadership, aiming to enhance its service offerings and meet the evolving needs of its customers across the Southeast U.S.

The appointment underscores TRP's commitment to maintaining its high standards of customer service and operational excellence while pursuing growth. Arlington Capital Partners, a private investment firm with a focus on government-regulated industries, has expressed confidence in Liebert's ability to drive TRP forward. With a strong foundation and a clear vision for the future, TRP Infrastructure Services is set to continue its trajectory as a best-in-class provider of critical roadway services.

Full Article

TRP Infrastructure Services, a Fort Worth-based leader in providing safety-critical roadway marking and traffic control solutions, has announced Andrew Liebert as its new Chief Executive Officer. This leadership change marks a significant moment for the company as it seeks to build on its strong foundation and explore new growth opportunities. Liebert, with his extensive experience in the highway construction and traffic safety industries, is expected to lead TRP into its next phase of expansion and innovation.

Andrew Liebert's appointment comes at a time when the infrastructure services sector is witnessing rapid changes, driven by increasing demands for safety and efficiency in roadway projects. His background in leading growth initiatives and strategic acquisitions positions him uniquely to address these challenges and opportunities. Liebert's focus on employee safety, customer service, and operational excellence aligns with TRP's commitment to quality and safety, making him an ideal leader for the company's future endeavors.

The transition also sees Rod Pekurney, co-founder and former CEO, moving into a Senior Advisor role. Pekurney's continued involvement ensures that the company benefits from his deep industry knowledge and leadership experience. The partnership with Arlington Capital Partners since 2021 has been a key factor in TRP's growth trajectory, and Liebert's leadership is expected to further this momentum.

TRP Infrastructure Services plays a crucial role in the Southeast U.S. infrastructure landscape, providing essential pavement marking, traffic control, and guardrail solutions. Under Liebert's guidance, the company is poised to enhance its service offerings and strengthen its market position. This leadership transition is not just a milestone for TRP but also a significant development for the infrastructure services industry in Texas and beyond, highlighting the importance of innovation and leadership in meeting the evolving needs of the sector.
