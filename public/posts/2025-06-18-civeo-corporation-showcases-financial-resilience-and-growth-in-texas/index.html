Civeo Corporation Showcases Financial Resilience and Growth in Texas

Building Texas Show News • June 18, 2025
Read Original Article →

Summary

Stonegate Capital Partners updates their coverage on Civeo Corporation, noting consistent positive free cash flow generation. In 3Q24, CVEO produced $28.3M in FCF and updated its full-year 2024 guidance to $45.0M-$50.0M. The Canadian segment faced revenue challenges due to the wind-down of LNG activities, while the Australian segment saw strong growth.

Full Article

Civeo Corporation, a notable player in the Texas business landscape, has recently reported a continuation of its impressive financial performance, as detailed in Stonegate Capital Partners' updated coverage. The company's ability to generate positive free cash flow consistently since 2014, including $28.3 million in Q3 2024, underscores its financial stability and dedication to enhancing shareholder value. This performance is particularly significant for Texas, where Civeo's operations contribute to the state's economic vitality.

In response to its strong financial health, Civeo has adjusted its full-year 2024 guidance upwards, with the lower end of its free cash flow range now set at $50.0 million, up from $45.0 million, and the midpoint increased to $55.0 million from $52.5 million. This adjustment reflects the company's confidence in its ongoing financial success and its potential to further impact the Texas economy positively.

The third quarter of 2024 saw Civeo surpassing revenue expectations, reporting $176.3 million against estimates of $161.1 million by Stonegate and $168.8 million consensus. This achievement was largely fueled by the Australian segment's robust performance, despite challenges in the Canadian market. Such resilience highlights Civeo's strategic adaptability and its role as a key economic driver in Texas.

Civeo's commitment to shareholder returns is evident through its share repurchase program and debt reduction efforts, alongside a declared quarterly cash dividend of $0.25 per share. These actions not only demonstrate the company's financial health but also its contribution to the broader economic ecosystem in Texas, where it operates.

Looking forward, Civeo has set its sights on maintaining this trajectory, with projected revenues between $675 million and $700 million for the full year 2024. The company's strategic focus on financial health and shareholder value, coupled with its ability to navigate market challenges, positions it as a model for economic resilience and growth in Texas and the global market.
