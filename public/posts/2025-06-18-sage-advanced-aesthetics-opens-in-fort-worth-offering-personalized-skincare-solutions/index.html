Sage Advanced Aesthetics Opens in Fort Worth, Offering Personalized Skincare Solutions

Building Texas Show News • June 18, 2025
Read Original Article →

Summary

Full Article

Veronica Lizalde, a licensed esthetician who turned her personal battle with acne into a professional passion, has recently opened Sage Advanced Aesthetics in Fort Worth. Located at the Salon and Spa Galleria Oakmont Boulevard, this new skincare clinic is dedicated to offering result-driven treatments in a serene setting. Lizalde's personal journey and professional background in dermatology offices and medical spas have shaped her approach to skincare, focusing on both effectiveness and relaxation.

Sage Advanced Aesthetics stands out for its comprehensive services tailored to meet individual skin needs, including acne, hyperpigmentation, uneven texture, and signs of aging. The clinic offers a variety of treatments such as facials, dermaplaning, chemical peels, and advanced therapies like radio frequency therapy, which stimulates collagen production for improved skin texture and reduced aging signs. Each treatment is customized, ensuring clients receive personalized care that addresses their specific concerns.

To introduce her services to the community, Lizalde is offering a 10% discount on first-time facial services. This initiative not only makes advanced skincare more accessible but also encourages Fort Worth residents to experience the benefits of professional, personalized skincare in a welcoming environment. The clinic's strategic location, flexible hours, and amenities like free parking and handicap accessibility further enhance its appeal to a broad clientele.

The opening of Sage Advanced Aesthetics is a testament to the evolving skincare industry, where the demand for treatments that combine clinical efficacy with a spa-like experience is on the rise. Lizalde's clinic meets this demand by offering a unique blend of medical-grade treatments and relaxation, setting a new standard for skincare services in Fort Worth. As the beauty industry continues to grow, Sage Advanced Aesthetics represents the shift towards more personalized, results-oriented care, highlighting the importance of professional expertise in achieving healthy, radiant skin.
