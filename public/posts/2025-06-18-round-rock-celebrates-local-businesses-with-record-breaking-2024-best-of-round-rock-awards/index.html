Round Rock Celebrates Local Businesses with Record-Breaking 2024 Best of Round Rock Awards

Building Texas Show News • June 18, 2025
Read Original Article →

Summary

Round the Rock is excited to announce the winners of the 2024 Best of Round Rock Awards, celebrating the outstanding businesses, organizations, and individuals that make the community vibrant. With an unprecedented 30,000 votes from the community, the awards highlight the diverse businesses and services that Round Rock offers, showcasing the places locals love most.

The winners reflect the dedication and commitment to quality that defines Round Rock’s unique local culture, covering a wide range of industries including food and drink, health and wellness, retail, and community services. The complete list of award winners is now available via the Round the Rock website, roundtherocktx.com, and community members are encouraged to support these exceptional businesses and share their favorite places with friends and family.

Full Article

The 2024 Best of Round Rock Awards have set a new benchmark for community engagement and local business recognition, with residents casting a record-breaking 30,000 votes. This year's event, the 11th annual celebration organized by Round the Rock, showcased the city's diverse and thriving business landscape across over 175 categories. From food and drink to health and wellness, retail, and community services, the awards comprehensively cover the sectors that make Round Rock a dynamic place to live and work.

Stephanie Tyler, Managing Editor of Round the Rock, highlighted the awards' role in celebrating the hard work and dedication of local businesses and individuals. The 'Best Overall Business' category, among others, recognizes those who have not only excelled in their fields but have also contributed significantly to the local economy. The inclusion of expanded nonprofit categories this year further emphasizes the importance of community support and connection.

The overwhelming participation in the voting process reflects the community's pride and support for local businesses, fostering a stronger bond among residents and business owners. For consumers, the awards serve as a trusted guide to the city's top services and establishments, encouraging increased patronage and support for local enterprises.

Beyond the recognition, the awards have tangible benefits for the winners, including enhanced customer loyalty and marketing opportunities. This is particularly impactful for smaller, locally-owned businesses competing in a market dominated by larger chains. The awards also promote a 'shop local' mentality, crucial for sustaining and growing the local economy.

The Best of Round Rock Awards not only celebrate the current achievements of local businesses but also inspire future innovation and community engagement. As Round Rock continues to grow, such initiatives are vital for maintaining the city's unique character and supporting its business ecosystem. Round the Rock's commitment to promoting local businesses and strengthening community ties plays a significant role in fostering a vibrant and supportive environment for all.
