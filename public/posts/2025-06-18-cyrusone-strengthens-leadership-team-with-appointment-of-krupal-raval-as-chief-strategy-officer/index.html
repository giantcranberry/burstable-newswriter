CyrusOne Strengthens Leadership Team with Appointment of Krupal Raval as Chief Strategy Officer

Building Texas Show News • June 18, 2025
Read Original Article →

Summary

CyrusOne, a global leader in data center development and operation, has announced the appointment of Krupal Raval as its new Executive Vice President and Chief Strategy Officer. Raval, who brings a wealth of experience from his previous roles at Equinix and Digital Realty, will spearhead the company's development initiatives in the U.S. and foster strategic partnerships with hyperscale clients. His appointment is seen as a strategic move to bolster CyrusOne's growth and market position, especially with over a dozen new data centers currently under construction. CEO Eric Schwartz highlighted Raval's industry expertise and leadership as key to driving the company's expansion and innovation in the digital infrastructure space.

CyrusOne, headquartered in Dallas, Texas, operates more than 55 data centers across the U.S., Europe, and Japan, offering tailored solutions for hyperscale and enterprise clients. The company's focus on sustainability and meeting the demands of AI-driven applications positions it as a pivotal player in the digital economy. For more details, visit cyrusone.com.

Full Article

CyrusOne, a prominent name in the global data center sector, has taken a significant step forward by welcoming Krupal Raval as its new Executive Vice President and Chief Strategy Officer. This appointment is poised to play a critical role in CyrusOne's ongoing and future development projects, particularly in the United States, and in strengthening its collaborations with hyperscale customers. Raval's extensive experience in the data center industry, including his previous roles at Equinix and Digital Realty, positions him as a key figure in driving CyrusOne's strategic growth and expansion.

With over 55 data centers spread across the United States, Europe, and Japan, CyrusOne has established itself as a leader in providing sophisticated digital infrastructure solutions. The company's current construction of more than a dozen new data centers underscores its aggressive expansion strategy. Raval's expertise in managing hyperscale data center businesses and his ability to forge multi-billion dollar joint ventures across various regions will be invaluable in navigating this growth. His leadership is expected to enhance CyrusOne's market positioning and its ability to meet the increasing demands of the digital economy.

Eric Schwartz, CEO of CyrusOne, has expressed confidence in Raval's ability to contribute to the company's ambitious goals. Raval's proven track record in strategic development and his deep relationships within the industry are seen as assets that will propel CyrusOne forward. This strategic hire not only reflects CyrusOne's commitment to innovation and excellence but also signals its readiness to address the complex needs of hyperscale and enterprise clients in an ever-evolving digital landscape.
