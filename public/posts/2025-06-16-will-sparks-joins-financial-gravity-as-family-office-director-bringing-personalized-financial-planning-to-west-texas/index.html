Will Sparks Joins Financial Gravity as Family Office Director, Bringing Personalized Financial Planning to West Texas

Building Texas Show News • June 16, 2025
Read Original Article →

Summary

Financial Gravity Companies, Inc. (OTC: FGCO) welcomes Will Sparks as the new Family Office Director, focusing on personalized financial planning for families in West Texas. Will's client-centered approach aims to provide confidence in retirement and legacy planning.

With a background in general business and a desire to serve others, Will brings a unique perspective to Financial Gravity, prioritizing tailored strategies and relationships over one-size-fits-all solutions. His commitment to in-person relationships sets him apart in a digital age.

Full Article

Financial Gravity Companies has announced the appointment of Will Sparks as its newest Family Office Director, a move that underscores the company's commitment to delivering personalized financial planning services to families in West Texas. Sparks, a Midland native with a rich background in general business from Texas A&M University, along with minors in history and agricultural economics, brings a unique blend of expertise and a personal touch to financial services. His approach is rooted in understanding the individual needs of clients and crafting customized financial strategies that resonate with their personal goals and relationships.

In an era dominated by digital interactions, Sparks places a premium on personal connections, believing that the value of a handshake and face-to-face meetings has only increased. This philosophy sets him apart in an industry often criticized for its impersonal nature. As Family Office Director, Sparks will leverage Financial Gravity's extensive network of experts to offer advanced tax solutions and comprehensive financial services, making strategies once reserved for the wealthy accessible to a wider audience.

Scott Winters, CEO of Financial Gravity, praised Sparks for his alignment with the company's client-centered values, noting his dedication to serving others. Sparks' experience in the Young Life ministry during his college years has profoundly shaped his professional ethos, driving his commitment to guiding clients through pivotal financial decisions. His mission is to empower families to navigate retirement, legacy planning, and financial futures with confidence.

This appointment marks a strategic step for Financial Gravity as it expands its footprint in regional markets, offering sophisticated financial planning with a distinctly local and personal approach. Sparks' deep roots in West Texas and his genuine dedication to personalized service exemplify the company's vision for growth and community impact.
