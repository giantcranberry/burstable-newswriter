Item by Item Launches Comprehensive Quality Management Training for Microsoft Dynamics 365 Users

Building Texas Show News • June 18, 2025
Read Original Article →

Summary

Item by Item (IBI) has launched a new training curriculum focused on Microsoft Dynamics 365 Quality Management, offering 28 micro-courses, 4 learning paths, and multiple assessment tests. Elif Item, Founder and CEO of Item by Item, emphasized the significance of this initiative in enhancing the educational offerings available through the IBI platform, aimed at optimizing quality assurance processes across diverse business sectors. The new training content is immediately accessible to both existing and new customers via the IBI Learning Platform, recognized as the world's exclusive self-serve online training resource for Microsoft Dynamics 365.

Full Article

Item by Item (IBI) has recently launched an innovative online training curriculum dedicated to Quality Management within the Microsoft Dynamics 365 ecosystem. This educational initiative, made available on August 19, 2024, is tailored to equip businesses with the essential knowledge and tools to optimize their quality assurance and control mechanisms. The curriculum's structure includes 28 micro-courses, 4 learning paths, and various assessment tests, covering key areas such as quality orders, non-conformances, and corrective actions.

The significance of this release lies in its potential to transform how businesses approach quality management in the digital age. With the increasing reliance on Microsoft Dynamics 365 for operational efficiency, the ability to manage quality effectively within this platform is critical. IBI's new training content addresses this need head-on, offering professionals the expertise required to implement and sustain high-quality management systems. This initiative not only enhances operational standards but also supports businesses in reducing errors and improving product and service quality.

Elif Item, Founder and CEO of IBI, highlighted the curriculum's role in empowering businesses to refine their quality control practices. The immediate availability of this training on the IBI Learning Platform allows organizations to swiftly integrate these resources into their operational strategies, ensuring a seamless transition towards improved quality management processes.

Beyond its immediate benefits, the introduction of the Quality Management curriculum underscores IBI's commitment to being a comprehensive learning resource for Dynamics 365 users. With an extensive library that now includes over 1,200 micro-courses and 84 learning paths across various D365 modules, IBI is setting a new standard for digital training solutions. This latest addition not only reinforces IBI's leadership in the self-serve online training market but also provides businesses with a competitive edge in an increasingly digital and quality-conscious marketplace.

As industries navigate the challenges of digital transformation, the demand for skilled professionals capable of leveraging Microsoft Dynamics 365 for quality management is on the rise. IBI's new curriculum meets this demand, offering flexible, in-depth training that can be accessed at the learner's own pace. This development is a testament to the evolving needs of businesses and the critical role of quality management in achieving operational excellence and customer satisfaction.
