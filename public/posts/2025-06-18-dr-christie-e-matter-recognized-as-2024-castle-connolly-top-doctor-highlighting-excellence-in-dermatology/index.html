Dr. Christie E. Matter Recognized as 2024 Castle Connolly Top Doctor, Highlighting Excellence in Dermatology

Building Texas Show News • June 18, 2025
Read Original Article →

Summary

Dr. Christie E. Matter, MD, has been recognized as a 2024 Castle Connolly Top Doctor, a prestigious honor awarded to only about 7 percent of the nation's licensed physicians. She is a dermatologist with Lockhart Matter Dermatology & Aesthetic Center and co-founder of the practice. In September 2024, the practice will open its second location in Plano, offering Mohs, facial reconstruction, and cosmetic surgery procedures.

Full Article

Dr. Christie E. Matter, a co-founder of Lockhart Matter Dermatology & Aesthetic Center in Plano, Texas, has been honored as a 2024 Castle Connolly Top Doctor, a distinction that places her among the top 7 percent of physicians in the nation. This recognition not only celebrates Dr. Matter's expertise in dermatology but also shines a light on the growing importance of specialized skin care in today's healthcare landscape.

The expansion of Lockhart Matter Dermatology with a new Mohs, facial reconstruction, and cosmetic surgery center in Plano this September signifies a pivotal moment for dermatological services in Texas. The addition of Dr. Aahtman Shah to the team will further enhance the practice's ability to offer specialized treatments, including Mohs surgery and cosmetic procedures, addressing a critical need for advanced dermatological care in the region.

Dr. Matter's comprehensive approach to dermatology, which emphasizes patient education and the use of cutting-edge treatments, reflects a broader shift towards patient-centered care in the medical field. Her focus on both the physical and emotional impacts of skin conditions sets a benchmark for holistic dermatological care, offering a model for practices nationwide.

The recognition of Dr. Matter as a Castle Connolly Top Doctor, based on peer nominations and selection, highlights the trust and respect she has garnered within the medical community. This accolade not only acknowledges her individual achievements but also underscores the vital role dermatologists play in promoting skin health and overall well-being.

As skin health continues to gain recognition as an essential component of overall wellness, Dr. Matter's work and the expansion of her practice represent significant advancements in the field of dermatology. Her dedication to providing high-quality, patient-focused care is a testament to the impact that skilled healthcare providers can have on their communities and the broader healthcare industry.
