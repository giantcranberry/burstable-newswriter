New Books by Dr. Gary Royer Offer Guidance on Spiritual Warfare

Building Texas Show News • June 18, 2025

Full Article

Dr. Gary Royer, a respected figure in the spiritual community with over fifty years of missionary and leadership experience, has introduced two new books aimed at aiding believers in their spiritual battles. 'Demolishing Guilt, Fear, and Other Spiritual Strongholds' and 'Out of Darkness Into His Wonderful Light' serve as comprehensive guides for individuals seeking to navigate the challenges of spiritual warfare. These works are not just theoretical but are grounded in practical advice drawn from Dr. Royer's extensive ministry experience.

The first book, 'Demolishing Guilt, Fear, and Other Spiritual Strongholds', offers a biblical approach to overcoming common spiritual struggles such as guilt, fear, and addiction. It is designed for both personal study and group discussions, making it a versatile resource for a wide audience. The endorsement by Rev. Tim Barker, District Superintendent of South Texas Assemblies of God, underscores the book's relevance and potential impact on believers striving for a victorious life in Christ.

'Out of Darkness Into His Wonderful Light', the second book, delves deeper into the themes of spiritual bondage and liberation. It acts as a study guide that encourages readers to explore the sources of their spiritual oppression and equips them with tools for freedom through prayer and biblical truths. The praise from Dr. Kermit S. Bridges, President of Southwestern Assemblies of God University, highlights the book's significance as a resource for confronting spiritual issues.

The timing of these releases is particularly pertinent, as many individuals are searching for ways to address spiritual warfare in the context of contemporary challenges. Dr. Royer's blend of biblical wisdom and practical strategies makes these books invaluable for pastors, counselors, and believers alike. The inclusion of testimonies from those who have experienced transformation through these teachings adds a layer of authenticity and relatability.

Beyond the individual level, Dr. Royer's work has the potential to influence church communities and ministries, fostering a movement towards spiritual renewal and empowerment. These publications stand as significant contributions to spiritual warfare literature, offering hope and practical solutions for those seeking liberation from spiritual bondage.
