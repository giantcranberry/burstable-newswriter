Dick Sayles Honored as Senior Statesperson by Chambers USA, Highlighting Legal Excellence in Texas

Building Texas Show News • June 15, 2025
Read Original Article →

Summary

This recognition highlights his exceptional career, marked by high-profile commercial disputes, intellectual property cases, and more, earning him accolades from peers and clients alike. Full Article

The legal community in Texas has once again been reminded of the towering figure that Richard A. 'Dick' Sayles represents, as Chambers USA bestows upon him the Senior Statesperson designation. This honor not only reflects Sayles' decades of dedication to the legal profession but also his ongoing impact and mentorship within the realms of trial and commercial litigation in Texas. The Senior Statesperson category is reserved for lawyers who have shown exceptional leadership and insight, often after many years of active practice, making Sayles' recognition a testament to his distinguished career.

Sayles, renowned as 'an outstanding trial lawyer,' has built a formidable practice that handles high-stakes commercial disputes and intellectual property cases, among others. His latest accolade from Chambers USA adds to a long list of honors, including recognitions from Thomson Reuters Super Lawyers and The Best Lawyers in America, further cementing his status as a legal luminary. The Chambers USA guide, a respected benchmark in the legal industry, employs a rigorous evaluation process that includes peer and client feedback, making Sayles' consistent recognition over the years a clear indicator of his technical legal ability, professional conduct, and the esteem in which he is held by his peers and clients alike.

Beyond the Chambers USA recognition, Sayles' career is adorned with numerous achievements, including board certifications in civil trial law and personal injury trial law, and accolades such as the Dallas Bar Association’s Trial Lawyer of the Year. With over 150 jury trials in state and federal courts under his belt, Sayles' extensive experience showcases his versatility and depth as a litigator. This latest honor not only celebrates his individual accomplishments but also underscores the vital role that seasoned attorneys play in shaping the legal landscape. Dick Sayles' career serves as a blueprint for legal excellence, blending rigorous advocacy with a steadfast commitment to mentorship and the broader legal community.
