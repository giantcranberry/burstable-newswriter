Wytec International's AI Gunshot Detection Technology Set to Enhance Public Safety Across Multiple States

Building Texas Show News • June 18, 2025
Read Original Article →

Summary

Wytec International, a telecommunications company, has been awarded a multi-state government contract to provide AI gunshot detection smart sensors and Private LTE technology to over 170 US Cities, 50 Counties, and 40 Special Districts. The contract was secured through the TXShare Cooperative Purchasing Program and the North Central Texas Council of Governments.

Wytec has partnered with Trabus Technologies and Lemko Corporation to develop their smart sensor solutions. Trabus Technologies specializes in wireless technologies and AI/ML, while Lemko Corporation deploys commercial networks worldwide using 4G and 5G technology. Wytec is offering a no-cost Proof of Concept demonstration of its AI smart sensor technology to select TXShare members.

Full Article

Wytec International's recent acquisition of a multi-state government Master Services Agreement (MSA) through the TXShare Cooperative Purchasing Program marks a pivotal moment in public safety technology. This agreement, facilitated by the North Central Texas Council of Governments, will allow over 170 US cities, 50 counties, and more than 40 special districts to access Wytec's state-of-the-art AI gunshot detection technology. The technology's deployment spans a wide array of public sector entities, including transit authorities, water utility boards, and police departments, underscoring its versatility and critical role in enhancing community safety.

The technology developed by Wytec is distinguished by its exceptional accuracy, with AI and machine learning algorithms that have undergone rigorous testing in over 750,000 lab trials. Achieving a 94% true positive and 95% true negative accuracy rate, the system stands as a global leader in gunshot detection. This level of precision is instrumental in reducing false alarms and ensuring that resources are allocated efficiently during emergencies, thereby saving lives and minimizing unnecessary panic.

Collaboration with Trabus Technologies and Lemko Corporation has been key to developing this advanced solution. Trabus Technologies, a minority-owned and Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business, provided the embedded AI/ML software solution for threat detection, while Lemko Corporation contributed a secure wireless backbone using Private LTE systems and mobile edge computing technologies. These partnerships highlight the collaborative effort behind the technology's development and its potential to revolutionize public safety measures.

Wytec's initiative to offer a no-cost Proof of Concept demonstration to select TXShare members further demonstrates the company's confidence in its technology's effectiveness. With plans to transition to the Nasdaq Capital Markets and ongoing discussions with chipset manufacturers for potential technology integration, Wytec is poised for significant growth. This contract not only represents a milestone for Wytec but also signifies the increasing reliance on AI technologies to safeguard communities, illustrating a forward-thinking approach to public safety and security infrastructure.
