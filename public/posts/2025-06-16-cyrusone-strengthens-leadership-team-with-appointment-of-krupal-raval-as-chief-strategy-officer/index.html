CyrusOne Strengthens Leadership Team with Appointment of Krupal Raval as Chief Strategy Officer

Building Texas Show News • June 16, 2025
Read Original Article →

Summary

CyrusOne, a global leader in data center development and operation, has announced the appointment of Krupal Raval as its new Executive Vice President and Chief Strategy Officer. This strategic move is aimed at bolstering the company's development program in the U.S. and enhancing its engagement with hyperscale customers. Raval, with his extensive background in leading hyperscale data center businesses at Equinix and Digital Realty, brings a wealth of experience and strategic vision to CyrusOne. His appointment is expected to accelerate the company's growth and strengthen its market position amidst an aggressive expansion pipeline.

Eric Schwartz, CEO of CyrusOne, highlighted Raval's exceptional industry experience and leadership qualities as key factors that will drive the company's strategic goals forward. CyrusOne, known for its innovative and flexible digital infrastructure solutions, operates over 55 data centers worldwide, catering to the evolving needs of hyperscale and enterprise companies. The company's focus on sustainability and AI-driven applications positions it as a pivotal player in the digital infrastructure space. For more details, visit cyrusone.com.

Full Article

CyrusOne, a leading global data center developer and operator, has taken a significant step forward in its strategic growth by appointing Krupal Raval as its new Executive Vice President and Chief Strategy Officer. This decision highlights the company's commitment to expanding its footprint in the data center industry and enhancing its services for hyperscale customers worldwide. Raval's extensive experience, particularly from his tenure at Equinix where he led the hyperscale data center business, positions him as a key player in CyrusOne's future endeavors.

The appointment is timely, as CyrusOne is in the midst of an aggressive expansion phase, including the development of more than a dozen new data centers. Raval's expertise in strategic vision and execution is expected to be instrumental in navigating the complexities of the global digital infrastructure market. His ability to forge strong relationships with hyperscalers and drive strategic partnerships will be crucial in achieving the company's ambitious goals.

Eric Schwartz, CEO of CyrusOne, has expressed optimism about Raval's contribution to the company's growth trajectory. Schwartz's confidence in Raval's capabilities underscores the strategic importance of this appointment. With over 55 data centers in its portfolio, CyrusOne is poised to address the increasing demand for AI-driven applications and services, offering solutions that not only meet but exceed customer expectations in terms of business and sustainability objectives.

Raval's arrival at CyrusOne signals a new chapter in the company's journey towards innovation and excellence in the data center sector. His leadership is expected to accelerate CyrusOne's growth, strengthen its market position, and enhance its ability to deliver sophisticated digital infrastructure solutions. This move is a clear indication of CyrusOne's dedication to remaining at the forefront of the data center industry, catering to the evolving needs of its customers and shaping the future of digital infrastructure.
