Texicare and TempoPay Partner to Revolutionize Healthcare Payment Options for Texas Small Businesses

Building Texas Show News • June 18, 2025
Read Original Article →

Summary

TempoPay and Texicare have partnered to provide employees of small business groups with access to the TempoPay flexible payment solution for managing everyday healthcare expenses. Texicare members can now access $1,500 in interest-free financing through TempoPay, allowing them to manage out-of-pocket expenses in a way that works for them. This partnership aims to make health care more accessible and affordable for all members, aligning with Texicare's mission to build a healthier, happier Texas.

Full Article

The recent partnership between Texicare, the health affiliate of Texas Mutual, and TempoPay marks a significant advancement in the Texas healthcare sector. This collaboration is set to provide employees of small business groups in Texas with a novel way to manage their out-of-pocket healthcare expenses through flexible payment options. The initiative leverages TempoPay's Visa® card, which allows for the payment of a wide range of health-related expenses over time without the burden of interest, fees, or credit checks, thereby making healthcare more accessible and manageable for many Texans.

Under this partnership, Texicare members will benefit from up to $1,500 in interest-free financing, integrated directly into their health plans. This feature is designed to align with employees' household budgets, offering them the flexibility to repay their healthcare expenses in a manner that suits their financial situation. Meredith Duncan, CEO of Texicare, highlighted the partnership's goal to remove financial barriers to healthcare, enabling members to prioritize their well-being without the immediate stress of out-of-pocket costs.

This initiative is a testament to Texicare's commitment to innovating the healthcare ecosystem for small businesses in Texas. By incorporating TempoPay's flexible payment solutions into its health plans, Texicare is enhancing its offerings to focus on the holistic well-being of employees and their families. Erika Davison-Aviles, Co-founder of TempoPay, expressed pride in the partnership, emphasizing the alignment with their mission to facilitate access to healthcare for hardworking individuals and their families.

The implications of this partnership extend beyond immediate financial relief for employees. Small businesses across Texas now have the opportunity to provide a more comprehensive health benefits package, potentially leading to improved health outcomes as employees are more likely to seek necessary medical care without the deterrent of upfront costs. Furthermore, this collaboration addresses a critical issue in the American healthcare system—affordability—by introducing a model that could inspire similar initiatives nationwide.

As the healthcare industry continues to seek solutions to make medical care more accessible, the partnership between Texicare and TempoPay stands out as a pioneering effort. By merging traditional health insurance with innovative financial tools, these organizations are setting a precedent for a more inclusive and user-friendly healthcare system in Texas, with potential ripple effects across the country.
