Royal Thai Massage and Wellness Center Opens in Benbrook, Offering Specialized Therapies

Building Texas Show News • June 18, 2025
Read Original Article →

Summary

Orawan Singhan announces the opening of Royal Thai Massage and Wellness Center in Benbrook, Texas, offering a variety of massage therapies, including Swedish, deep-tissue, traditional Thai, and Thai combination. Orawan specializes in traditional Thai massage, specifically calibrated for athletes and those looking to enhance performance. The center also offers a 10% discount to veterans and first responders, as well as a choice of free hot stone, free aroma oil, or free tiger balm for new customers.

Full Article

The Royal Thai Massage and Wellness Center has recently opened its doors at the Salon and Spa Galleria Benbrook in Benbrook, Texas, marking a significant addition to the local wellness industry. Founded by Orawan Singhan, a licensed massage therapist, the center specializes in a variety of massage therapies, including Swedish, deep-tissue, traditional Thai, and Thai combination treatments. These services are designed not only for relaxation but also for addressing specific health concerns such as pain relief and mobility improvement.

Singhan's inspiration to enter the field of massage therapy stemmed from a personal experience of aiding her grandfather in Thailand with pain management. This led her to pursue formal training at the ELM Health Institute in Fort Worth, where she developed her expertise in traditional Thai massage. This particular therapy is noted for its benefits to athletes and individuals looking to enhance their physical performance, utilizing back-walking techniques and yoga-inspired stretches to improve movement fluidity.

The center's commitment to community service is evident through its 10-percent discount offered to veterans and first responders. Additionally, to celebrate its grand opening, new customers are presented with the option of a 10-percent discount or complimentary add-on treatments, such as hot stone therapy or aroma oil treatments. This initiative not only fosters community engagement but also introduces residents to the benefits of massage therapy.

Located within the Salon and Spa Galleria Benbrook, the Royal Thai Massage and Wellness Center is easily accessible to residents of Benbrook and surrounding areas, including Granbury, Fort Worth, Aledo, Whiskey Flats, and Cresson. The facility boasts ample free parking and is handicap-friendly, ensuring a welcoming environment for all clients.

The establishment of the Royal Thai Massage and Wellness Center is a testament to the growing demand for alternative and complementary therapies in Texas. By offering a blend of traditional and modern massage techniques, the center addresses a wide range of health and wellness needs, contributing to the broader wellness industry's expansion. This development not only enhances the local economy but also provides residents with access to specialized therapeutic services, underscoring the importance of wellness in community health.
