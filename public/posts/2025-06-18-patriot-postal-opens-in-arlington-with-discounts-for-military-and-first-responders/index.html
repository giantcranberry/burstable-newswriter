Patriot Postal Opens in Arlington with Discounts for Military and First Responders

Building Texas Show News • June 18, 2025
Read Original Article →

Summary

The owners of a new pack & ship store, Patriot Postal, are offering 10% off to military and first responders. The store will offer shipping through major carriers, notary services, document shredding, and private mailboxes. The site will also have the scent of fresh popcorn and is the first of ten stores planned to be opened during the next three years.

Full Article

Patriot Postal has recently opened its doors in Arlington, Texas, introducing a new player in the pack & ship industry with a strong patriotic stance. Located at 5100 W Sublett Rd, the store is not just another shipping outlet but a business with a mission to honor and support those who serve the nation. Offering a 10% discount every day to military personnel and first responders, Patriot Postal sets itself apart by acknowledging the sacrifices of these groups in a tangible way.

The store provides a comprehensive suite of services, including shipping through major carriers like UPS, FedEx, USPS, and DHL, notary and mobile notary services, document shredding, electronic fingerprinting, and a selection of packing materials and office supplies. Among its standout offerings are private mailboxes at competitive rates, a service particularly appealing to home-based businesses and individuals concerned about package theft.

Adding to its customer-friendly approach, Patriot Postal offers free freshly popped popcorn to all visitors, creating a welcoming atmosphere that owner Brad Fry compares to entering a movie theater. This unique touch, combined with the store's commitment to community service and customer relationships, underscores its ambition to redefine the pack & ship experience.

With plans to expand to ten locations over the next three years, Patriot Postal is poised for significant growth. The Arlington store, secured through RDS Real Estate, marks the beginning of what the company hopes will be a widespread presence. Jim Eaton, Vice-President of RDS Real Estate, has expressed satisfaction with the new tenant, highlighting the potential for Patriot Postal to make a lasting impact in the industry.

The opening of Patriot Postal in Arlington offers local residents and businesses a new option for shipping and related services, with a focus on community support and customer care. Its patriotic discounts, comprehensive services, and plans for expansion signal a promising future for the store in the Arlington area and beyond.
