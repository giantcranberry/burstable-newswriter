Canter Power Systems Expands Texas Footprint with Acquisition of Grasten Power Technologies

Building Texas Show News • June 16, 2025
Read Original Article →

Summary

Canter Power Systems, a Generac Power Pro Premier dealer, has acquired Grasten Power Technologies, a premier installer and service provider of home standby generators in Houston and Texas. The partnership aims to meet the growing demand for reliable backup power solutions in the Texas market, addressing grid instability and severe weather events. This marks Canter’s third add-on acquisition since partnering with Rotunda Capital Partners in September 2022.

Full Article

The acquisition of Grasten Power Technologies by Canter Power Systems represents a strategic move to bolster the latter's presence in Texas, a state that has recently faced significant power reliability issues. This merger is particularly timely, given the increasing demand for dependable backup power solutions amidst growing grid instability and the heightened frequency of severe weather events causing power outages.

Grasten Power Technologies, renowned in Houston for its comprehensive generator services, brings to the table a sterling reputation for customer service, quality, and responsiveness. This acquisition allows Canter Power Systems to leverage Grasten's established market presence and skilled workforce, enhancing its service offerings and reliability for Texas residents and businesses.

Brian Lopatka, CEO of Canter Power Systems, and Josh Keller, President of Grasten, both highlighted the synergistic potential of this partnership. The merger not only expands Canter's geographic reach but also combines Grasten's local expertise with Canter's national scale and resources, promising improved service delivery and market expansion.

Supported by Rotunda Capital Partners, this acquisition is a testament to Canter Power Systems' aggressive growth strategy in the residential backup power market. The company's accolades, including being Home Depot's largest national residential generator installer and receiving the Home Depot Service Provider of the Year award in 2022, underscore its leadership and quality in the industry.

As climate change continues to exacerbate weather-related challenges and strain power grids, the demand for reliable backup power solutions is expected to rise. Canter Power Systems' expansion into Texas through this acquisition not only addresses immediate needs but also positions the company as a key player in enhancing energy resilience across the state and potentially beyond.

This development reflects a broader industry trend towards improving grid resilience and offering consumers more reliable power options. With Canter Power Systems at the helm, the residential backup power landscape is poised for significant evolution, promising greater reliability and service quality for homeowners and businesses alike.
