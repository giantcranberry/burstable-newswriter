Huntington Fine Jewelers Hosts Exclusive Summer Estate Jewelry Trunk Show Across Oklahoma

Read Original Article →

Summary

Huntington Fine Jewelers, Oklahoma's premier jeweler, is set to dazzle the public with its Summer Estate Jewelry Trunk Show across various locations in the state throughout June. This exclusive event showcases a meticulously curated collection of one-of-a-kind estate jewelry, offering timeless pieces that combine vintage allure with exceptional craftsmanship. Attendees can enjoy significant savings, with all trunk show items available at buy one, get one FREE deals, making it a must-visit for both seasoned collectors and those in search of unique jewelry pieces. The event kicks off in Shawnee on June 3–4, moving through Oklahoma City, Yukon, Norman, Stillwater, and concluding in Midwest City on June 18–20. With limited availability, early RSVP is encouraged to secure access to these extraordinary treasures. For more details, interested parties are advised to contact their nearest Huntington Fine Jewelers location or visit their website for professional jewelry services and an extensive selection of fine jewelry.

Full Article

Huntington Fine Jewelers, a name synonymous with luxury and quality in Oklahoma, is gearing up to present its Summer Estate Jewelry Trunk Show across various locations in the state this June. This event is not just a showcase but a celebration of vintage jewelry, offering pieces that carry the weight of history and the finesse of unmatched craftsmanship. From June 3–4 in Shawnee to a grand finale from June 18–20 in Midwest City, the trunk show is a golden opportunity for jewelry enthusiasts to acquire heirloom-quality items at up to 50% off, with the added allure of a buy one, get one free offer.

The trunk show is meticulously curated to feature one-of-a-kind estate jewelry, including rings, necklaces, bracelets, and more, each telling its own story. This event caters not only to seasoned collectors but also to those on the lookout for unique pieces that stand apart from the mainstream. The limited availability of these treasures makes early RSVP a must for those wishing to secure a piece of history.

Huntington Fine Jewelers' commitment to excellence is evident in this event, reflecting over 35 years of dedication to providing the finest jewelry, certified loose diamonds, Swiss-made timepieces, and top-tier design and repair services. The Summer Estate Trunk Show is a testament to their unwavering standards, offering customers not just jewelry, but a legacy. For business and technology leaders with an eye for timeless elegance and investment-worthy pieces, this event underscores the enduring value of craftsmanship and the importance of preserving history through exquisite jewelry.
