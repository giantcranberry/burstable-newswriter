Vector Choice Technologies Elevates Mike Bazar to Chief Revenue Officer to Drive Growth

Building Texas Show News • June 18, 2025
Read Original Article →

Summary

Mike Bazar has been promoted to Chief Revenue Officer at Vector Choice Technologies, a leading IT provider in Lubbock, TX. As the former Chief Technology Officer, Bazar's new role will focus on revenue growth and implementing comprehensive revenue generation strategies. With over 20 years of experience, Mike Bazar is set to drive new strategies and opportunities for Vector Choice.

Full Article

Vector Choice Technologies, a leader in cybersecurity, compliance, and managed IT services, has announced a pivotal leadership transition with Mike Bazar stepping into the role of Chief Revenue Officer (CRO) from his previous position as Chief Technology Officer (CTO). This move, effective June 19, 2024, highlights the company's strategy to merge technological prowess with revenue generation in a rapidly evolving industry.

Bazar's tenure as CTO was marked by significant contributions to Vector Choice's technological advancements and strategic direction. His leadership not only propelled innovation but also cultivated an environment of continuous learning within the organization. Transitioning to CRO, Bazar is set to oversee revenue strategies, market expansion, and cross-departmental synergies, leveraging his extensive experience to fuel the company's growth.

With over twenty years in the IT sector, Bazar's background includes founding Bazar Solutions in Lubbock, Texas, and pioneering in mining technology automation. His merger with Vector Choice in 2023 was a strategic alliance that combined strengths, setting a foundation for the company's current trajectory. Bazar's appointment as CRO is timely, as Vector Choice aims to navigate the complexities of the IT services and cybersecurity landscape, where demand for innovative solutions and robust security measures is at an all-time high.

The implications of this leadership change extend beyond Vector Choice, offering insights into how IT service providers might align technological innovation with business growth strategies. Bazar's dual expertise in technology and business positions him uniquely to drive Vector Choice's expansion, potentially influencing industry standards for leadership roles in the sector.

As Vector Choice Technologies embarks on this new chapter under Bazar's revenue leadership, the move underscores the importance of integrating technical and business strategies to stay competitive. The industry's response to this strategic realignment will be closely monitored, as it may herald a new approach to leadership in the IT services domain.
