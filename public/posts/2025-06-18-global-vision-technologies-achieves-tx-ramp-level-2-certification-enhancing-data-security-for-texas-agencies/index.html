Global Vision Technologies Achieves TX-RAMP Level 2 Certification, Enhancing Data Security for Texas Agencies

Building Texas Show News • June 18, 2025
Read Original Article →

Summary

Global Vision Technologies (GVT) has achieved TX-RAMP Level 2 Certification, demonstrating their commitment to data security. The FAMCare platform, GVT's proprietary case management solution, has built-in security controls to protect sensitive data. GVT's software solutions are vital for health and human services agencies managing critical populations such as juvenile justice, child welfare, and patient healthcare information.

Full Article

Global Vision Technologies (GVT) has reached a pivotal milestone by obtaining TX-RAMP Level 2 Certification for its FAMCare human services software platform. This achievement underscores the platform's compliance with Texas's stringent data security standards, offering enhanced protection for sensitive information processed by state agencies, higher education institutions, and public colleges.

The Texas Risk and Authorization Management Program (TX-RAMP), established under Texas Senate Bill 475, requires the Texas Department of Information Resources to enforce a uniform security assessment and monitoring framework for cloud computing services. GVT's FAMCare platform, now TX-RAMP Level 2 certified, meets these rigorous standards, ensuring the security and privacy of data across critical sectors such as juvenile justice, child welfare, elderly care, and healthcare.

George Ritacco, VP of Client Services at GVT, highlighted the company's unwavering commitment to data security, noting the certification as a testament to their dedication. FAMCare's architecture, built on GVT's Visions Server platform, facilitates rapid development of forms and workflows while minimizing security risks, making it an ideal solution for agencies prioritizing data protection.

This certification not only simplifies the procurement process for Texas agencies by ensuring compliance with state security mandates but also sets a precedent for other states contemplating similar security frameworks. As cyber threats grow more sophisticated, TX-RAMP certification emerges as a critical benchmark for cloud-based services, with GVT leading the charge in secure case management solutions.

Beyond Texas, GVT's achievement signals to health and human services agencies nationwide the importance of adhering to high data security standards. This development could influence the selection of case management systems, especially for those agencies where data security and compliance are paramount. GVT's proactive approach in securing TX-RAMP Level 2 Certification positions FAMCare as a frontrunner in the industry, potentially establishing new security benchmarks for software solutions in the public and healthcare sectors.
