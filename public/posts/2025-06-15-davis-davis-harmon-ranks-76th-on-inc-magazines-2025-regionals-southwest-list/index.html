Full Article

Davis Davis & Harmon, a Dallas-based sales tax consulting firm, has achieved a significant milestone by ranking 76th on Inc. magazine's 2025 Regionals: Southwest list. This recognition underscores the firm's impressive growth trajectory and its substantial impact on the regional economy. The Inc. Regionals: Southwest list celebrates the fastest-growing private companies in Arizona, New Mexico, Oklahoma, and Texas, with Davis Davis & Harmon standing out among 137 featured businesses that collectively added 13,809 jobs and contributed $15.9 billion to the regional economy.

The firm's inclusion on this prestigious list is a reflection of its strategic expansion and innovative approach to overcoming business challenges. Over the past five years, Davis Davis & Harmon has broadened its service offerings by venturing into international markets, enhancing its expertise in VAT tax, exploring new industry verticals, and integrating IT services. This diversification has not only fueled the company's growth but also solidified its reputation as a multinational leader in sales tax consulting.

Chanel Christoff Davis, CEO of Davis Davis & Harmon, credits the company's success to its ability to turn challenges into opportunities. The firm specializes in sales tax refund recovery and audit defense, with its team recovering over $500 million annually for clients. Such achievements highlight the firm's critical role in supporting businesses to navigate complex tax landscapes, ultimately contributing to their financial health and sustainability.

To qualify for the Inc. Regionals list, companies must meet stringent criteria, including being U.S.-based, privately held, for-profit, and independent, with demonstrated revenue growth over two years. Davis Davis & Harmon's recognition on this list not only celebrates its past achievements but also signals its potential for future growth and innovation in the sales tax consulting industry. As editorial director Bonny Ghosh of Inc. magazine notes, companies like Davis Davis & Harmon are at the forefront of driving economic growth in their regions and industries, making their stories of success all the more important to share.
