Jest Paint's GTX Water Resistant Cream Kit Revolutionizes Face Painting Industry in Texas

Building Texas Show News • June 15, 2025
Read Original Article →

Summary

Jest Paint launches the GTX Water Resistant Cream Kit, a silicone-based face paint perfect for professional face painters and enthusiasts. The innovative formula is long-lasting, water-resistant, and easy to apply, ideal for summer parties, festivals, and events.

Full Article

The face painting industry in Texas is witnessing a transformative shift with the launch of Jest Paint's GTX Water Resistant Cream Kit. This silicone-based solution is setting new standards for temporary skin decoration, combining durability, versatility, and safety in a way that was previously unavailable. The kit's 15 airless pump bottles, each containing 15ml of specialized cream paint, are designed to meet the demands of both professional artists and hobbyists, especially in challenging environments like outdoor festivals and summer parties.

What sets the GTX Water Resistant Cream Kit apart is its innovative formulation. Unlike traditional alcohol-based paints, it eliminates strong odors and reduces the risk of skin irritation, making it a safer choice for users of all ages. The product's slow-drying properties and exceptional color retention allow for intricate designs and full-face artwork, providing artists with unparalleled creative freedom. The inclusion of diverse shades such as True Black, Rodeo Red, and Blue Bonnet ensures a comprehensive palette for every artistic need.

Developed by Amber Massa, a professional with a background in permanent makeup ink development, the GTX line is the result of global collaboration with artists. Its registration under the MOCRA in the United States and use of FDA-compliant pigments underscore the product's commitment to quality and safety. The ease of application and removal, along with simple brush maintenance, further enhances its appeal to the face painting community.

The implications of this innovation are vast for the Texas economy and the global face painting industry. By offering a product that is not only superior in performance but also in safety and environmental resistance, Jest Paint is poised to attract a wide range of users, from professional artists at large-scale events to parents hosting birthday parties. This could lead to increased demand for face painting services, fostering growth within the industry and encouraging more individuals to explore face painting as a profession or hobby. The GTX Water Resistant Cream Kit is more than just a new product; it's a game-changer for the art of temporary skin decoration.
