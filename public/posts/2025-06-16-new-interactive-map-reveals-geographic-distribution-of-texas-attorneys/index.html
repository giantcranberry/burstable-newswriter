New Interactive Map Reveals Geographic Distribution of Texas Attorneys

Building Texas Show News • June 16, 2025
Read Original Article →

Summary

Darrow Law Firm, P.C. unveiled its 2024 Texas Attorneys Map, showcasing the distribution of lawyers across Texas' counties. The interactive tool, based on data from the American Bar Association and State Bar of Texas, aids in locating legal representation and assessing market opportunities.

Full Article

The legal landscape of Texas has been meticulously mapped out in a new interactive digital resource, the 2024 Texas Attorneys Map. Developed by Darrow Law Firm, this innovative tool draws on data from the American Bar Association and the State Bar of Texas to provide a real-time overview of where licensed attorneys are practicing across the state's 254 counties. This resource is not just a technological achievement but a significant step forward in understanding the distribution of legal services in Texas.

With over 75,000 attorneys plotted, the map reveals stark contrasts in legal representation between densely populated urban centers like Harris County and more sparsely populated rural areas. These disparities raise important questions about access to justice and the equitable distribution of legal resources. For individuals in need of legal representation, the map serves as a guide to finding attorneys in their area. For legal professionals and researchers, it offers a wealth of data for analyzing market trends and studying the demographics of the legal profession.

The implications of this tool extend beyond immediate practical applications. By highlighting areas with potentially underserved populations, the 2024 Texas Attorneys Map contributes to ongoing discussions about how to ensure all Texans have access to legal services. It also provides a model for how data visualization can be used to address complex issues of resource allocation and access to justice. This resource is a testament to the power of technology to illuminate disparities and foster a more informed dialogue about solutions.
