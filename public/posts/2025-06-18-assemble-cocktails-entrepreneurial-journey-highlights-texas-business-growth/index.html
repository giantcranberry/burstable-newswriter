Assemble Cocktail's Entrepreneurial Journey Highlights Texas Business Growth

Building Texas Show News • June 18, 2025
Read Original Article →

Summary

The Building Texas Show, hosted by Justin McKenzie, features an interview with Lillian Oler, founder of Assemble Cocktail. Oler shares her entrepreneurial journey from launching a unique cocktail experience in a cozy cottage to expanding her brand with a franchising model. Listeners will hear about Oler’s strategic pivot to a mobile format and her current plans for a new brick-and-mortar space in San Antonio, as well as her innovative approach to franchising.

Full Article

Lillian Oler's Assemble Cocktail began as a unique mixology workshop in Bernie, Texas, with a vision to bring people together through the art of cocktail making. The business's evolution from a cozy cottage to a mobile service and now to a brick-and-mortar location in San Antonio showcases the dynamic nature of Texas's entrepreneurial spirit. Oler's story, as featured on The Building Texas Show, highlights the challenges and triumphs of scaling a business in today's economy.

The decision to pivot to a mobile format allowed Assemble Cocktail to reach a wider audience, demonstrating the importance of flexibility in business growth. Oler's strategic move to establish a physical presence in San Antonio marks a significant milestone, reflecting confidence in the brand's appeal and scalability. Her statement, 'If it can be done in Bernie, Texas, it can be done anywhere,' speaks volumes about the potential for small businesses to expand beyond their initial markets.

Perhaps the most groundbreaking aspect of Assemble Cocktail's growth strategy is the introduction of a franchise model. This approach not only expands the brand's footprint but also offers aspiring entrepreneurs a turnkey opportunity to replicate Oler's success in their communities. The franchise model is designed to be accessible and enjoyable, emphasizing the brand's core values of community and connection.

The interview with Oler on The Building Texas Show provides invaluable insights into the entrepreneurial journey, from launching during a pandemic to navigating the complexities of brand building. Her background in hospitality and hands-on approach have been instrumental in creating an immersive experience that resonates with customers. This story is a testament to the power of passion, adaptability, and a focus on community in achieving business success.

As Texas continues to foster a vibrant environment for entrepreneurs, stories like Oler's serve as a blueprint for sustainable business growth. The Building Texas Show plays a pivotal role in showcasing the innovative businesses that are shaping the state's economy, offering lessons and inspiration for current and future business owners. Assemble Cocktail's journey from a small workshop to a franchising model underscores the evolving nature of entrepreneurship and the endless possibilities within Texas's business landscape.
