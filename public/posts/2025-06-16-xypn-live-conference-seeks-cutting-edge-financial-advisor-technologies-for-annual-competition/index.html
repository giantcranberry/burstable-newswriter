XYPN LIVE Conference Seeks Cutting-Edge Financial Advisor Technologies for Annual Competition

Building Texas Show News • June 16, 2025
Read Original Article →

Summary

The 11th annual XYPN LIVE 2025 event, aimed at fee-only financial advisors, will take place from Sept. 25-27 in Austin. The event will feature an AdviceTech competition where innovative tech platforms can showcase tools that support financial advisors in delivering more efficient and impactful practices. Finalists will present their technology to an audience of approximately 1,000 attendees, with awards including 'Best in Show' and 'Advicer's Choice'.

Full Article

The XYPN LIVE conference, a pivotal event for financial advisors and technology innovators, is once again setting the stage for the AdviceTech Competition. Scheduled for September 25-27 in Austin, this competition is a beacon for emerging technology platforms aiming to revolutionize the financial advisory sector. With a focus on solutions that streamline operations and enhance client advice delivery, the competition is a critical opportunity for startups and new offerings to showcase their potential to a targeted audience of approximately 1,000 industry professionals.

Eligibility for the competition is tailored to ensure that only the most innovative and promising technologies are considered. Companies must either be a new offering from an existing entity, have less than $1 million in revenue, or have launched within the last 12 months. This criteria ensures a level playing field for true innovators to stand out. Finalists are granted a unique platform to demonstrate their solutions, including a seven-minute presentation slot, two complimentary exhibit hall passes, and promotional support through XYPN's extensive channels.

The competition not only offers visibility but also tangible rewards that can propel a startup's growth. The 'Best in Show' and 'Advicer's Choice' awards, judged by industry experts and attendees respectively, come with benefits designed to foster long-term success. Past winners like Snappy Kraken, Vestwell, and Holistiplan have gone on to make significant impacts in the financial advisory technology space, underscoring the competition's role in identifying and nurturing future industry leaders.

With a submission deadline of June 26 at 5:00 PM Mountain Time, the clock is ticking for innovators to seize this opportunity. The AdviceTech Competition at XYPN LIVE is more than just a contest; it's a gateway to recognition, growth, and integration into the financial advisory technology ecosystem. For emerging platforms, participation could mark the beginning of a transformative journey in the industry.
