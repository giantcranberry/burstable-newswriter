Car Garage Expert Launches 24/7 Roadside Assistance in Dubai, Setting New Standards for Automotive Care

Building Texas Show News • June 18, 2025
Read Original Article →

Summary

Car Garage Expert is proud to announce its dependable roadside assistance services in Dubai. With a focus on customer satisfaction, the company provides rapid and reliable solutions to car troubles, ensuring motorists are never left stranded. The CEO, Muhammad Fahad Malik, emphasizes the goal of offering peace of mind to drivers across Dubai, with 24/7 availability and a state-of-the-art facility for comprehensive automotive care.

Full Article

Car Garage Expert has taken a significant step forward in addressing the automotive needs of Dubai by launching a comprehensive 24/7 roadside assistance service. This initiative is designed to offer immediate support to drivers encountering unexpected vehicle issues, ranging from flat tires to engine breakdowns, ensuring minimal disruption to their journeys. With a team of skilled technicians ready to respond at any hour, the service promises to keep Dubai's roads safer and more efficient.

The introduction of this service by Car Garage Expert is a response to the growing demand for reliable automotive support in Dubai, a city where efficient transportation is vital. By providing round-the-clock assistance, the company not only aids individual motorists but also contributes to the broader goal of reducing traffic congestion and enhancing urban mobility. This service is particularly beneficial in a city that thrives on its reputation for speed and efficiency, offering both residents and visitors peace of mind when on the road.

Muhammad Fahad Malik, CEO of Car Garage Expert, highlighted the company's dedication to customer satisfaction, emphasizing the importance of fast, reliable, and affordable assistance. This commitment is reflected in the company's holistic approach to automotive care, which combines immediate roadside support with comprehensive maintenance services. Such an approach not only addresses the immediate needs of drivers but also promotes long-term vehicle health, potentially saving customers time and money in the long run.

The impact of Car Garage Expert's new roadside assistance service extends beyond the immediate benefits to drivers. By minimizing the time vehicles spend immobilized on the road, the service could play a crucial role in improving traffic flow and reducing congestion. This aligns with Dubai's urban planning objectives, which aim to enhance mobility and reduce the stress associated with transportation in the city. Furthermore, the availability of reliable 24/7 automotive support adds to Dubai's appeal as a tourist destination, offering visitors an added layer of security and convenience.

As Car Garage Expert rolls out this innovative service, it sets a new benchmark for automotive care in Dubai. The company's comprehensive approach, combining rapid roadside assistance with expert garage services, positions it as a leading solution for vehicle owners in the city. This initiative not only meets the current needs of Dubai's motorists but also raises the bar for customer expectations and industry standards in the automotive service sector.
