BlackSky Technology Advances Satellite Capabilities and Secures Major Contracts

Building Texas Show News • June 15, 2025
Read Original Article →

Summary

Stonegate Capital Partners updates their coverage on BlackSky Technology, Inc. (NYSE: BKSY), reporting revenue, adj EBITDA, and EPS for 4Q24. BKSY secured significant contract awards, launched its first Gen-3 satellite, and ended the quarter with a strong balance sheet. The company also set its FY25 revenue guidance and provided a valuation analysis.

Full Article

BlackSky Technology, Inc. (NYSE: BKSY) has recently announced its fourth-quarter financial results, showcasing a remarkable period of growth and technological advancement. The company's achievements include the successful launch of its first Gen-3 satellite and the securing of multiple nine-figure, multi-year contracts, underscoring its growing influence in the satellite technology sector.

Financially, BlackSky demonstrated substantial improvement, with consolidated gross margins increasing to 77.4% from 66.2% in the previous quarter. The company reported a quarterly revenue of $30.4 million, with significant contributions from its Imagery and Software Analytics segment at $17.5 million and the Professional and Engineering segment at $12.9 million. This financial uptick is a testament to the company's operational efficiency and market demand for its services.

The launch of the first Gen-3 satellite marks a significant milestone for BlackSky, with the satellite's performance exceeding customer expectations. The company plans to further expand its satellite fleet with seven additional launches in the next 12 months. These advanced satellites are designed to offer high-frequency monitoring and 35cm very high-resolution imaging capabilities, enhancing BlackSky's competitive edge in the market.

Contract awards have played a pivotal role in BlackSky's recent success, with the company's backlog expanding from $261 million to approximately $390 million. Some of these contracts extend up to seven years, highlighting BlackSky's ability to meet long-term customer needs and secure premium agreements. This expansion not only solidifies the company's financial foundation but also its reputation as a reliable partner in the satellite imagery and analytics industry.

With a strong liquidity position of $53.8 million at the end of the quarter and an anticipated additional $27.9 million over the next 12 months through milestone achievements, BlackSky is well-positioned for sustained growth. The company's fiscal year 2025 revenue guidance is set between $125.0 million and $142.0 million, with an adjusted EBITDA range of $14.0 million to $22.0 million, reflecting confidence in its strategic direction and market potential.

BlackSky's recent accomplishments and future plans underscore its commitment to innovation and excellence in the satellite technology sector. By leveraging its advanced satellite capabilities and securing long-term contracts, the company is not only enhancing its market position but also contributing to the broader economic impact in Texas and beyond. These developments are indicative of BlackSky's potential to drive significant advancements in satellite imagery and analytics, offering valuable insights and solutions to industries worldwide.
