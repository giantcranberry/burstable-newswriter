Primi Digital Launches Vendi360: A Game-Changer in Smart Vending Technology

Newswriter Business and Technology News • June 19, 2025
Read Original Article →

Summary

Primi Digital introduces Vendi360, a cutting-edge platform combining Smart Vending Software and full-service support for a seamless vending experience. The system offers end-to-end payment support, custom branding, AI-driven inventory intelligence, actionable analytics, and reliable 24/7 support. Vendi360 transforms automated retail by delivering modern vending encounters that adapt to user behaviors and location needs.

Full Article

The introduction of Vendi360 by Primi Digital marks a significant leap forward in the realm of automated retail, offering businesses a comprehensive smart vending platform that integrates advanced technology with user-friendly services. This innovative solution is designed to streamline vending operations, providing a level of efficiency and customization previously unseen in the industry.

At the heart of Vendi360's appeal is its end-to-end ecosystem, which supports a variety of payment methods including credit cards, mobile wallets, and NFC technologies. This flexibility ensures seamless transactions across a wide range of environments, from airports and college campuses to office buildings and fitness centers, catering to the modern consumer's demand for convenience and speed.

One of the platform's standout features is its AI-driven inventory intelligence, which offers real-time stock tracking, automatic replenishment alerts, and strategic product mapping. These capabilities not only help businesses reduce waste but also minimize operational downtime, ensuring that vending machines are always stocked and ready to meet customer needs. Furthermore, the actionable analytics dashboard provides operators with valuable insights into sales trends, customer behavior, and machine performance, enabling data-driven decision-making.

Custom branding is another area where Vendi360 shines, allowing businesses to fully customize the visual appearance of their vending machines and interfaces. This feature transforms vending machines from simple transactional devices into powerful brand touchpoints, enhancing customer engagement and loyalty.

Yousuf Hasan, CEO of Primi Digital, highlights the transformative potential of Vendi360, noting its ability to enhance brand presence while simplifying the vending ecosystem. As the demand for unmanned commerce grows, Vendi360 emerges as a scalable, future-ready solution that can adapt to the evolving needs of businesses, whether they're managing a single machine or a citywide network.

The launch of Vendi360 represents a pivotal moment in the automated retail sector, offering businesses the tools they need to thrive in an increasingly digital and convenience-driven marketplace. With its combination of advanced technology, operational efficiency, and brand customization, Vendi360 is set to redefine the standards of smart vending.
