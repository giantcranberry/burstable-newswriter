1979 Clénet Series I Roadster Joins DFW Car and Toy Museum Collection

Building Texas Show News • June 15, 2025
Read Original Article →

Summary

The DFW Car & Toy Museum in Fort Worth, TX is showcasing the rare 1979 Clénet Series I Roadster from the Ron Sturgeon Collection, a limited edition handcrafted beauty with only 250 units ever produced. This vintage luxury car boasts a Ford V8 engine, distinctive features, and a blend of old-world sophistication with modern performance.

Owner Ron Sturgeon describes the Clénet as a masterpiece of automotive art, offering visitors a chance to experience bespoke craftsmanship and witness a piece of automotive history. The museum also features a rich collection of classic cars, toys, and memorabilia, making it a must-visit destination for car enthusiasts and admirers of timeless elegance.

Full Article

The DFW Car and Toy Museum in North Fort Worth has recently welcomed a new addition to its prestigious collection, a 1979 Clénet Series I Roadster. This vehicle, numbered 236 out of a limited production of 250, stands as a testament to the ingenuity and craftsmanship of Clénet Coachworks from Santa Barbara, California. The roadster's design is a nod to the elegance of 1930s automobiles, featuring a brown soft top, louvered hood, and distinctive 15-inch wire wheels, among other classic details.

Under the hood, the Clénet Series I Roadster is powered by a Ford V8 engine paired with a three-speed automatic transmission, offering a perfect balance of power and sophistication. The vehicle's construction combines steel bodywork with fiberglass fenders on a Ford chassis, equipped with front disc brakes for enhanced performance. Inside, the roadster boasts a meticulously crafted interior, complete with a Nardi steering wheel and comprehensive VDO instrumentation, making it a masterpiece of automotive art.

With only 1,300 miles on its odometer, this Clénet Roadster is in pristine condition, offering visitors a rare opportunity to witness a unique piece of automotive design history. The DFW Car and Toy Museum, known for its climate-controlled environment, provides the perfect setting for this and other remarkable vehicles, allowing enthusiasts to explore the evolution of automotive innovation and nostalgia. The museum's commitment to preserving such treasures underscores the importance of automotive heritage in Texas and beyond, offering free admission and parking to all visitors.

This acquisition not only enriches the museum's collection but also serves as a reminder of the enduring appeal of classic automobiles. The Clénet Series I Roadster's blend of luxury, performance, and exclusivity highlights a significant moment in automotive design, making it a must-see for anyone interested in the history and artistry of cars. The DFW Car and Toy Museum continues to be a beacon for automotive enthusiasts and history buffs, showcasing vehicles that have left an indelible mark on the industry.
