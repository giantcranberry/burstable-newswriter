Dr. Felix Liao Inducted into ASBA Hall of Fame for Pioneering Dental Sleep Medicine

Building Texas Show News • June 15, 2025
Read Original Article →

Summary

Dr. Felix Liao, DDS, has been inducted into the American Sleep and Breathing Academy Hall of Fame for his pioneering work in airway dentistry and holistic oral health solutions. His groundbreaking approach to dental sleep medicine and Impaired Mouth Syndrome™ has reshaped how dental professionals address sleep-related challenges and overall wellness.

Full Article

Dr. Felix Liao, a leader in dental sleep medicine, has been honored with induction into the American Sleep and Breathing Academy (ASBA) Hall of Fame. This recognition celebrates his decades of work in bridging the gap between oral health and systemic wellness, introducing groundbreaking concepts like Impaired Mouth Syndrome™ and Holistic Mouth Solutions™. These approaches advocate for non-invasive, comprehensive treatment methods, challenging conventional dental practices.

With over 40 years of experience, Dr. Liao has not only transformed clinical practices but also contributed to the field through his authorship of five influential books. Titles such as 'Six-Foot Tiger, Three-Foot Cage' and 'Licensed to Thrive' delve into the critical connections between dental health and overall bodily function, offering new perspectives on patient care.

Beyond his written contributions, Dr. Liao is shaping the future of dental medicine through his Airway Mouth Doctor™ training programs. These initiatives aim to equip the next generation of dental professionals with advanced, patient-centered techniques, further disseminating his holistic approach to oral health.

The ASBA Hall of Fame accolade not only honors Dr. Liao's innovative methodologies, such as advanced 3D Jaw Diagnostics™, but also highlights the broader implications of his work. By demonstrating how dental interventions can profoundly affect patients' overall well-being and quality of life, Dr. Liao's contributions are paving the way for a new standard in dental care, emphasizing comfort, healing, and non-invasive procedures.

This recognition serves as a testament to the evolving landscape of dental medicine, where the integration of oral health and systemic wellness is becoming increasingly paramount. Dr. Liao's work exemplifies the potential for dental professionals to play a pivotal role in enhancing patients' health beyond the mouth, offering a model for future practices in the industry.
