Full Article

McCann Systems, a prominent name in the audiovisual integration industry, has recently announced a significant expansion of its team with the addition of 11 professionals. This strategic move is aimed at enhancing the company's service offerings and operational capabilities, particularly in the Texas market. The expansion includes the establishment of a new office in Texas, with AJ Flores and Pat Seymour joining to cater to the growing client base in the Dallas-Fort Worth metro area. This development underscores McCann Systems' commitment to delivering exceptional AV experiences and its preparation for a strong presence at InfoComm 2025 in Orlando.

Leading the new hires is Will Amos, who takes on the role of Senior Account Executive. Amos's extensive experience in building client relationships and delivering high-value AV solutions is expected to significantly contribute to the growth of McCannX, the company's experiential AV division. The diverse skill set of the new team members, including Melissa Barnes as Project Manager and Elliot Nyfield as Senior Systems Design Engineer, among others, promises to elevate McCann's technical and operational excellence.

Josh Bittner, SVP of Sales and Marketing at McCann Systems, highlighted the company's focus on organic growth through this expansion. The addition of these professionals not only reflects McCann's strategic vision of fostering a strong culture but also ensures continuity and enhanced service for its clients. Known for creating innovative and immersive AV solutions across various sectors, McCann Systems' latest move is a clear indication of its ambition to remain a leader in the audiovisual integration industry.

This expansion is particularly significant for the Texas economy and the AV industry at large, as it brings new jobs and advanced AV solutions to the region. For businesses and organizations in Texas, this means access to cutting-edge AV technologies and services that can transform their operational and communication strategies. The implications of this announcement extend beyond the immediate benefits to McCann Systems, signaling a positive trend in the AV industry's growth and innovation.
