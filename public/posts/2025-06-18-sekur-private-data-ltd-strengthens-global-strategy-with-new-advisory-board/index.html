Sekur Private Data Ltd. Strengthens Global Strategy with New Advisory Board

Newswriter Business and Technology News • June 18, 2025
Read Original Article →

Summary

Sekur Private Data (CSE: SKUR) (OTCQB: SWISF) (FSE: GDT0) has formed a Strategic Advisory Board with high-profile members Jill Kelley, James M. Connelly, and Nikolas Perrault to support global expansion and partnerships. The board aims to elevate Sekur's brand and pursue contracts with U.S. and international institutions for secure communications.

CEO Alain Ghiai highlighted the board's contributions to active discussions across regions, reinforcing Sekur's mission to provide privacy-first cybersecurity solutions. The company offers Swiss-hosted secure communication tools for consumers, businesses, and governments worldwide.

Full Article

Sekur Private Data Ltd. has taken a significant step forward in its global expansion and government engagement efforts by establishing a Strategic Advisory Board. This board is composed of three highly respected professionals: Jill Kelley, James M. Connelly, and Nikolas Perrault, each bringing a wealth of experience in diplomatic relations, corporate strategy, and international business development to the table.

Jill Kelley, known for her work as a diplomatic advisor and privacy advocate, is set to play a pivotal role in elevating Sekur's brand among government and enterprise stakeholders. Her strategic insights are expected to significantly boost the company's visibility and credibility in key markets. James M. Connelly, with his extensive background in diplomatic and corporate relations in Washington, D.C., will aid Sekur in securing contracts with U.S. and international institutions in need of secure communication solutions. Nikolas Perrault, with his expertise in finance and mergers and acquisitions, will focus on fostering global partnerships and expansion, particularly in European and African markets.

CEO Alain Ghiai emphasized the immediate benefits of the advisory board, noting ongoing discussions in various regions. This initiative underscores Sekur's dedication to offering privacy-first cybersecurity solutions at a time when digital threats and data privacy concerns are at an all-time high. The formation of this board not only highlights Sekur's proactive approach to addressing these challenges but also its commitment to delivering comprehensive cybersecurity solutions to a global clientele, including consumers, businesses, and governments.

The strategic assembly of this advisory board reflects Sekur Private Data Ltd.'s recognition of the critical need for secure, reliable communication tools in today's digital age. By leveraging the diverse expertise of its new members, Sekur is well-positioned to navigate the complexities of the global market and reinforce its mission to protect digital privacy and security worldwide.
