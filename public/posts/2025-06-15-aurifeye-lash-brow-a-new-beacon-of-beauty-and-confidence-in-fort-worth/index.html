Aurifeye Lash & Brow: A New Beacon of Beauty and Confidence in Fort Worth

Building Texas Show News • June 15, 2025
Read Original Article →

Summary

Brooke Luitjens opens Aurifeye Lash & Brow, offering various eyelash and eyebrow services. Located in Suite 402 of Salon & Spa Galleria Hulen, Fort Worth, TX. Services include classic eyelash extensions, volume extensions, hybrid sets, tinting, lifting, brow tint, threading, and brow lamination.

Full Article

Aurifeye Lash & Brow has recently opened its doors in Fort Worth, marking a significant addition to the city's beauty and wellness landscape. Located in Suite 402 of the Salon & Spa Galleria Hulen, this new establishment is the brainchild of Brooke Luitjens, a licensed esthetician and certified lash technician. Luitjens' venture into the beauty industry is inspired by her personal battle with Vitiligo, an auto-immune condition that led her to appreciate the transformative power of beauty services in boosting self-esteem and confidence.

The salon specializes in a variety of lash and brow services, including classic and volume extensions, hybrid sets, lash tinting, lifting, brow tinting, threading, and lamination. These services are designed not just for aesthetic enhancement but also to cater to the individual needs and comfort of each client, ensuring a personalized beauty experience. The salon's name, Aurifeye, which means to turn into gold, reflects Luitjens' philosophy of transforming and enhancing natural beauty.

Strategically located off Loop 820, Aurifeye Lash & Brow is designed with client comfort in mind, featuring ergonomically designed furniture and a luxurious ambiance. The salon operates from Monday to Thursday, 10am-6pm, Friday 10am-3pm, and alternating Saturdays 10am-1pm, offering new clients a 15% discount on their first visit. Luitjens describes her approach as a blend of technical expertise and emotional support, highlighting the salon's commitment to not just beauty enhancement but also to fostering a positive self-image among its clients.

The opening of Aurifeye Lash & Brow is more than just the launch of another beauty salon; it represents a beacon of hope and confidence for individuals seeking to embrace their unique beauty. In a world where appearance often impacts self-perception, Luitjens' salon stands out as a place where beauty services go beyond the surface, offering a transformative experience that celebrates individuality. This establishment is set to make a significant impact on Fort Worth's beauty industry, offering services that prioritize personal comfort, natural enhancement, and the empowerment of clients through beauty.
