Ferrari's SF90 Stradale Showcases the Future of Supercars at DFW Car and Toy Museum

Building Texas Show News • June 15, 2025
Read Original Article →

Summary

The DFW Car and Toy Museum unveils the 2022 Ferrari SF90 Stradale, a groundbreaking Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicle with 986 horsepower and advanced technology. This supercar, part of the Ron Sturgeon Collection, blends tradition with innovation to redefine performance on four wheels.

Full Article

The DFW Car and Toy Museum recently added a groundbreaking exhibit to its collection, the 2022 Ferrari SF90 Stradale, a vehicle that not only celebrates Ferrari's 90th anniversary but also marks the company's first foray into plug-in hybrid electric vehicle (PHEV) technology. This addition is not just a showcase of luxury and speed but a testament to the evolving landscape of automotive engineering, where performance meets sustainability.

The SF90 Stradale is powered by a combination of a twin-turbocharged 4.0-liter V8 engine and three electric motors, culminating in an astounding 986 horsepower. This hybrid powertrain represents a significant advancement in automotive technology, offering a glimpse into the future of high-performance vehicles that do not solely rely on fossil fuels. The intelligent all-wheel-drive system ensures that this power is delivered efficiently, providing an unparalleled driving experience.

Beyond its impressive powertrain, the SF90 Stradale features cutting-edge technology designed to enhance performance and safety. The electronic Side Slip Control and electric traction control systems work in harmony to distribute torque dynamically, ensuring optimal handling and grip under various conditions. Inside, the car boasts a meticulously designed interior with a carbon-fiber and leather-trimmed steering wheel, digital instrument cluster, and head-up display, all of which contribute to a driver-focused experience.

The thermal management system of the SF90 Stradale is another highlight, capable of handling extreme temperatures while protecting the vehicle's sensitive electronic components. The strategic placement of the 7.9-kWh lithium-ion battery pack not only improves the car's balance but also its efficiency, further underscoring Ferrari's commitment to innovation.

For enthusiasts and industry professionals alike, the SF90 Stradale's presence at the DFW Car and Toy Museum offers a unique opportunity to witness the future of supercars. This vehicle exemplifies how traditional performance benchmarks can be achieved through hybrid technology, setting a new standard for the automotive industry. As the world moves towards more sustainable solutions, the SF90 Stradale serves as a beacon of what's possible when innovation and environmental consciousness drive design.
