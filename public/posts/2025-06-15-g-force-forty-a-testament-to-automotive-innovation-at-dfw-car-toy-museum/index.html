G Force Forty: A Testament to Automotive Innovation at DFW Car & Toy Museum

Building Texas Show News • June 15, 2025
Read Original Article →

Summary

The DFW Car & Toy Museum welcomes the 1940 Ford DeLuxe Coupe Custom 'G Force Forty' to its collection, showcasing a blend of vintage design and modern performance. This high-performance street rod boasts 711 horsepower and superior handling thanks to its C5 Corvette suspension. The museum offers free admission, inviting visitors to experience the thrill of hot-rodding up close.

Full Article

The DFW Car & Toy Museum in North Fort Worth has become the new home for an automotive masterpiece that epitomizes the zenith of street rodding innovation. The 1940 Ford Deluxe Coupe Custom, affectionately dubbed the 'G Force Forty', stands as a beacon of how classic aesthetics can harmoniously blend with state-of-the-art performance engineering. This vehicle is not just a display of beauty but a powerhouse of performance, equipped with a C5 Corvette suspension system that ensures unparalleled road control and handling, setting it apart from conventional street rods that often sacrifice performance for appearance.

At the heart of the G Force Forty lies an Iron Eagle block paired with Brodix-12 18-degree raised-port cylinder heads, a combination that unleashes a staggering 711 horsepower at 6,200 rpm and 607 lb-ft of torque at 5,000 rpm. Remarkably, these figures are achieved using standard pump gas, underscoring the vehicle's engineering marvel. This level of performance not only rivals but in some aspects surpasses that of modern supercars, making the G Force Forty a significant milestone in automotive customization.

Part of The Ron Sturgeon Collection, the G Force Forty is now a centerpiece in the museum's vast 150,000-square-foot facility, where it is displayed alongside over 200 other cars and 3,000 toys. The museum, which offers free admission, provides a unique platform for automotive enthusiasts and the general public to appreciate the craftsmanship and innovation that define the street rodding culture. The inclusion of the G Force Forty in the museum's collection not only enriches its offerings but also serves as an educational tool, illustrating the potential of combining traditional design with contemporary technology.

The significance of the G Force Forty extends beyond its technical specifications; it represents the relentless pursuit of automotive excellence that characterizes the street rodding community. By pushing the boundaries of what is possible in automotive customization, the G Force Forty inspires both current and future generations of car enthusiasts to explore the intersection of art and engineering. The DFW Car & Toy Museum, open Tuesday through Saturday, invites visitors to witness this extraordinary vehicle and the legacy it represents, offering a glimpse into the passion and creativity that drive the automotive industry forward.
