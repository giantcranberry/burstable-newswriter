Texas Whiskey Festival 2025 Winners Showcase State's Distilling Excellence

Building Texas Show News • June 18, 2025
Read Original Article →

Summary

The Texas Whiskey Festival announces the winners of its 2025 competition, showcasing the best whiskeys in the Lone Star State. Co-Founder Jake Clements highlights the blind-judging process and the emphasis on flavor over hype. Award categories include bourbon, rye, malt, cask-finished, light, and flavored whiskeys, with fan favorites also recognized. Exciting upcoming whiskey experiences are on the horizon for enthusiasts to enjoy.

Full Article

The Texas Whiskey Festival's 2025 competition has once again put the spotlight on the state's distilling prowess, with winners across multiple categories demonstrating the depth and diversity of Texas whiskey. Still Austin Cask Strength Bourbon and Devils River 5-Year Straight Rye Whiskey were among the standout winners, securing gold in the Best Bourbon and Rye Whiskey categories, respectively. Ranger Creek Texas Landmark Single Malt's victory in the Malt Whiskey segment further illustrates the expanding portfolio of Texas whiskeys.

Jake Clements, Co-Founder of the Texas Whiskey Festival, highlighted the event's role in showcasing the innovation and passion driving the Texas distilling community. The inclusion of unique categories such as Cask-Finished Whiskey and Best Flavored Whiskey allowed distilleries like Giant Texas Distilling and Mossy Horn to demonstrate their creativity, with their Tejas Collaboration Cigar Blend and Pecan whiskey winning gold in their respective categories.

The People's Choice Awards, determined by festival attendees, celebrated favorites such as Mossy Horn Whiskey and Andalusia Whiskey Co., emphasizing the strong bond between distillers and whiskey lovers. With upcoming events like the Frisco StrEATs Whiskey Tasting and Whiskey Unexpected, the festival continues to foster appreciation for Texas whiskey, reinforcing the state's growing stature in the global spirits industry.

This recognition not only celebrates the achievements of individual distilleries but also signals the broader economic and cultural impact of the whiskey industry in Texas. As the state's distilleries gain national and international acclaim, they contribute to job creation, tourism, and the global reputation of Texas as a hub for quality spirits. The Texas Whiskey Festival's 2025 winners are a testament to the innovation, craftsmanship, and community that define the state's whiskey industry.
