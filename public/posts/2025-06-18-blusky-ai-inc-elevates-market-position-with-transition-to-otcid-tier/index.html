Full Article

BluSky AI Inc., a Salt Lake City-based modular data center company, has announced its transition from the OTC Pink Sheets to the OTCID tier, effective July 1, 2025. This move is a clear indicator of the company's progress in enhancing its reporting, compliance, and transparency standards, aligning with its strategic growth objectives and bolstering investor confidence.

The transition follows a series of notable achievements by BluSky AI, including the expansion of its modular AI datacenter, forging new infrastructure partnerships, introducing a GPU-as-a-Service offering, and making significant strides in financial governance. CEO Trent D’Ambrosio highlighted that this upgrade is a reflection of the company's commitment to creating long-term value, enhancing market visibility, and securing access to capital.

At the heart of BluSky AI's mission is the provision of innovative and sustainable infrastructure solutions that support the burgeoning AI revolution. The company's turnkey modular solutions are designed to mitigate the global AI supply shortage, offering AI companies the computational power essential for driving innovation and growth.

The upgrade to the OTCID tier not only underscores BluSky AI's fortified market stance but also casts a spotlight on the escalating significance of AI infrastructure within the technology sector. As AI continues to be a cornerstone of innovation across various industries, the role of companies like BluSky AI in ensuring the availability of critical computational resources becomes increasingly vital. This development is particularly relevant for investors and industry watchers, signaling a promising direction for the company and the broader AI infrastructure market.
