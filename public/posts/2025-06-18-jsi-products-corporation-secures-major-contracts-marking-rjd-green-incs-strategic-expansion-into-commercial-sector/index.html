JSI Products Corporation Secures Major Contracts, Marking RJD Green Inc.'s Strategic Expansion into Commercial Sector

Building Texas Show News • June 18, 2025
Read Original Article →

Summary

RJD Green Inc. CEO Ron Brewer announced JSI Products Corporation, a Silex Holdings Inc. Tulsa Division, has executed agreements for two multifamily projects creating over $500,000 in revenue. The company will focus on providing quality products and services including stone products, cabinetry, doors, tile, and other related products in a four-state regional market. Silex Holdings Inc. is a specialty construction products company offering manufacturing, sales & distribution, and installation of multiple products with a primary focus on cabinetry, stone countertops, walls, and flooring for various clients.

Full Article

The recent announcement by RJD Green Inc. regarding its subsidiary, Silex Holdings Inc., and its newly established division, JSI Products Corporation, marks a significant milestone in the company's expansion strategy. With the securing of two multifamily projects, JSI Products Corporation has not only generated over $500,000 in revenue but also positioned itself for an additional $400,000 in a subsequent phase. This achievement underscores the company's successful foray into the commercial sector, a move that is expected to refine its operational capabilities and broaden its market reach within a four-state region.

JSI Products Corporation's focus on providing a wide array of quality products and services, including stone products, cabinetry, and tile, targets General Contractors initially, with plans to extend into the homebuilder market in the Greater Tulsa area. Beyond these contracts, the team is tasked with researching and developing new products, aiming to expand distribution through Silex Holdings locations and potentially establish new distributors in other regions. This initiative reflects a strategic effort to enhance the company's market presence and diversify its product offerings.

The significance of this development extends beyond the immediate financial gains for RJD Green Inc. and its subsidiaries. It represents a strategic expansion into new markets, demonstrating the company's ability to adapt and grow in the competitive specialty construction products industry. For investors and industry observers, this move signals RJD Green's commitment to diversification and growth, particularly within its Silex Holdings Division. The success of these initial contracts could herald further expansion and revenue opportunities, reinforcing the company's position in the market and its potential for future growth.

As RJD Green Inc. continues to navigate the complexities of the specialty construction products industry, the achievements of JSI Products Corporation serve as a testament to the company's strategic vision and operational excellence. This development not only benefits the company and its stakeholders but also contributes to the broader economic landscape in Texas and beyond, highlighting the impact of innovative business strategies on regional and industry-wide growth.
