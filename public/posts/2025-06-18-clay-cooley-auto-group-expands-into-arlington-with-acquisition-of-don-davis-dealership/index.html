Clay Cooley Auto Group Expands into Arlington with Acquisition of Don Davis Dealership

Building Texas Show News • June 18, 2025
Read Original Article →

Summary

Clay Cooley Auto Group announces the acquisition of Don Davis Chrysler Jeep Dodge Ram in Arlington, Texas, marking a significant milestone in the company's ongoing expansion and commitment to delivering superior automotive experiences across North Texas. The acquisition aligns with the group's mission to serve the unique needs of the Arlington community and introduces a fresh approach, enhanced service options, and a seamless experience rooted in the group's values of integrity and customer care. With a quarter-century legacy of excellence, Clay Cooley Auto Group has become a trusted automotive leader in Dallas-Fort Worth, providing a wide array of new and pre-owned vehicles across various brands.

Full Article

The Clay Cooley Auto Group, a stalwart in the Dallas-Fort Worth automotive sector, has taken a significant step forward by acquiring Don Davis Chrysler Jeep Dodge Ram in Arlington, Texas. This move not only expands the group's footprint but also reinforces its commitment to delivering exceptional service and value to the North Texas community. The newly rebranded Clay Cooley Chrysler Jeep Dodge Ram dealership is set to offer a fresh approach to automotive sales and service, grounded in the group's longstanding values of integrity and customer care.

Chase Cooley, representing the company, highlighted the acquisition as a pivotal moment in the group's 25-year history. This expansion is not merely about growth but about deepening the group's ties with the Arlington community. Customers, both existing and new, can look forward to an enhanced dealership experience, with access to a broader range of services and vehicles. The transition is designed to be seamless, ensuring that the high standards of customer service and quality that the Clay Cooley Auto Group is known for are upheld at the new location.

This acquisition is particularly significant as it aligns with the group's 25th anniversary, marking a quarter-century of leadership and innovation in the automotive industry. The move into Arlington is a testament to the group's strategic vision and its dedication to community engagement. Beyond the immediate benefits to consumers, the acquisition is expected to bring economic advantages to the area, including potential job creation and increased access to a diverse inventory of vehicles.

For the Arlington community and the broader Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex, the arrival of the Clay Cooley brand represents an opportunity to experience a new level of automotive retail. The group's reputation for transparency, quality, and customer loyalty is now extended to Arlington, offering residents a trusted option for their automotive needs. As the industry evolves, this expansion positions the Clay Cooley Auto Group to adapt to changing consumer preferences and market trends, ensuring its continued relevance and success in the years to come.
