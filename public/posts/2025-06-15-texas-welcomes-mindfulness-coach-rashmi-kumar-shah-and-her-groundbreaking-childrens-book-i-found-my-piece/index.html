Texas Welcomes Mindfulness Coach Rashmi Kumar Shah and Her Groundbreaking Children's Book 'I Found My Piece'

Building Texas Show News • June 15, 2025
Read Original Article →

Summary

Rashmi Kumar Shah, a local author, recently captivated children and their families at the Hendersonville Pride family zone with her interactive Mindful Storytime event, showcasing her new book, I Found My Piece. This beautifully illustrated children's book delves into mindfulness, emotions, and the quest for inner calm, offering young readers a unique tool to navigate their feelings. Following the reading, children engaged in a creative activity, decorating oversized cardboard puzzle pieces to represent what brings them peace, mirroring the book's central theme.

Published by Archway Publishing, a collaboration between Simon & Schuster and Author Solutions, I Found My Piece has quickly risen to #35 on Amazon's Top 100 Paid Books list in the New Age Meditation category, standing out as the only children's book in the top 100. Shah's journey from corporate immigration law to mindfulness coaching and authorship highlights her commitment to fostering emotional growth and connection among children. Her Mindful Storytime Tour continues to inspire families across the country, with upcoming stops in North Carolina and Texas.

Full Article

The arrival of Rashmi Kumar Shah's 'I Found My Piece' in Texas marks a significant moment for families and educators seeking innovative tools for children's emotional and mindfulness education. The book, which has already secured the #35 spot on Amazon's Top 100 Paid Books in the 'New Age Meditation' category, stands out as the only children's book in the top 100. This achievement not only showcases Shah's unique contribution to the field but also reflects a growing recognition of the importance of mindfulness practices from an early age.

Shah's 'Mindful Storytime Tour' has been instrumental in bringing the concepts of her book to life, engaging children and their families in interactive activities that foster emotional connection and understanding. The tour's upcoming stop in Austin, TX, is eagerly anticipated, promising to extend the book's impact to the Texas community. Through activities like decorating oversized cardboard puzzle pieces, Shah's events create a tangible connection to the book's themes, making mindfulness accessible and enjoyable for children.

The significance of 'I Found My Piece' extends beyond its Amazon ranking. Published by Archway Publishing, a collaboration between Simon & Schuster and Author Solutions, the book serves as a vital resource for emotional education. Shah's transition from corporate immigration law to mindfulness coaching and authorship exemplifies her dedication to personal growth and helping others find peace. As Texas prepares to welcome Shah and her book, the potential for 'I Found My Piece' to influence childhood development and mindfulness education in the state is immense, offering a beacon of hope for families navigating the complexities of emotional growth.
