Donaldson Company Elevates Richard Lewis to Chief Operating Officer Role

Read Original Article →

Summary

Donaldson Company, a global leader in filtration products and solutions, has announced the appointment of Richard Lewis as its new chief operating officer, effective August 1, 2025. Lewis, who currently serves as president of the company's Life Sciences segment, will oversee Donaldson's three main segments, enterprise operations, supply chain, and corporate technology functions, reporting directly to Tod Carpenter, the company's chairman, president, and CEO. With over 23 years at Donaldson, Lewis brings a wealth of experience and a deep understanding of the company's global portfolio, end markets, and customer needs. His appointment is seen as a strategic move to strengthen the execution of Donaldson's strategy and drive value creation for stakeholders. Lewis expressed his honor in stepping into the COO role and his eagerness to work with the leadership team to deliver innovative solutions worldwide. Donaldson, founded in 1915, operates in over 140 locations across six continents, offering filtration solutions through its Mobile Solutions, Industrial Solutions, and Life Sciences segments. More information can be found at www.Donaldson.com.

Full Article

Donaldson Company, a stalwart in the global filtration industry, has announced the promotion of Richard Lewis to the position of Chief Operating Officer, effective August 1, 2025. With a tenure of 23 years at Donaldson, Lewis's ascent to COO is a testament to the company's belief in nurturing internal talent to steer its future growth and operational excellence.

Lewis's new role encompasses oversight of Donaldson's three primary segments, alongside enterprise operations, supply chain, and corporate technology functions, reporting directly to Tod Carpenter, the company's chairman, president, and CEO. Carpenter's endorsement of Lewis underscores a confidence rooted in Lewis's extensive experience and his demonstrated ability to navigate the company through varying market dynamics successfully.

Currently at the helm of the Life Sciences segment, Lewis will continue in this capacity until a successor is named. His career at Donaldson has been marked by leadership roles that have significantly contributed to the company's operational and strategic milestones, including his tenure as President of Mobile Solutions and Senior Vice President of Global Operations.

Lewis's academic credentials, including a bachelor's degree in industrial engineering from the University of Louisville and an MBA from Indiana University, complement his practical experience, equipping him with a holistic understanding of the business and technological challenges in the filtration sector.

In his statement, Lewis expressed his eagerness to collaborate with the leadership team to innovate and deliver solutions that meet the evolving needs of customers worldwide, further solidifying Donaldson's market position. This leadership transition is pivotal for Donaldson as it seeks to reinforce its commitment to operational excellence and innovation in a competitive global landscape, ensuring sustained value creation for its stakeholders.
