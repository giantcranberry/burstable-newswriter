Bruce A. Watterson Joins Peapack Private Bank & Trust as Senior Managing Director

Newswriter Business and Technology News • June 3, 2025
Read Original Article →

Summary

Peapack-Gladstone Financial Corporation and Peapack Private Bank & Trust have announced the appointment of Bruce A. Watterson as a Senior Managing Director, Managing Group Director within their Commercial Banking division. Watterson, a seasoned banker with over 40 years of experience, joins from Flagstar Bank, where he served as a senior vice president and managing group director. His expertise in middle market and high net worth client financial solutions will be pivotal in his new role at Peapack Private's Garden City, New York location. Watterson's extensive background includes positions at CitiGroup Private Bank, National Westminster Bank, Marine Midland Bank, and Mellon Financial Services, highlighting his deep industry knowledge and leadership capabilities.

Watterson's academic credentials from Pace University’s Lubin School of Business, along with his professional certifications and active community involvement, underscore his commitment to excellence and service. Peapack Private Bank & Trust, with its client-centric approach and comprehensive financial solutions, stands to benefit significantly from Watterson's leadership. The bank, part of Peapack-Gladstone Financial Corporation, boasts $7.1 billion in total assets and $11.8 billion under management or administration, reflecting its strong position in the banking sector. For more details, visit www.peapackprivate.com.

Full Article

Bruce A. Watterson has recently taken on the role of Senior Managing Director and Managing Group Director at Peapack Private Bank & Trust, a move that underscores the bank's commitment to bolstering its commercial banking division with top-tier talent. Based in Garden City, New York, Watterson's extensive background in corporate, private, and middle market banking is expected to significantly contribute to the bank's expansion in the NY Metro area.

With over forty years of experience in the financial sector, Watterson's career includes key positions at Flagstar Bank, CitiGroup Private Bank, and others, where he honed his expertise in delivering tailored financial solutions. His academic credentials from Pace University’s Lubin School of Business, along with his professional certifications, further validate his capability to drive Peapack Private's client-focused banking initiatives forward.

Beyond his professional achievements, Watterson's active engagement in the Long Island community through various committees and boards reflects his holistic approach to banking—one that values community development as much as financial success. His involvement with organizations like The Energeia Partnership and The Education and Assistance Corp (EAC) demonstrates his commitment to making a positive societal impact.

Peapack Private Bank & Trust, under the umbrella of Peapack-Gladstone Financial Corporation, is poised to leverage Watterson's deep industry knowledge and dedication to client service excellence. This strategic hire not only enhances the bank's ability to offer customized and innovative financial solutions but also strengthens its reputation as a leading provider of wealth management, investment banking, and commercial and personal banking services.

The implications of Watterson's appointment are far-reaching, offering potential benefits not just for Peapack Private's clientele but also for the broader financial services industry. His leadership is expected to foster growth, innovation, and a heightened focus on client satisfaction, setting a new benchmark for banking excellence in the NY Metro area and beyond.
