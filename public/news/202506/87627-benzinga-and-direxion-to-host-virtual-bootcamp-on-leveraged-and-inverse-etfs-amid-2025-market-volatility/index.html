Benzinga and Direxion to Host Virtual Bootcamp on Leveraged and Inverse ETFs Amid 2025 Market Volatility

Newswriter Business and Technology News • June 23, 2025
Read Original Article →

Summary

Benzinga and Direxion are organizing a virtual bootcamp to educate on leveraging and inverse ETFs for navigating 2025's market volatility, featuring insights from industry experts.

Full Article

In response to the evolving financial landscape of 2025, Benzinga, in partnership with Direxion, announces a virtual educational bootcamp focused on 'Navigating Market Volatility with Direxion’s Leveraged and Inverse ETFs.' Scheduled for June 26, 2025, at 11:00 AM ET, this initiative seeks to clarify the role of leveraged and inverse ETFs as strategic instruments during periods of market uncertainty.

The bootcamp will delve into critical areas including the operational framework of leveraged and inverse ETFs, approaches for thriving in volatile markets, and a forward-looking perspective on the 2025 market. Shawn Edwards, Vice President – Institutional ETF Strategist at Direxion, is slated to impart his knowledge, with Gianni Di Poce, a distinguished market analyst, facilitating the conversation. Tailored for both experienced investors and novices to alternative investment options, the event promises to equip attendees with the acumen to navigate the complexities of the current market scenario.

For those interested in enhancing their understanding of these financial tools, registration is available at https://www.benzinga.com/events/direxions-leveraged-and-inverse-etfs/.
