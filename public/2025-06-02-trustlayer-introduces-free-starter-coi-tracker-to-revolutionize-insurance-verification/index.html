TrustLayer Introduces Free Starter COI Tracker to Revolutionize Insurance Verification

Newswriter Business and Technology News • June 2, 2025
Read Original Article →

Summary

TrustLayer, a San Francisco-based company, has launched the TrustLayer Starter COI Tracker, a free tool designed to revolutionize the way businesses verify Certificates of Insurance (COIs). This innovative solution addresses the cumbersome and error-prone manual processes traditionally associated with COI verification, offering a streamlined, user-friendly platform for uploading, storing, and managing COIs. With features aimed at enhancing collaboration and transparency between businesses and their partners, the COI Tracker is set to improve operational efficiency and reduce costly delays. TrustLayer's commitment to accessibility and security is evident in the tool's design, which includes robust security measures and extensive support resources. The launch of the Free COI tool underscores a broader shift towards digital transformation in business processes, with TrustLayer leading the charge in insurance technology innovation.
