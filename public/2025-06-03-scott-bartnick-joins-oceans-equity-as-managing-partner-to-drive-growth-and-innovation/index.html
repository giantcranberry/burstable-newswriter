Scott Bartnick Joins Oceans Equity as Managing Partner to Drive Growth and Innovation

Summary

Miami-based private equity firm Oceans Equity has announced the appointment of Scott Bartnick as Managing Partner. Bartnick, who brings a wealth of experience in driving growth across various industries, will co-lead the firm's operations, investment activities, and portfolio oversight. His previous role at Boyne Capital saw him play a pivotal role in sourcing and executing investments, as well as working closely with management teams to foster long-term value creation.

Josh Bilmes, Managing Partner of Oceans Equity, expressed his enthusiasm for Bartnick's joining, highlighting their long-standing friendship and shared vision for the firm. Bartnick himself is excited to contribute to the firm's momentum, which includes securing commitments from top institutional investors and closing its first platform investment. Oceans Equity, known for its focus on service businesses and niche product companies, continues to build a deep pipeline of actionable opportunities under its new leadership.

Full Article

Oceans Equity, a Miami-based private equity firm, has taken a significant step forward by appointing Scott Bartnick as its new Managing Partner. This strategic hire is expected to play a pivotal role in enhancing the firm's operational capabilities, investment strategies, and portfolio management. Bartnick's extensive experience in fostering growth across diverse industries, including business services, consumer, and healthcare, positions him as a key asset to Oceans Equity's mission.

With a career marked by successful collaborations with founders and executives, Bartnick has a proven track record of driving both organic growth and facilitating mergers and acquisitions. His previous tenure as a Managing Director at Boyne Capital was instrumental in sourcing and executing investments, as well as in developing strategic initiatives aimed at long-term value creation. His expertise is anticipated to bring a fresh perspective to Oceans Equity's investment approach.

Josh Bilmes, Managing Partner of Oceans Equity, expressed his enthusiasm about Bartnick's appointment, citing their longstanding professional relationship and shared vision for the firm's future. Bilmes highlighted Bartnick's leadership qualities and deep industry knowledge as critical factors that will support the firm's objectives of empowering entrepreneurs and executives within its portfolio.

Bartnick's arrival comes at a strategic time for Oceans Equity, as the firm looks to capitalize on a strong pipeline of investment opportunities and commitments from leading institutional investors. His role will be crucial in accelerating the firm's growth trajectory and in identifying high-growth investment opportunities for its partners. Bartnick's commitment to sustainable growth and collaboration aligns with the firm's ethos, setting a solid foundation for future success.

This leadership enhancement underscores Oceans Equity's commitment to being at the forefront of the private equity sector, with a focus on service businesses and niche product companies. The firm's dedication to fostering sustainable growth and innovation, under the co-leadership of Bartnick and Bilmes, signals a promising future for Oceans Equity and its stakeholders.
