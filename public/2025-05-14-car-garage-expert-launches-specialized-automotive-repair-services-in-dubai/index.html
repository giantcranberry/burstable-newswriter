Car Garage Expert Launches Specialized Automotive Repair Services in Dubai

Newswriter Business and Technology News • May 14, 2025
Read Original Article →

Summary

Car Garage Expert, a premier automotive repair brand, has launched operations in Dubai, offering specialized services for sunroof repair and car electrical systems. The company brings industry experience and certified professionals to meet the growing demand for these services in the emirate.

With modern diagnostic tools, transparent pricing, and a customer-first approach, Car Garage Expert aims to become the go-to name for auto care in Dubai. CEO Mian Muhammad Fahad Malik emphasizes the importance of expert-level service that is accessible and affordable for Dubai drivers.
