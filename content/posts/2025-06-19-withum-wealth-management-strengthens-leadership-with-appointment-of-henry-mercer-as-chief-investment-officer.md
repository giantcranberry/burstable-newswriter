<p>Withum Wealth Management has taken a significant step in bolstering its leadership team by appointing Henry Mercer as its new Chief Investment Officer. This strategic hire is poised to enhance the firm's ability to deliver personalized investment solutions and comprehensive financial guidance to its clients. Mercer's extensive career, spanning over four decades in investment management, brings a depth of knowledge and strategic insight that is expected to propel Withum Wealth Management forward in the competitive financial advisory sector.</p><p>Mercer's professional journey includes founding Mercer Capital Advisers, Inc. in 1999, where he served as President until his retirement in 2022. His tenure as President and Founding Partner of Mercer, Bokert, Buckman & Reid, Inc. further solidifies his reputation as a seasoned investment advisor. Scott Brown, President of Pinnacle Associates, Ltd., has lauded Mercer for his strategic acumen and the strong relationships he has built over the years, qualities that are anticipated to align seamlessly with Withum Wealth Management's objectives.</p><p>Expressing his excitement about joining Withum Wealth Management, Mercer shared his admiration for the firm's growth trajectory and his eagerness to contribute to its continued success. His leadership is expected to be instrumental in driving the firm's mission of helping clients achieve long-term financial success. Beyond his professional achievements, Mercer's commitment to community engagement and education, evidenced by his service on the Board of Trustees at Monmouth University, underscores his holistic approach to leadership and excellence.</p><p>The appointment of Henry Mercer as Chief Investment Officer is a clear indication of Withum Wealth Management's commitment to excellence and innovation in financial advisory services. For industry leaders and clients alike, this development signals a promising future for the firm, as it seeks to leverage Mercer's expertise to navigate the complexities of the financial markets and deliver superior investment solutions.</p>
