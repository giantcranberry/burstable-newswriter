This move is part of Meta's broader strategy to reinforce its position in the competitive AI sector, following setbacks with its Llama 4 model and delays in launching the 'Behemoth' AI model. The partnership will see Scale AI's CEO, Alexandr Wang, joining Meta to lead a new 'superintelligence' lab, collaborating with a dedicated research team to push the boundaries of AI technology.</p><p>The investment comes at a time when U.S. antitrust authorities are intensifying their scrutiny of minority investments, particularly under the Clayton Act, raising potential regulatory challenges for Meta.
