<p>Mobile-first fintech company mPWR has launched its inaugural U.S. investment opportunity, generating early investor interest since opening on September 15. The offering provides accredited investors with access to a global financial inclusion movement that targets both financial returns and social impact objectives.</p><p>The company's mission centers on empowering the 1.1 billion underbanked individuals and 1.4 billion micro-entrepreneurs worldwide through a comprehensive mobile ecosystem. Each affordable smartphone, available through small installment payment plans, includes bundled connectivity, digital wallet services, remittance capabilities, microloans, and access to live television, streaming content, and educational resources. The platform employs AI-driven credit scoring technology and operates using the mPWR Utility Token, which enables seamless payment processing and rewards systems.</p><p>CEO Oscar Rojas noted the early momentum, emphasizing that accredited investors now have the opportunity to participate in a mobile-first platform ecosystem designed to deliver both profitability and global social impact. The investment offering is structured under Regulation D 506(c), with mPWR engaging Andes Capital Group as the broker-dealer responsible for overseeing issuance, compliance matters, regulatory filings, KYC/AML procedures, and investor accreditation verification. Additional investment structure details are accessible at <a href="https://invest.thempwr.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://invest.thempwr.com</a>.</p><p>The company emphasizes several key investment advantages, including global scale with U.S. market access, enabling American investors to participate in a fintech inclusion movement beginning in Mexico with planned worldwide expansion. The leadership team brings over 300 years of combined expertise across fintech, telecommunications, media, cryptocurrency, and emerging markets. The dual focus on impact and profitability targets serving massive underserved populations while pursuing strong financial returns through a customer-centric value proposition designed to drive adoption and long-term loyalty.</p><p>mPWR has partnered with KoreTransfer, which serves as registered transfer agent responsible for securities issuance and tracking, maintaining the company's shareholder register, and providing investor communications and reporting infrastructure through digital platforms. The comprehensive financial inclusion approach combines affordable smartphone access with integrated banking tools, micro-lending, digital services, education, and gamified engagement, creating sustainable value for both underserved communities and investors. Additional information and investment participation are available through the official portal at <a href="https://invest.thempwr.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://invest.thempwr.com</a>.</p>
