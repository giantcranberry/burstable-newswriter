<p>One World Products, Inc., which will soon be renamed Isiah Enterprises, has expanded its Board of Directors from three to seven members with the appointment of three distinguished leaders. This strategic move enhances the company's governance structure as it accelerates growth in sustainable manufacturing and prepares for its next phase of expansion under the leadership of Chairman and CEO Isiah Thomas.</p><p>The newly elected board members bring diverse expertise across political strategy, corporate operations, and financial leadership. Minyon Moore, recognized as one of the nation's top strategic thinkers, brings deep experience in political and corporate affairs, public policy, and brand strategy. She leads Dewey Square Group's State and Local Affairs and Multicultural Strategies practices and most recently served as Chair of the 2024 Democratic National Convention in Chicago.</p><p>Timothy Woods joins the board as an accomplished executive with extensive expertise in corporate operations, business development, and strategic partnerships. His background includes leadership positions across multiple industries with a proven track record of scaling organizations and fostering innovation while specializing in growth strategies that align operational efficiency with sustainable business practices.</p><p>William Rowland adds veteran financial leadership with decades of experience in corporate finance, accounting, and governance. His background includes serving as an advisor and senior executive for both public and private companies, where he guided financial strategy, strengthened compliance, and ensured transparency for stakeholders.</p><p>Our expansion from three to seven board members marks an important milestone for One World Products as we prepare to transition into Isiah Enterprises, said Isiah Thomas, Chairman and CEO. Minyon Moore, Timothy Woods, and Bill Rowland each bring unmatched expertise and integrity that will strengthen our governance, sharpen our strategy, and help position the company to lead in sustainable manufacturing while delivering long-term value for our shareholders.</p><p>The company recently acquired Eco Bio Plastics Manufacturing in Michigan, a facility specializing in pelletizing, micronizing, and pulverizing rubber and other bio-based feedstocks into advanced materials for the automotive and packaging industries. One World Products partners with major industry players including Stellantis, Flex-N-Gate, Orbis Corporation, West Michigan Compounding, and Full Circle Technologies, and is currently shipping tens of thousands of pounds of product each week.</p><p>As the first minority-controlled, athlete-led publicly traded company in sustainable manufacturing, One World Products is committed to innovation, community empowerment, and measurable environmental impact. The company processes rubber, cellulose, hemp, and a wide range of bio-feedstocks into advanced composites for automotive, industrial, and food packaging applications. For more information about the company's operations and strategic direction, visit <a href="https://oneworldproducts.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://oneworldproducts.com</a>.</p>
