<p>Royalty Management Holding Corporation (Nasdaq: RMCO) has issued a reminder to shareholders regarding the upcoming payment of its quarterly cash dividend. The company confirmed that shareholders of record on September 30, 2025, will receive a dividend payment of $0.0025 per share, with payment scheduled for October 10, 2025. This represents the second payment in a series of quarterly cash dividends implemented by the company earlier this year.</p><p>The dividend initiative maintains an annualized rate of $0.01 per share, distributed in quarterly installments of $0.0025 per share. Following this payment, additional quarterly dividends are scheduled for shareholders of record on December 31, 2025, and March 31, 2026. The consistent dividend schedule provides investors with predictable income streams and demonstrates the company's financial stability.</p><p>Thomas Sauve, Chief Executive Officer of Royalty Management, emphasized the significance of the dividend program in the company's shareholder return strategy. With the recent implementation of our quarterly cash dividend, we wanted to take the opportunity to simply remind our shareholders that the next dividend record date was close at hand on September 30, 2025, Sauve stated. He noted that this payment follows the previous quarterly dividend declared on June 30, 2025, as part of the series established by the company's Board of Directors.</p><p>The dividend program reflects Royalty Management's financial position and its approach to capital allocation. We are thankful to be in a good cash-generating position from our core assets to be able to have this quarterly cash dividend as part of our overall toolkit for mechanisms to provide positive returns to our shareholders, Sauve explained. The company's ability to maintain regular dividend payments signals strong operational performance and reliable cash flow generation from its diverse asset portfolio.</p><p>Sauve further elaborated on the company's broader financial strategy, stating, Furthermore, as we have emphasized in the past, our focus is on continual revenue and cash flow growth, returning capital back to our shareholders through these dividends, and opportunistic stock repurchases. He indicated that as the company continues to strengthen and grow, management will work with the Board of Directors to ensure the cash dividend remains balanced while continuing to serve as an important mechanism for shareholder returns.</p><p>Royalty Management operates as a royalty company that builds shareholder value by acquiring and developing assets across various market environments. The company's approach involves structuring cash flow streams around assets that can support communities while monetizing existing cash flows and identifying transitional cash flows for future growth. Additional information about the company is available at <a href="https://www.royaltymgmtcorp.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.royaltymgmtcorp.com</a>.</p><p>For business leaders and technology investors, Royalty Management's consistent dividend program represents a significant indicator of financial health and strategic capital management. The company's ability to generate sufficient cash flow to support regular shareholder returns while maintaining growth initiatives demonstrates a balanced approach to corporate finance that appeals to income-focused investors in the technology and resource sectors.</p>
