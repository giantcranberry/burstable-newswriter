<p>Datavault AI Inc. has secured a $150 million strategic investment from Scilex Holding Company that will be executed in Bitcoin across two tranches. The investment includes an initial $8.1 million closing on September 26, 2025, with the remaining $141.9 million following stockholder approval. This substantial capital infusion is expected to significantly strengthen Datavault AI's balance sheet while funding expansion in key growth areas including supercomputing infrastructure, independent data exchanges, and new revenue-generating platforms.</p><p>The investment represents a major validation of Datavault AI's position as a leader in AI-driven data visualization, valuation, and monetization technologies. The company's cloud-based platform provides comprehensive solutions with a collaborative focus through its Acoustic Science and Data Science Divisions. The Acoustic Science Division features patented technologies including WiSA, ADIO, and Sumerian foundational spatial and multichannel wireless HD sound transmission technologies with intellectual property covering audio timing, synchronization, and multi-channel interference cancellation.</p><p>Datavault AI's Data Science Division leverages Web 3.0 and high-performance computing to provide solutions for experiential data perception, valuation, and secure monetization. The company's technology serves multiple industries through HPC software licensing for sports and entertainment, events and venues, biotech, education, fintech, real estate, healthcare, energy, and other sectors. The Information Data Exchange enables Digital Twins and licensing of name, image, and likeness by securely attaching physical real-world objects to immutable metadata objects, fostering responsible AI with integrity.</p><p>The company's complete technology suite offers AI and Machine Learning automation, third-party integration, detailed analytics, marketing automation, and advertising monitoring capabilities.
