<p>TX Group will commence its previously announced share buyback program on September 26, 2025, marking a significant capital management initiative for the Swiss media company. The program, first detailed in the company's half-year results press release on August 26, 2025, involves repurchasing up to 6.25% of the outstanding share capital over a maximum three-year period. This substantial commitment corresponds to a volume of up to 662,500 registered shares, representing one of the most significant capital return initiatives in the Swiss media sector.</p><p>The primary objectives of this initiative are the efficient use of capital and returning funds to shareholders, reflecting the company's confidence in its financial position and commitment to shareholder value.
