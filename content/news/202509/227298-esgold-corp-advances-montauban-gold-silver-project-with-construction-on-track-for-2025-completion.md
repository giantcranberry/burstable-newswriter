The company reported being fully funded to complete the Montauban project while simultaneously advancing preparatory work at its Colombian operations. This financial stability allows ESGold to pursue both immediate production goals and long-term strategic development across its portfolio.</p><p>Technical work at the Montauban property has identified large continuous geological structures extending to depths of 1,200 meters, significantly expanding the project's potential scale. The property, located 80 kilometers west of Quebec City, represents ESGold's flagship operation and serves as a model for the company's commitment to responsible mining practices. According to CEO Gordon Robb, ESGold is positioned to generate near-term cash flow while pursuing long-term growth opportunities, balancing immediate operational needs with strategic expansion.</p><p>Upcoming catalysts for the company include concentrate test results from both the Montauban and Colombian projects, which will provide critical data for production planning.
