<p>PowerBank Corporation (NASDAQ: SUUN) will present at the ArcStone-Kingswood Growth Summit on September 18, 2025, in Toronto, where President and CEO Dr. Richard Lu will outline the company's recent achievements and future growth strategy in renewable energy development. The summit brings together more than 20 high-growth companies with institutional and retail investors through keynote presentations, panel discussions, and one-on-one meetings designed to connect investment opportunities.</p><p>The company's focus on distributed and community solar projects across Canada and the United States positions it at the forefront of the transition to clean energy. PowerBank develops solar and Battery Energy Storage System (BESS) projects that sell electricity to utilities, commercial, industrial, municipal, and residential off-takers, maximizing returns through a diversified portfolio approach. With a potential development pipeline exceeding one gigawatt and already having developed projects with over 100 megawatts of capacity, the company demonstrates significant scalability in renewable infrastructure.</p><p>This presentation comes at a critical time for renewable energy investment, as markets increasingly prioritize sustainable infrastructure. The company's participation in events like the ArcStone-Kingswood Growth Summit, accessible through <a href="https://www.powerbankcorp.com/investors" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">their investor relations portal</a>, provides visibility into how distributed energy projects contribute to grid stability and decarbonization goals. Investors can review detailed information about forward-looking statements and risk factors in the company's SEC filings available at <a href="https://www.powerbankcorp.com/regulatory" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">their regulatory disclosures page</a>.</p><p>The growth summit represents an important venue for emerging companies in the renewable sector to engage with capital markets. PowerBank's emphasis on community solar and virtual net metering projects addresses growing demand for localized energy solutions that benefit both consumers and utilities. As the company continues to expand its footprint across North America, its development strategy offers insights into how renewable energy projects can achieve both environmental impact and financial returns.</p>
