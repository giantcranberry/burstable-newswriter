<p>B2i Digital, Inc. has been selected as Marketing Partner for the 4th Annual ROTH Healthcare Opportunities Conference, scheduled for October 8-9, 2025 at the Metropolitan Club in New York City. This partnership represents a significant development in how investor conferences leverage digital marketing expertise to enhance engagement between growth-oriented healthcare companies and institutional investors.</p><p>The invitation-only event serves as a premier forum connecting institutional investors with senior executives from approximately 30 innovative healthcare companies in biotechnology, medical technologies, and pharmaceuticals. The conference structure includes both one-on-one and small group meetings lasting 25 minutes each, designed to facilitate meaningful dialogue between investors and company management. Themed panel discussions will cover critical healthcare topics including small firms tackling blockbuster indications, acute myeloid leukemia developments, FDA regulatory insights, and innovation in underserved medical indications.</p><p>As Marketing Partner, B2i Digital will leverage its proprietary network of over 1.4 million market participants to enhance investor attendance and provide targeted visibility for presenting companies. David Shapiro, CEO of B2i Digital, emphasized the significance of the partnership, stating that ROTH literally wrote the playbook on investor conferences and that his firm is humbled to play a role in advancing that legacy. The collaboration underscores B2i Digital's mission to connect growth-oriented companies with appropriate investors through strategic digital marketing and event partnerships.</p><p>The conference partnership demonstrates the evolving landscape of investor relations, where digital marketing technology combines with capital markets expertise to deliver institutional-caliber exposure. B2i Digital's approach through its <a href="https://www.b2idigital.com/featured-conference" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">Featured Conference</a>, <a href="https://www.b2idigital.com/featured-company" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">Featured Company</a>, and <a href="https://www.b2idigital.com/featured-expert" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">Featured Expert programs</a> represents a modern methodology for connecting healthcare innovators with family offices, portfolio managers, and high-net-worth investors. ROTH Capital Partners, as a full-service investment bank focused on growth companies, maintains its position as a trusted partner in the life sciences and healthcare sectors through conferences like this healthcare opportunities event.</p><p>This marketing partnership highlights the increasing importance of targeted digital outreach in the capital markets ecosystem, particularly for healthcare companies seeking institutional investment. The conference's exclusive nature and structured engagement format reflect the sophisticated approach required to match innovative healthcare companies with appropriate funding sources in today's competitive investment landscape.</p>
