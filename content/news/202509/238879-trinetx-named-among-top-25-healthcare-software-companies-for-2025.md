<p>The Healthcare Technology Report has named TriNetX one of the Top 25 Healthcare Software Companies of 2025, recognizing the company's rapid growth and transformative contributions to life sciences and healthcare. This prestigious award acknowledges TriNetX's pioneering software solutions that leverage real-world data to accelerate clinical research and drug development. Steve Kundrot, Chief Operating Officer of TriNetX, emphasized that this recognition reflects the company's vision of powering innovation across the healthcare ecosystem through data, intelligence, and technology.</p><p>The company's approach combines a global, privacy-compliant network of real-world data with advanced AI and analytics to address current clinical research challenges while building foundations for future advancements in precision and speed in drug development. TriNetX's flagship platform, TriNetX LIVE, integrates de-identified patient data from over 300 million patients across more than 200 healthcare organizations and 8,800 sites worldwide. This globally federated network provides researchers with enriched datasets including electronic health records, genomics, and claims data, enabling precise patient cohort building and real-time advanced analyses. Additional information about The Healthcare Technology Report's Top 25 Healthcare Software Companies of 2025, including the full list of awardees, can be found on <a href="https://thehealthcaretechnologyreport.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">their website</a>.</p><p>In 2025, TriNetX continues to innovate with next-generation features that simplify workflows, improve collaboration, and enhance data usability. The platform's upcoming integration of large language models promises to revolutionize how researchers query electronic health records, making clinical insights accessible through simple natural language questions. For more information about TriNetX's platform and services, visit <a href="https://www.trinetx.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">www.trinetx.com</a>.</p><p>The practical impact of TriNetX's technology is demonstrated through multiple applications. AI-driven models show promise in improving patient recruitment and enabling earlier detection in diseases such as Crohn's, pancreatic cancer, and lupus. Protocol optimization tools have helped reduce costly trial amendments by up to 50%, while digital waiting rooms and site intelligence are reshaping clinical trial recruitment methods to improve representativeness, efficiency, and results. TriNetX's network has supported over 2,000 peer-reviewed publications and is actively influencing clinical practice, regulatory decisions, and global health policy.</p><p>This recognition underscores the growing importance of real-world data and advanced analytics in driving healthcare innovation and improving patient outcomes worldwide. For business and technology leaders, TriNetX's achievements signal a broader transformation in how data-driven approaches are reshaping healthcare delivery and pharmaceutical development. The company's success demonstrates the commercial viability of healthcare technology platforms that can effectively bridge the gap between clinical research and real-world patient care, creating new opportunities for innovation across the healthcare ecosystem.</p>
