<p>Cycurion, Inc. has announced $4.6 million in new contract awards to be earned over the next year, building on previously announced $69 million in contracts for a total of $73.6 million in AI-powered growth across multiple sectors. The company is leveraging its proprietary AI-enhanced Cyber Shield / ARx platform across more than 15 engagements with contract durations ranging from 1 to 10 years.</p><p>The additional contracts, launching in September 2025, include several key initiatives that highlight the expanding role of artificial intelligence in critical infrastructure protection. Cycurion will deliver AI-powered IT and cybersecurity assessment services for a major county government, enhancing operational resilience and security with advanced threat detection capabilities.</p><p>The company is partnering with a leading system integrator to support a critical health and human services IT implementation, leveraging AI to optimize and streamline essential operations. Another significant engagement involves providing AI-driven cybersecurity, business continuity, and disaster recovery solutions for a prominent healthcare organization, strengthening defenses against evolving threats through predictive analytics.</p><p>Cycurion will also support networking and cybersecurity infrastructure for a large federal government agency, utilizing AI to ensure secure, efficient, and resilient operations. The company is launching a significant engagement with a technology partner to deliver AI-enhanced solutions for another large county government, driving operational excellence through advanced technology integration.</p><p>The contract expansion includes advancing national computer-aided dispatch (CAD) and 911 initiatives through a strategic partnership, integrating AI to improve public safety outcomes and response times. This initiative represents the growing importance of artificial intelligence in emergency response systems and public safety infrastructure.</p><p>Cycurion projects the $4.6 million run rate in additional contracts will contribute $2.1 million in the third quarter of 2025 and an additional $2.5 million by the end of the fourth quarter of 2025. The company's growth reflects the increasing market demand for AI-powered cybersecurity solutions as organizations across multiple sectors seek advanced protection against evolving digital threats.</p><p>Through these strategic partnerships, Cycurion is positioning itself at the forefront of AI-driven digital transformation in cybersecurity. The company's expanding contract portfolio demonstrates the critical role artificial intelligence plays in modern cybersecurity infrastructure and the growing confidence in AI-enhanced solutions for protecting sensitive government, healthcare, and corporate operations. More information about the company's services and solutions is available at <a href="https://www.cycurion.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.cycurion.com</a>.</p>
