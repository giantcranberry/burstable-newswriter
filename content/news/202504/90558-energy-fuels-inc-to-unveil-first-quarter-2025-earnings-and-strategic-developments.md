<p>Energy Fuels Inc., a key player in the U.S. critical minerals sector, has announced its first-quarter 2025 earnings conference call scheduled for Thursday, May 8, at 9:00 a.m. Mountain Time. This event is anticipated to shed light on the company's operational progress and strategic initiatives, particularly in uranium production, rare earth elements (REEs), vanadium, and medical isotopes.</p><p>The company's White Mesa Mill in Utah stands as the nation's sole fully licensed conventional uranium processing facility, underscoring Energy Fuels' pivotal role in the nuclear energy and critical minerals industries. With an expanding focus on REEs and medical isotopes, the company is poised to contribute significantly to advancements in technology and healthcare, including potential breakthroughs in cancer treatment technologies through the recovery of medical isotopes from uranium processing streams.</p><p>Energy Fuels' international endeavors, including projects in Kenya, Madagascar, Brazil, and Australia, further demonstrate its strategic positioning in the global critical minerals market. The upcoming earnings call is expected to provide investors and industry observers with valuable insights into the company's performance and future direction, particularly its efforts to capitalize on the growing demand for critical minerals and clean energy solutions.</p>
