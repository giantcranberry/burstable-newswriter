<p>Sekur Private Data Ltd. (CSE: SKUR) (OTCQX: SWISF) (FSE: GDT0), a Swiss-hosted cybersecurity and private communications platform, has announced a significant step in its global expansion strategy with the appointment of Christophe Kabeya as Director of Government and Corporate Sales for Central and Southern Africa. Kabeya's role is pivotal in driving the company's growth in the Democratic Republic of Congo and Angola, leveraging his over 25 years of experience in finance, capital markets, and investor relations.</p><p>The company aims to initiate government-level discussions and achieve full operational status in these countries by September 2025. This expansion is part of Sekur's broader strategy to tap into a combined market of over 150 million people, offering its Corporate and Premium bundled solutions. These solutions include encrypted emails, secure messengers, and other communication tools designed to meet the increasing demand for secure data management and private communications.</p><p>This move comes at a time when the region is facing heightened vulnerabilities to cyberattacks, making Sekur's offerings particularly relevant. The appointment of Kabeya is expected to accelerate the company's penetration into these markets, contributing to substantial revenue growth. For more information, visit <a href='https://www.Sekur.com' rel='nofollow' target='_blank'>https://www.Sekur.com</a>.</p>
