<p>Energy Fuels Inc. (NYSE American: UUUU) (TSX: EFR), a leading U.S. uranium and critical minerals producer, has announced a conference call for August 7, 2025, to discuss its second-quarter financial results. This event is set to provide insights into the company's performance and its impact on the critical minerals market, which is integral to carbon-free nuclear energy and innovative cancer treatments.</p><p>The conference call, scheduled for 9:00 AM MT, will be accessible via a Rapid Connect link or by dialing 1-800-715-9871. Energy Fuels' operations, notably the White Mesa Mill in Utah—the sole fully licensed conventional uranium processing facility in the U.S.—and its global heavy mineral sands projects, underscore its significant contribution to the supply chain of critical minerals.</p><p>This financial review comes at a time when the demand for uranium and rare earth elements is surging, driven by the global shift towards clean energy and the development of advanced medical technologies. Energy Fuels' upcoming discussion on its Q2 results is not just a reflection of the company's health but also a barometer for the critical minerals sector at large. Stakeholders and industry observers are keenly awaiting this update, as it may offer valuable insights into the future direction of this vital industry.</p>
