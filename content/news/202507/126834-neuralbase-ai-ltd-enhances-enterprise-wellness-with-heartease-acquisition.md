<p>NeuralBase AI Ltd. (OTC: NBBI), through its enterprise brand BMP AI, has announced the acquisition of HeartEase, an AI-powered diagnostic platform that leverages smartphone microphones for early detection of heart abnormalities. This feature not only enhances accessibility but also ensures real-time health monitoring, making preventive care a seamless part of the daily digital experience for employees. Vighnesh Dobale, CEO of NeuralBase, underscored the transformative impact of HeartEase, highlighting its potential to revolutionize early detection of heart conditions through the use of artificial intelligence and mobile technology.</p><p>With its backend AI engine already trained on validated clinical datasets, HeartEase is on track to launch a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) by Q4 2025. The MVP aims to serve enterprise clients, wellness programs, and rural health campaigns, further emphasizing the platform's scalability and global accessibility.
