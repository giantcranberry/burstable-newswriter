<p>RegScale has announced the promotion of Dale Hoak to the position of Chief Information Security Officer (CISO), a move that highlights the company's commitment to strengthening its cybersecurity framework and leadership in the industry. Hoak, a seasoned cybersecurity expert and U.S. Navy veteran, has played a pivotal role in RegScale's journey towards achieving FedRAMP High authorization, a testament to the company's robust security measures and compliance standards.</p><p>Travis Howerton, Co-Founder and CEO of RegScale, commended Hoak for his exceptional leadership and contributions, particularly in advancing Continuous Controls Monitoring and securing key certifications such as SOC 2 Type 2 and CSA STAR. These achievements have not only elevated RegScale's status in the cybersecurity domain but have also streamlined the compliance process for its customers, significantly cutting down on certification times and audit preparation efforts.</p><p>In his elevated role as CISO, Hoak is set to lead RegScale's cybersecurity strategy, ensuring that security remains at the forefront of innovation, trust, and business growth. His vast experience, including significant roles within the Department of Defense and the private sector, brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to RegScale, promising to further solidify the company's position as a leader in integrating compliance into DevSecOps pipelines.</p><p>This strategic promotion reflects RegScale's ongoing dedication to cybersecurity excellence and its ability to attract and retain top-tier talent like Hoak. As cybersecurity threats continue to evolve, Hoak's leadership will be instrumental in navigating these challenges, ensuring that RegScale and its customers remain at the cutting edge of security and compliance solutions.</p>
