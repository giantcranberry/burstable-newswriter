<p>Sekur Private Data Ltd. (CSE: SKUR) (OTCQX: SWISF) (FSE: GDT0), a Swiss-hosted cybersecurity and communications solutions provider, has announced a price increase across all its service tiers. This strategic move is in direct response to the escalating demand for private cybersecurity solutions, driven by the uptick in Business Email Compromise (BEC) and AI-driven attacks. Targeting high-value clients in finance, law, and government sectors, the company aims to boost its average revenue per user by at least 50%.</p><p>Alongside the price adjustment, Sekur is rolling out new features aimed at bolstering security and compliance. These enhancements include alias emails, SEC-compliant message archiving, and white-label capabilities. The company's proprietary platform stands out by not engaging in data mining, offering secure communication solutions that operate independently of big tech infrastructure. This unique approach ensures the privacy and security of client data, directly addressing the increasing global concerns over data privacy and cybersecurity threats.</p><p>The decision to increase prices reflects the growing value and necessity of robust cybersecurity measures in today's digital landscape. With cyber threats becoming more sophisticated, businesses and government entities are prioritizing secure communication channels to protect sensitive information. Sekur's latest offerings and pricing strategy underscore its commitment to providing top-tier security solutions that cater to the evolving needs of its clientele.</p>
