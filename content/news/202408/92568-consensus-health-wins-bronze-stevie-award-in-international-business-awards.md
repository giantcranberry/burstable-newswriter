<p>Consensus Health, a New Jersey-based healthcare organization, has been awarded a bronze Stevie® Award in the 21st Annual International Business Awards® (IBAs), marking a significant achievement in the Company of the Year - Health Products & Services - Large category. This accolade highlights the organization's leadership and innovative approach in the healthcare sector, amidst fierce competition from over 3,600 nominations across 62 nations and territories.</p><p>Michael Lovett, CEO of Consensus Health, shared his pride in the company's recognition, linking it to their recent accolades in the American Business Awards and their unwavering commitment to supporting independent physicians in New Jersey. The company's growth over the past two years, particularly in expanding its provider network, locations, and patient enrollment in value-based care programs, played a pivotal role in securing this prestigious award.</p><p>As a physician-owned and -governed medical group, Consensus Health operates 56 practices across 69 locations in 18 New Jersey counties, with a total of 166 member physicians. Their model emphasizes clinical autonomy, enabling physicians to deliver high-quality patient care tailored to local needs. This approach has not only improved healthcare delivery and patient outcomes but also set a benchmark for balancing physician independence with organizational growth.</p><p>The Stevie Awards, known for recognizing excellence in the workplace globally, further validate Consensus Health's innovative strategies in healthcare management. Their success serves as an inspiration for other healthcare organizations navigating the complexities of modern healthcare delivery. For more details on the International Business Awards and the winners, visit <a href='https://www.StevieAwards.com/IBA' rel='nofollow' target='_blank'>https://www.StevieAwards.com/IBA</a>.</p>
