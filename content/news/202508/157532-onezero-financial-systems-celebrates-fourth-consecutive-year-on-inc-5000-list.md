<p>oneZero Financial Systems has achieved a notable milestone by securing its position on the Inc. 5000 list for the fourth consecutive year, a testament to its sustained growth and innovation in the capital markets technology sector. This prestigious ranking by Inc. magazine highlights the most dynamic and successful independent businesses in the U.S. economy, with oneZero's inclusion reflecting its financial performance and resilience in the face of economic challenges.</p><p>Andrew Ralich, CEO and Co-Founder of oneZero, credited the company's success to its dedicated team and the trust of its clients and partners. The company's focus on developing resilient, client-driven, and scalable technology has been central to its growth. The median three-year revenue growth rate among the top 500 companies on this year's list was an impressive 1,552 percent, with these companies collectively adding over 48,678 jobs to the U.S. economy. oneZero's consistent presence underscores its role in fostering economic vitality. For more information on the Inc. 5000 list, visit <a href='https://www.inc.com/inc5000' rel='nofollow' target='_blank'>https://www.inc.com/inc5000</a>.</p><p>Since its founding in 2009 in Cambridge, Massachusetts, oneZero has expanded its services from the foreign exchange market to providing multi-asset class enterprise trading technology to a global clientele. Recent strategic moves, including the acquisition of Autochartist and investment by Golden Gate Capital, highlight oneZero's commitment to innovation and expansion. The Inc. 5000 list not only celebrates the achievements of companies like oneZero but also serves as a reminder of the entrepreneurial spirit that drives the U.S. economy forward.</p>
