<p>Bolt Insight, led by CEO and Co-Founder Hakan Yurdakul, has launched BoltChatAI, a groundbreaking platform that uses artificial intelligence to redefine the qualitative research landscape. Since its inception in 2019, BoltChatAI has enabled real-time conversations with consumers in over 50 markets, offering businesses a swift and efficient method to collect and analyze consumer feedback. This innovation tackles the longstanding issues of qualitative research, including high costs, slow processing times, and lack of flexibility, by merging the depth of qualitative interviews with the speed and scalability of AI technology.</p><p>The platform has made a significant impact across various sectors, including FMCG, healthcare, retail, and financial services, by allowing companies to test product ideas, refine creative concepts, and explore new markets with remarkable speed and depth. A notable example includes a global brand that utilized BoltChatAI to test unbranded product ideas across the U.S. market, swiftly gathering hundreds of in-depth responses that guided strategic decisions and unified internal teams.</p><p>Yurdakul emphasizes the progression of AI in consumer insights from automation to augmentation, where AI now facilitates real-time conversations, generates summaries, and identifies key themes. This advancement has not only improved the functionality of research platforms but also increased the strategic importance of insight teams within organizations. As a result, board-level executives are now more aware of the strategic value of consumer insights, with AI being instrumental in converting complex data into actionable intelligence.</p><p>Looking to the future, Yurdakul anticipates a scenario where conversational intelligence enables brands to interact with dynamic consumer personas and test ideas in real time. BoltChatAI's capability to deliver continuous, self-serve insights represents a major shift from reactive research to a proactive, always-on methodology. The triumph of Bolt Insight and BoltChatAI is rooted in their combination of deep research expertise, AI, and product innovation, ensuring that the human aspect remains central to consumer insights. For more details on Bolt Insight's transformative approach to consumer research, visit <a href='https://www.boltinsight.com/' rel='nofollow' target='_blank'>https://www.boltinsight.com/</a>.</p>
