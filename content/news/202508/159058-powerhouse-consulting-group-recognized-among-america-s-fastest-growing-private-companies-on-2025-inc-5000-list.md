<p>Powerhouse Consulting Group has achieved a notable milestone by being ranked No. 410 on the 2025 Inc. 5000 list, a prestigious compilation of America's fastest-growing private companies. This recognition not only celebrates the company's exceptional growth but also its contribution to revolutionizing the home service sector with tailored software solutions aimed at enhancing operational efficiency and revenue growth for contractors.</p><p>Jenny Benbrook, CEO and co-founder of Powerhouse Consulting Group, shared her pride in this achievement, viewing it as a validation of the company's mission to transform the technological landscape for contractors. The Inc. 5000 list is renowned for featuring industry giants in their early stages, such as Microsoft and Under Armour, making Powerhouse Consulting Group's inclusion a testament to its potential and impact. The list serves as a benchmark for entrepreneurial success, with this year's honorees demonstrating remarkable resilience and innovation in the face of economic challenges.</p><p>The median three-year revenue growth rate among the top 500 companies on the list stood at an impressive 1,507 percent, collectively adding over 52,805 jobs to the U.S. economy. Powerhouse Consulting Group's recognition reflects its significant role in the home service sector, where it provides strategic guidance and software optimization services to contractors. For those interested in exploring the full list, more information is available at <a href='https://www.inc.com/inc5000' rel='nofollow' target='_blank'>https://www.inc.com/inc5000</a>.</p><p>Mike Hofman, editor-in-chief of Inc., praised the tenacity and vision of this year's honorees, underscoring their contribution to propelling the U.S. economy forward. The achievements of these companies, including Powerhouse Consulting Group, will be celebrated at the 2025 Inc. 5000 Conference & Gala in Phoenix, with the top 500 companies also featured in the Fall issue of Inc. magazine.</p><p>Specializing in software consultation for the home service sector, Powerhouse Consulting Group offers tech audits and strategic advice to help contractors maximize their software return on investment. Their expertise in field service management solutions has established them as a trusted partner for contractors aiming to streamline operations and foster growth. Additional details about their services can be found at <a href='https://mypowerhouse.group' rel='nofollow' target='_blank'>https://mypowerhouse.group</a>.</p>
