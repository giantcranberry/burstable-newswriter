<p>Agile Consulting Group has achieved a notable milestone by securing the No. 2020 spot on the 2025 Inc. 5000 list, a prestigious ranking that identifies America's fastest-growing private companies. This accolade not only highlights the company's impressive growth trajectory but also its ability to withstand economic uncertainties, inflationary pressures, and a volatile labor market. Specializing in sales and use tax consulting since its inception in 2005, Agile Consulting Group has carved a niche for itself by assisting clients in navigating the complexities of tax laws, ensuring compliance, and optimizing exemptions.</p><p>Aaron Giles, the Founder and Managing Principal of Agile, shared his pride in this achievement, emphasizing that being ranked among the top companies out of 33.2 million small businesses in the U.S. places Agile in the 99.99th percentile. This recognition serves as a testament to the company's sustained growth and the efficacy of its services in a specialized yet crucial domain of business operations. The Inc. 5000 list not only acknowledges the accomplishments of these companies but also underscores their contribution to job creation and economic expansion. With a median three-year revenue growth rate of 1,552 percent among the top 500 companies, the list reflects the dynamic nature of these businesses, which have collectively added over 48,678 jobs to the U.S. economy in the last three years. For more insights into the list and its featured companies, visit <a href='https://www.inc.com/inc5000' rel='nofollow' target='_blank'>https://www.inc.com/inc5000</a>.</p><p>Mike Hofman, editor-in-chief of Inc., praised the honorees for their resilience and foresight, particularly in the face of rising costs and evolving global dynamics. The 2025 Inc. 5000 Conference & Gala in Phoenix will honor these companies, with the top 500 also being spotlighted in the Fall issue of Inc. magazine. Agile Consulting Group's inclusion in the Inc. 5000 list marks a significant achievement, underscoring its leadership in the sales and use tax consulting industry and its capacity to adapt and flourish in an ever-changing business landscape.
