<p>ESGold Corp has marked a significant advancement in its Montauban Project located in Quebec by welcoming Peter Espig to its board of directors and making notable progress in site construction. Espig, who brings a wealth of experience in project finance and the transition of junior mining companies into producers, has been granted 500,000 restricted share units (RSUs) as part of his appointment. This move underscores the company's strategic direction towards achieving production goals efficiently.</p><p>The construction at the Montauban processing site is reported to be on schedule, with the imminent delivery of Humphrey Spirals being a critical component for the site's gravity recovery circuit. These developments are pivotal for ESGold Corp as it adheres to its timeline, targeting the commencement of commercial production by the end of 2025. The Montauban property, strategically located 80 kilometers west of Quebec City, is not only a cornerstone for ESGold's production ambitions but also exemplifies the company's commitment to responsible mining practices.</p><p>Peter Espig's appointment is a testament to ESGold's focus on leveraging expert knowledge in project finance and corporate development to navigate the path to production. His involvement is anticipated to bring valuable insights that will aid the company in its transition to becoming an active producer of gold and silver. The progress in construction and the strategic enhancement of the board reflect ESGold's dedication to sustainable resource recovery and exploration within Quebec's mining sector, setting a precedent for responsible and efficient mining operations.</p>
