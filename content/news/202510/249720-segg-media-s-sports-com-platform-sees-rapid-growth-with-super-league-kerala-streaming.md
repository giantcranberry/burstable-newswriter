<p>SEGG Media Corporation has reported significant viewership growth for its Sports.com platform following the launch of live streaming coverage for Super League Kerala Season 2. The platform has captured an estimated 500,000 views and is projected to exceed one million views with the second weekend of football matches, indicating rapid audience expansion across India and international markets.</p><p>The upcoming match between Thiruvananthapuram Kombans FC and Forca Kochi FC is scheduled for 7:30 p.m. IST (10:00 a.m. ET) at Chandrasekharan Nair Stadium in Trivandrum. The game will stream live on Sports.com and air on Sony Ten 2, Ten 2 HD, and DD Malayalam. Director Marc Bircham stated that the viewership surge demonstrates Sports.com's capacity to deliver premium, high-volume sports content to global audiences, which is driving brand visibility and commercial growth across emerging markets.</p><p>SEGG Media operates as a global sports, entertainment, and gaming conglomerate with a portfolio of digital assets including Sports.com, Concerts.com, and Lottery.com. The company focuses on immersive fan engagement, ethical gaming, and AI-driven live experiences, positioning itself to redefine how global audiences interact with sports and entertainment content.
