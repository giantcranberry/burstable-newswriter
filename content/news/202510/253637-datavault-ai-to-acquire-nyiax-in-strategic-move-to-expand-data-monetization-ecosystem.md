<p>Datavault AI Inc. has signed a letter of intent to acquire NYIAX Inc., a technology and services company with operations spanning the United States, Europe, and Dubai. The proposed acquisition, which requires Nasdaq approval and execution of a definitive agreement, would integrate NYIAX's blockchain-powered exchange, patent portfolio, marketing-as-a-service agency, and advertising unit into Datavault AI's expanding ecosystem. This strategic combination represents a significant advancement in data valuation and monetization technologies that could reshape how businesses across multiple sectors manage and profit from their digital assets.</p><p>The transaction builds upon an existing licensing partnership between the two companies for Datavault AI's ADIO ultrasonic technology. This integration will enhance Datavault AI's patented Information Data Exchange platform by adding transparent, automated trading capabilities. The combined technologies create a powerful infrastructure for secure data exchange and monetization, addressing growing market demand for privacy-first solutions in an increasingly data-driven economy. The acquisition positions Datavault AI to capitalize on emerging opportunities in Web 3.0 technologies and high-performance computing solutions.</p><p>Datavault AI plans to launch several specialized exchanges over the next two quarters, including the International Elements Exchange, International NIL Exchange, and American Political Exchange. These platforms will leverage the combined technologies to create new marketplaces for data and digital assets. CEO Nathaniel Bradley stated that the acquisition positions Datavault AI to redefine data monetization across industries, while NYIAX CEO Teri Gallo described the merger as uniting financial-market precision with next-generation, privacy-first technologies.</p><p>The company's cloud-based platform serves multiple industries including sports and entertainment, biotech, education, fintech, real estate, healthcare, and energy. The Information Data Exchange platform enables Digital Twins and licensing of name, image, and likeness by securely attaching physical real-world objects to immutable metadata objects, fostering responsible AI with integrity. This technology integration addresses critical needs for data security and transparency across global markets.
