<p>Datavault AI has entered into a definitive licensing agreement with Nature's Miracle Holding Inc. that grants the sustainable agriculture company global rights to develop, distribute, and commercialize blockchain-powered carbon credit solutions. The $2 million licensing deal includes a 35% royalty on all gross revenue generated from the technology, representing a significant expansion of Datavault AI's patent portfolio into the growing sustainability sector.</p><p>The agreement centers on Datavault AI's patented Carbon Credit Tokenization System, which utilizes blockchain technology to create scalable, transparent, and fraud-resistant carbon credit verification and trading platforms. This technology addresses critical challenges in carbon markets where verification and transparency have historically been problematic. The system's blockchain integration enhances sustainability reporting capabilities while providing immutable records of carbon credit transactions.</p><p>Nathaniel Bradley, CEO of Datavault AI, described the partnership as a significant milestone in the company's strategy to commercialize its patent portfolio across high-growth industries. The licensing model allows Nature's Miracle to leverage Datavault AI's established technology while Datavault AI receives both upfront compensation and ongoing revenue through the royalty structure. This approach enables rapid deployment of carbon credit solutions without the development costs typically associated with blockchain implementation.</p><p>The technology leverages Datavault AI's broader platform capabilities, including its Information Data Exchange that enables secure attachment of physical real-world objects to immutable metadata objects. This foundation supports the carbon credit tokenization by providing verifiable digital representations of environmental assets.
