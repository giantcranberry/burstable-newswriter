<p>Datavault AI Inc. has unveiled a comprehensive global expansion strategy that includes relocating its corporate headquarters to downtown Philadelphia while establishing new research centers in Georgia and London. The Philadelphia headquarters relocation strengthens the company's East Coast presence while maintaining its Beaverton, Oregon site to support WiSA Engineering and Pacific Rim operations, creating a balanced coast-to-coast operational framework.</p><p>The company is establishing a new Center for AI and Quantum Computing Excellence in Sandy Springs, Georgia, representing a significant investment in advanced research infrastructure. This facility is designed specifically for cutting-edge work in AI, quantum computing, and digital twins and will operate in collaboration with Georgia Tech and the University of Georgia, leveraging academic partnerships to drive innovation in emerging technologies.</p><p>Concurrently, Datavault AI is launching a Quantum Computing & Digital Twin Embassy in London's Knightsbridge district, marking the company's strategic push into European markets. This international expansion demonstrates the company's commitment to global market penetration and technological leadership in quantum computing and digital twin technologies.</p><p>The expansion strategy includes strengthening the company's strategic direction through advisory board enhancements. Datavault AI has added Dr. John Ratzan, Accenture plc Financial Services Data and AI North America Lead, and Mr. Mustaq Patel to its advisory board, with additional appointments anticipated by the end of October.</p><p>These developments align with Datavault AI's core business focus on AI experience, valuation, and monetization of assets in the Web 3.0 environment. The company's cloud-based platform provides comprehensive solutions through its Acoustic Science and Data Science Divisions. The Acoustic Science Division features WiSA, ADIO and Sumerian patented technologies, including industry-first foundational spatial and multichannel wireless HD sound transmission technologies. More information about the company's technology offerings can be found at <a href="https://www.datavaultsite.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.datavaultsite.com</a>.</p><p>The Data Science Division leverages Web 3.0 and high-performance computing to provide solutions for experiential data perception, valuation and secure monetization. The company's Information Data Exchange enables Digital Twins and licensing of name, image, and likeness by securely attaching physical real-world objects to immutable metadata objects, fostering responsible AI with integrity. Investors can access the latest company updates through the newsroom at <a href="https://ibn.fm/DVLT" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://ibn.fm/DVLT</a>.</p><p>This coordinated expansion across multiple continents and technological domains positions Datavault AI to capitalize on growing demand for AI-driven data solutions and Web 3.0 technologies. The strategic placement of facilities in key innovation hubs demonstrates the company's methodical approach to global growth and technological advancement in the rapidly evolving digital asset landscape, serving multiple industries including sports & entertainment, events & venues, biotech, education, fintech, real estate, healthcare, and energy.</p>
