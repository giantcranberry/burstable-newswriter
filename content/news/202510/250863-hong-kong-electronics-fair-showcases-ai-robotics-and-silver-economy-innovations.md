<p>The 45th Hong Kong Electronics Fair (Autumn Edition) and 28th electronicAsia opened today at the Hong Kong Convention and Exhibition Centre, featuring more than 3,200 exhibitors from 20 countries and regions. The four-day event showcases significant technological advancements in artificial intelligence, robotics, and digital entertainment while addressing growing market segments including the silver economy. Prof Frederick Ma, Chairman of the Hong Kong Trade Development Council (HKTDC), emphasized the organization's commitment to supporting enterprises in global expansion and transformation during his visit to the fair.</p><p>This year's exhibition highlights three major focus areas: AI and robotics, digital entertainment, and products serving the aging population. The RoboPark event space brings together prominent Chinese technology companies from both Hangzhou's Six Little Dragons and Shenzhen's Eight Great Guardians of Embodied Intelligence. Companies including DEEP Robotics, BrainCo, DIGIT, and PaXini Tech are demonstrating advanced robotic systems with applications across service, logistics, healthcare, and industrial sectors. Over 30 live demonstrations will take place in RoboPark throughout the exhibition period, featuring robots from various regions showcasing business, rehabilitation, and living applications.</p><p>The humanoid robot TORA One from PaXini Technology features advanced tactile sensing and AI vision capabilities suitable for service, logistics, and healthcare applications. DEEP Robotics presents the X30 Quadruped Robodog equipped with waterproof protection, intelligent navigation, and thermal imaging cameras designed for challenging environments. Booster Robotics showcases the Booster T1 humanoid robot, which won RoboCup 2025 and performs complex movements including soccer kicks and kung fu maneuvers. Hong Kong Robotics Limited demonstrates Cloud Ginger, a cloud-based intelligent humanoid wheeled robot with advanced AI for facial recognition and autonomous navigation.</p><p>Digital entertainment innovations include the Head-Mounted Display from GooVision Technology Company Limited, providing high-definition visuals suitable for surgical assistance and pilot training applications. The RD-839 Smart Projector from Guangzhou Rigal Electronics Company Limited supports 4K resolution for home theater systems while maintaining portability for outdoor use. Gaming accessories such as the Cypher 81 Keyboard from Epomaker Inc and Gaming Power Strip from Ruyuan Lighting Electric Appliance Company Limited offer enhanced user experiences with customizable features and efficient space utilization.</p><p>Products targeting the silver economy include BloomCap from AGreen Marketing Limited, featuring patented technology to stimulate hair follicles and promote regrowth. The At-home Pill Dispenser from Kin Technology Limited automates medication dispensing for up to 12 different types of drugs while utilizing IoT technology for remote management. Safety-focused innovations include the Smart Walking Stick from Megastek Technologies Ltd, integrating GPS tracking, emergency alerts, and obstacle detection systems. Vcom International Limited presents an All-in-One Health Monitor that tracks multiple vital signs including heart rate, blood pressure, and oxygen levels.</p><p>Concurrent forums and seminars address key industry developments, including the 10th Symposium on Innovation & Technology co-organized by the HKTDC and the Hong Kong Electronics & Technologies Association. The symposium explored robotics technology applications across land, sea, and sky environments, examining how interdisciplinary collaboration drives innovation in areas including smart logistics and unmanned vessels. Additional events include the Hong Kong Electronic Forum focusing on advanced battery technology and energy storage innovations, along with the Future Stage: AI x Performing Arts Awards showcasing young innovators combining AI with performing arts. The exhibitions operate under the EXHIBITION+ hybrid model, allowing participants to connect through the <a href="https://www.hktdc.com/event/hktdc-click2match/en" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">HKTDC Click2Match platform</a> alongside physical attendance. More information is available through the <a href="https://www.hktdc.com/event/electronicasia/en" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">electronicAsia website</a> and <a href="https://mediaroom.hktdc.com/en" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">HKTDC Media Room</a>.</p>
