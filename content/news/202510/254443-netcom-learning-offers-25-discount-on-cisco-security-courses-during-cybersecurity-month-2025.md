<p>NetCom Learning has announced an exclusive 25% discount on Cisco Security Courses throughout Cybersecurity Month 2025, running through October 31, 2025. This limited-time initiative aims to help organizations and IT professionals strengthen their security posture and stay ahead of today's fast-evolving digital threats. The discount applies to a range of high-demand Cisco security programs including Implementing and Operating Cisco Security Core Technologies (SCOR), Performing Cyber Ops Using Cisco Security Technologies (CBRCOR), and Implementing and Configuring Cisco Identity Services Engine (SISE).</p><p>Additional courses covered by the discount include Firewall Threat Defense and Intrusion Prevention (SFWIPF), Securing Email with Cisco Email Security Appliance (SESA), Cybersecurity Operations Fundamentals (CBROPS), and Automation for Cisco Security Solutions (SAUI). Each course is led by certified instructors and incorporates hands-on labs that align with industry certifications and enterprise requirements, providing practical skills that can be immediately applied in workplace environments.</p><p>Complementing the course discounts, NetCom Learning is hosting a live virtual podcast titled Cybersecurity in the Age of AI: Real Threats, Real Solutions on October 28, 2025. This event will feature cybersecurity experts exploring how artificial intelligence is reshaping cyber defense strategies, sharing practical insights and actionable strategies for modern enterprises facing increasingly sophisticated threats.</p><p>The timing of these initiatives coincides with growing concerns about cybersecurity vulnerabilities across industries. As organizations continue digital transformation efforts and remote work becomes more prevalent, the need for comprehensive security training has never been more critical. NetCom Learning, with more than 26 years of experience in managed learning solutions, positions these offerings as essential for building innovative learning organizations that can adapt to rapidly changing technological landscapes.</p><p>The company's approach focuses on closing critical skill gaps through authorized training from leading technology vendors including Microsoft, AWS, Cisco, Google Cloud, AI Certs, EC-Council, and CompTIA. By making professional cybersecurity education more accessible during Cybersecurity Month, NetCom Learning aims to support organizational changes and drive growth through workforce development in security technologies that protect against contemporary digital threats.</p>
