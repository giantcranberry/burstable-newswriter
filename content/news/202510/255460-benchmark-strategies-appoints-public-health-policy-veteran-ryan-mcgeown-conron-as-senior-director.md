<p>Benchmark Strategies has appointed Ryan McGeown-Conron as Senior Director of Public Affairs, bringing nearly a decade of public policy experience to Boston's leading public affairs firm. McGeown-Conron joins from the Massachusetts Department of Public Health, where he served as Director of Legislative Affairs and played a central role in the state's COVID-19 pandemic response, vaccine rollout, and vaccine equity initiatives.</p><p>In his new position, McGeown-Conron will lead public sector consulting engagements across multiple industries including Information Technology, Artificial Intelligence, Cybersecurity, MedTech, Life Sciences, and Law Enforcement Technology. His background includes spearheading legislative and government affairs efforts during critical public health emergencies, including leading legislative outreach for DPH Steward Healthcare Incident Command and managing the complex policy landscape during the pandemic response.</p><p>Patrick Bench, President of Benchmark Strategies, emphasized the strategic value of McGeown-Conron's appointment. "Ryan's deep legislative and executive agency experience in public health and healthcare policy further strengthens our team's capabilities and reach.
