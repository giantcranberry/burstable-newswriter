<p>Global Tax Network (GTN), a leading mobility tax services firm, has been named an OnCon Icon Award Top 50 Operations Team for 2025. The OnCon Icon Awards recognize the most impactful operations teams worldwide, celebrating those who demonstrate exceptional leadership, innovation, and community engagement. Winners are selected through peer and community voting, recognizing teams that have made a meaningful impact within their organizations and across their industries.</p><p>This year's awards were presented during the virtual OnCon 2025 awards celebration on October 21. The recognition underscores GTN's commitment to operational excellence in the specialized field of mobility tax services. Craig Dexheimer, COO/Data Privacy & Security Officer at GTN, expressed pride in the achievement, stating that the recognition reflects the dedication and collaboration of the entire operations team.</p><p>"This recognition reflects the dedication and collaboration of our entire operations team, whose focus on innovation and process improvement drives the exceptional experiences we deliver to our team and clients every day," Dexheimer said. The award highlights how operational efficiency and innovation contribute directly to client satisfaction in the complex field of cross-border taxation.</p><p>OnCon is a global organization connecting top professionals across industries through conferences, awards, and peer communities. Its mission is to advance collaboration, innovation, and leadership development among business executives and organizations worldwide. The Top 50 Operations Team recognition places GTN among elite companies demonstrating operational excellence across various industries. For those interested in learning more about the 2025 OnCon Icon award winners, additional information is available at <a href="https://www.onconferences.com/25-team-winners-ops/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.onconferences.com/25-team-winners-ops/</a>.</p><p>The recognition comes as GTN continues to expand its services supporting expatriates, foreign nationals, business travelers, remote workers, and permanent transfers navigating cross-border tax complexities. The award validates GTN's operational approach in a rapidly evolving global workforce landscape where mobility and remote work arrangements are becoming increasingly common. As companies adapt to new work models, the operational infrastructure supporting mobile employees becomes increasingly critical for compliance and risk management. This recognition positions GTN as a leader in developing operational frameworks that support the growing demand for flexible work arrangements across international borders.</p><p>For business leaders navigating the complexities of global workforce management, GTN's recognition signals the importance of robust operational infrastructure in supporting mobile employees. The award demonstrates how specialized service providers are adapting to meet the challenges of an increasingly borderless workforce, where tax compliance and regulatory requirements vary significantly across jurisdictions. This operational excellence becomes particularly crucial as companies expand their global footprint and implement hybrid work models that transcend traditional geographic boundaries.</p>
