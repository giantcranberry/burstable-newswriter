<p>Critical Infrastructure Technologies Ltd. commenced trading on the OTCQB Venture Market in the United States under the symbol CITLF, representing a significant expansion of the company's access to U.S. capital markets. The October 31, 2025 listing provides enhanced visibility, liquidity, and accessibility for U.S.-based investors seeking exposure to the company's autonomous communications and security technology platforms. This development marks a strategic milestone for the company as it positions itself for growth in North American markets.</p><p>The OTCQB Venture Market, operated by OTC Markets Group Inc., serves as a leading public market for entrepreneurial and growth-oriented companies. Qualification for trading on this platform demonstrates CiTech's commitment to transparency, financial integrity, and strong corporate governance standards. Investors can access real-time market information through <a href="https://www.otcmarkets.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.otcmarkets.com</a>. The listing enables U.S. investors to trade and hold CiTech shares in U.S. dollars through standard brokerage accounts, improving market access and liquidity for American market participants.</p><p>Concurrent with the OTCQB listing, CiTech has engaged Native Ads, Inc., a digital marketing firm specializing in financial marketing and brand development for growth-stage companies. The partnership aims to expand brand awareness and market reach across North America through a comprehensive digital media and distribution campaign. Native Ads will implement data-driven, compliant investor marketing strategies to enhance understanding of CiTech's technology and growth trajectory.</p><p>Brenton Scott, CEO of CiTech, emphasized the strategic importance of these initiatives, stating that trading on the OTCQB represents a significant milestone in expanding the company's global investor base. The partnership with Native Ads will enable more effective communication of CiTech's value proposition to new U.S. investors as the company grows its footprint across defense and communications markets. Jon Malach from Native Ads highlighted the mission to introduce CiTech to a broader U.S. audience through compliant investor marketing that raises awareness of the company's innovation and global potential.</p><p>CiTech's technology portfolio includes the Nexus 16 platform, which provides critical mobile telecommunications for mining, defense, border security, surveillance, and emergency services sectors. The company's self-deploying platform addresses limitations of current rapidly deployable communication solutions through patented technologies that enhance tower strength and operational deployment capabilities. Additional information about the company's technology and corporate developments is available at <a href="https://www.citech.com.au" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.citech.com.au</a>.</p><p>The dual initiatives align with CiTech's broader objective to strengthen engagement within defense, communications, and technology markets while broadening its U.S. investor base. The expansion into U.S. markets through the OTCQB listing and targeted marketing partnership represents a strategic move to capitalize on growing demand for autonomous communications and security solutions in North American defense and infrastructure sectors. This market development comes as CiTech completes its research and development phase and moves toward commercializing its first products in the rapidly evolving critical infrastructure technology space.</p>
