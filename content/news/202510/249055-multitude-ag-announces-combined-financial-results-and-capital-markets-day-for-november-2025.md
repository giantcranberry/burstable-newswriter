<p>Multitude AG, a listed European FinTech company, will host a combined 9M Financial Results Earnings Call and Capital Markets Day 2025 webcast on November 13, 2025. The virtual event brings together financial analysts, investors, and media to review the company's performance and strategic direction in digital lending and online banking services. This dual presentation approach provides stakeholders with comprehensive insight into both current financial performance and future strategic ambitions.</p><p>The event schedule begins with the 9M 2025 Financial Results Earnings Call at 13:00 CET, followed by the Capital Markets Day presentation at 13:45 CET. During these sessions, the Group's Leadership Team will present current nine-month financial figures while providing updates on Multitude's strategic ambitions, growth opportunities, and financial targets. Registration for the webcast is available through the company's financial calendar section at <a href="https://www.multitude.com/investors/financial-calendar" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.multitude.com/investors/financial-calendar</a>. The virtual format ensures broad accessibility for international participants interested in the company's progress across European markets.</p><p>Multitude operates as a significant player in the European FinTech landscape, serving consumers, small and medium-sized enterprises, and other FinTech companies that traditional banks often overlook. The company delivers services through three distinct business units: Consumer Banking under the Ferratum brand, SME Banking through CapitalBox, and Wholesale Banking via Multitude Bank. These operations are supported by the company's internal Growth Platform, which enables scalable service delivery across multiple markets.</p><p>The company maintains substantial operational scale, employing over 700 people across 25 countries while offering services in 17 markets. This geographic diversity positions Multitude to capitalize on various European financial markets and regulatory environments. The company reported a combined turnover of 274 million euros in 2024, demonstrating its established presence in the competitive FinTech sector. Founded in Finland in 2005 and now registered in Switzerland, Multitude trades on the Prime Standard segment of the Frankfurt Stock Exchange under the symbol 'MULT'.</p><p>For business and technology leaders, Multitude's upcoming event represents a critical indicator of European FinTech health and direction. The company's performance and strategic updates will provide insights into how digital financial services are evolving in response to market conditions, regulatory changes, and technological advancements. The combined presentation of current results and future strategy allows stakeholders to assess both immediate performance and long-term viability in a sector experiencing rapid transformation.</p><p>The timing of this event during November 2025 positions it as a key milestone for investors and industry observers evaluating year-end performance and 2026 strategic planning. Multitude's focus on underserved segments—consumers, SMEs, and other FinTech companies—highlights ongoing opportunities in markets where traditional banking services remain inadequate. The company's multi-country operational model also provides valuable case studies on navigating diverse European regulatory environments while maintaining service consistency and growth momentum.</p>
