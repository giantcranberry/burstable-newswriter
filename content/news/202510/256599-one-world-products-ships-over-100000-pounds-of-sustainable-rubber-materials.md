<p>One World Products, Inc. has achieved a significant operational milestone with its subsidiary Eco Bio Plastics Manufacturing in Michigan, shipping more than 100,000 pounds of pelletized compounded rubber. This accomplishment represents rapid expansion of the company's sustainable materials product line and validates the scalability of its manufacturing methodology.</p><p>Under Chairman and CEO Isiah Thomas's leadership, Eco Bio Plastics successfully implemented a new rubber compounding line using existing equipment at its Michigan facility. This strategic approach demonstrates the flexibility of the company's sustainable manufacturing model, which focuses on repurposing infrastructure to meet increasing demand for environmentally friendly materials across multiple industries. Thomas emphasized that this milestone provides concrete evidence that the company's strategy is effective.</p><p>The company's innovation within existing manufacturing footprints has enabled expanded capabilities, improved production efficiency, and positioned the organization to serve additional partners in automotive, packaging, and industrial applications. This achievement illustrates how sustainable manufacturing principles and sound business practices can effectively complement each other, creating both environmental and economic value.</p><p>Located within America's manufacturing corridor, Eco Bio Plastics Michigan utilizes its strategic positioning and proximity to key industrial partners to accelerate production, strengthen logistics, and support the company's growing role in advanced materials manufacturing. The company manufactures and supplies advanced bio-based materials for partners including Stellantis, Flex-N-Gate, Orbis Corporation, West Michigan Compounding, and Full Circle Technologies.</p><p>One World Products continues scaling production of pelletized, micronized, and compounded materials designed for next-generation manufacturing applications. As the first minority-controlled, athlete-led publicly traded company in sustainable manufacturing, the organization is redefining responsible business practices by generating value through innovation, job creation, and measurable environmental impact. For additional information about the company's operations and sustainable materials, visit <a href="https://oneworldproducts.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://oneworldproducts.com</a>.</p>
