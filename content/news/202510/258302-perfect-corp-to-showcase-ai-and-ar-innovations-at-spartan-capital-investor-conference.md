<p>Perfect Corp. will present at the Spartan Capital Securities Second Annual Investor Conference on November 3, 2025, at the Marriott Marquis Hotel in New York City. The company's selection for this prestigious event underscores the increasing mainstream acceptance of artificial intelligence and augmented reality technologies in consumer markets, particularly as these innovations demonstrate growing commercial viability and attract institutional investment.</p><p>The conference, organized in partnership with B2i Digital, will showcase more than 60 carefully selected companies and is expected to attract over 500 institutional and high-net-worth investors. John D. Lowry, Founder and Chief Executive Officer of Spartan Capital Securities, LLC, expressed enthusiasm about Perfect Corp's inclusion, stating that the conference represents a powerful extension of Spartan Capital's mission to help growth companies access the right investors and resources.</p><p>Perfect Corp's presence signals growing investor interest in AI- and AR-powered solutions that are transforming consumer experiences across multiple industries. The company offers both direct consumer applications and enterprise services for major beauty, skincare, fashion, jewelry, and watch brands. David Shapiro, CEO of B2i Digital, emphasized the strategic nature of Perfect Corp's invitation, noting that their participation reflects a broader commitment to connecting investors with diverse growth companies.</p><p>The event format includes main-stage presentations, expert panels, curated one-on-one meetings, and extensive networking sessions designed to create meaningful dialogue between growth companies and the investment community. This structure provides companies like Perfect Corp with multiple platforms to demonstrate their technological innovations and growth potential to serious capital providers. For more information about the conference, visit <a href="https://spartancapital.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://spartancapital.com</a>.</p><p>The conference represents a significant opportunity for emerging technology companies to access the institutional investment needed to scale operations and expand market reach. As AI and AR technologies continue to evolve and demonstrate commercial viability, they attract greater attention from investors seeking exposure to innovative growth sectors. This event serves as a critical bridge between technological innovation and investment capital, facilitating the development of next-generation consumer experiences that are reshaping how consumers interact with beauty, fashion, and retail brands worldwide.</p>
