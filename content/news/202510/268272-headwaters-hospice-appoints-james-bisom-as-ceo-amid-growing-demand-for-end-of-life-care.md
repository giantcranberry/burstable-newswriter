<p>Headwaters Hospice and Palliative Care has appointed James Bisom, MHA, as its new Chief Executive Officer, bringing over 14 years of healthcare leadership experience to the veteran-owned healthcare provider. Bisom assumes leadership with a clear mission to advance quality of life, dignity, and comfort for patients and families throughout Montana.</p><p>The appointment signals Headwaters Hospice and Palliative Care's continued commitment to strengthening its leadership team during a period of growing demand for specialized end-of-life care services. Bisom brings extensive management and senior leadership experience from multiple healthcare organizations across Montana, positioning him to address increasing challenges in delivering specialized care services to rural and underserved communities.</p><p>Throughout his career, Bisom has championed strategic initiatives that improved patient safety, expanded access to specialized services, and enhanced overall care delivery. His leadership approach has consistently driven organizational growth and operational excellence while maintaining focus on patient satisfaction and community collaboration. As a Montana native with both a Bachelor's degree in Business Administration and Master's degree in Health Administration, Bisom brings local understanding combined with professional expertise to his new role.</p><p>"I'm honored to join a team so deeply committed to compassion and integrity," said Bisom. "At Headwaters, we believe great care begins with great people. Together, we'll continue to build a culture that reflects our values of respect, accountability, and excellence in every interaction."</p><p>Bisom expressed particular pride in joining a veteran-owned company whose mission to provide meaningful, heart-centered care aligns with his professional philosophy. The leadership transition comes as healthcare organizations across Montana face increasing challenges in delivering specialized care services, making Bisom's extensive background in healthcare management particularly valuable for maintaining Headwaters' commitment to patient-centered care. For more information about Headwaters Hospice and its palliative care services, visit <a href="https://www.headwatershospice.org" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.headwatershospice.org</a>.</p>
