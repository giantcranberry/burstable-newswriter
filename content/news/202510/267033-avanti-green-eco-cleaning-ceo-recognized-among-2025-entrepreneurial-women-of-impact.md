<p>Claudia Meneses, Co-Founder and CEO of Avanti Green Eco Cleaning, has been named among the 2025 Entrepreneurial Women of Impact by the Women Presidents Organization in partnership with Women Elevating Women. The awards, sponsored by J.P. Morgan Commercial Banking, recognize 24 outstanding women entrepreneurs who combine business success with meaningful community impact. This recognition highlights the growing importance of sustainable business practices and female leadership in traditionally male-dominated industries.</p><p>Meneses founded Avanti Green Eco Cleaning in 2011 and has led the company's growth in the competitive Las Vegas and Henderson markets by promoting residential and commercial eco-friendly cleaning solutions. The company offers customized service offerings including carpet cleaning, window cleaning, pressure washing, and floor care, with a strong emphasis on team training, quality assurance, and sustainability. Under her leadership, the company has distinguished itself for its personalized customer experience, green branding and innovative digital marketing efforts.</p><p>The technological advancement of Avanti Green represents a significant shift in the home services industry. Earlier this year, the company won first prize and $35,000 USD at the Amazon Web Services AI Pitch Competition, enabling the development of its AI-powered scheduling and route optimization system. This system is designed to provide real-time availability, create upselling opportunities and increase operational efficiency. The innovation caught the attention of AWS, which continues to support its product development through their platform at https://aws.amazon.com.</p><p>Additionally, Avanti Green's idea was selected among the top 10 startups in Nevada's Startup World Cup, showcasing its growing leadership in sustainability-driven innovation and technology for the home services industry. The company was also the 2024 Small Business Development Center's Business of the Year. These achievements demonstrate how traditional service businesses can leverage technology to gain competitive advantages while maintaining environmental responsibility.</p><p>The Women Presidents Organization is a global non-profit peer advisory community for women business leaders of privately-held, multi-million-dollar companies. WPO's mission is to accelerate business growth, enhance competitiveness and promote economic security for women entrepreneurs by providing access to peer networks, executive education and confidential round-table chapters through their website at https://women-presidents.com. This recognition signals broader industry trends toward sustainability and technological integration in service-based businesses.</p><p>For business leaders and investors, Avanti Green's success story illustrates several key market developments. The integration of AI into traditional service industries represents a significant opportunity for operational optimization and customer experience enhancement. The company's dual focus on environmental sustainability and technological innovation positions it well for future growth in an increasingly eco-conscious market. As consumers and businesses prioritize green solutions, companies like Avanti Green that combine sustainability with smart technology are likely to see increased market share and investor interest.</p>
