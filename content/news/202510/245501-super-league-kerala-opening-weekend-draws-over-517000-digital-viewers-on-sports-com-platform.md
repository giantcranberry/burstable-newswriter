<p>SEGG Media Corporation reported impressive audience engagement metrics for the opening weekend of Super League Kerala streamed live on its Sports.com platform. The sports, entertainment, and gaming conglomerate revealed that three competitive matches attracted more than 517,000 digital viewers with an average watch time of 60 minutes per viewer. The successful streaming event featured Calicut FC's 2-1 victory over Forca Kochi FC among other matches that drew strong stadium attendance alongside the substantial digital audience.</p><p>The Super League Kerala launch represents a significant achievement in Sports.com's strategic expansion across India and MENA regions. Backed by sponsors Sports.com and Amul, with Sony handling linear broadcasts, the event demonstrated the platform's growing capability to drive fan engagement while generating sponsorship revenue. The combination of strong digital viewership and physical attendance underscores the dual approach SEGG Media is taking to capture both online and offline sports audiences.</p><p>The opening weekend performance reinforces SEGG Media's broader strategy to expand its global football streaming presence while strengthening fan engagement through digital platforms. The company operates a portfolio of digital assets including Sports.com, Concerts.com and Lottery.com, focusing on immersive fan experiences and AI-driven live content delivery.
