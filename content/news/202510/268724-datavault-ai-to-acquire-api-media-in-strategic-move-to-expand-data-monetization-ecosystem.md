<p>Datavault AI Inc. has entered a definitive agreement to acquire API Media in December 2025, marking a significant expansion of the company's AI-powered data activation and monetization capabilities. The acquisition will preserve API Media's brand while strengthening its operations with Datavault's patented technologies, creating a powerful combination in the data monetization landscape.</p><p>The strategic move represents Datavault's effort to scale its proprietary data-monetization ecosystem by integrating API Media's established brand and industry expertise. API Media, based in New Jersey, provides advanced audio, visual, and IT services for premier sports venues and enterprise clients, positioning the combined entity to capitalize on growing data monetization opportunities in live entertainment environments.</p><p>Nathaniel Bradley, CEO of Datavault AI, described the acquisition as a decisive next step in the company's growth strategy. He emphasized that API Media's brand and expertise will enrich Datavault's culture and enhance the value delivered to enterprise clients through verified, tokenized data assets. This integration addresses the increasing demand for secure data monetization solutions across multiple industries.</p><p>API Media executives Frank Tomaino and David Reese highlighted how the acquisition will expand client access to AI, analytics, and secure data monetization tools while accelerating Datavault's growth and recurring revenue across data, advertising, and AI-as-a-Service markets. The combination creates significant synergies between Datavault's cloud-based platform and API Media's venue technology services.</p><p>Datavault AI's technology platform includes comprehensive solutions serving multiple industries through its Acoustic Science and Data Science Divisions. The company's patented technologies include WiSA, ADIO and Sumerian foundational spatial and multichannel wireless HD sound transmission technologies. More information about Datavault AI's technology offerings is available at <a href="https://www.datavaultsite.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.datavaultsite.com</a>.</p><p>The Information Data Exchange enables Digital Twins and licensing of name, image, and likeness by securely attaching physical real-world objects to immutable metadata objects, fostering responsible AI with integrity. This technology integration with API Media's venue services could create new data monetization opportunities across the sports and entertainment industries where API Media currently operates. Additional company updates and news relating to DVLT are available in the company's newsroom at <a href="https://ibn.fm/DVLT" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://ibn.fm/DVLT</a>.</p><p>For business leaders and technology executives, this acquisition signals the growing convergence of physical venue technologies with advanced data monetization platforms. The combination positions the merged company to capture value from the massive data streams generated in sports and entertainment venues, potentially creating new revenue models while addressing data privacy and security concerns through tokenization technologies.</p>
