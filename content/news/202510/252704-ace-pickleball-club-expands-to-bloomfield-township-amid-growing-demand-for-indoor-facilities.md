<p>Ace Pickleball Club has opened its newest location in Bloomfield Township at 2285 Franklin Rd., with doors officially opening on October 25. The expansion represents the franchise's continued growth in Michigan following its successful Grand Rapids launch earlier this year. Developed by SOLU Enterprises, led by Adam M. Lutz and Matthew Sosin, the Bloomfield Township location continues the developers' pattern of transforming underutilized retail spaces into vibrant community destinations.</p><p>The opening addresses the rapidly increasing demand for pickleball facilities in the area, providing year-round, climate-controlled courts for players of all skill levels. According to Adam M. Lutz, co-owner of SOLU Enterprises, the Grand Rapids community quickly embraced the high-quality indoor pickleball experience, prompting the expansion to Bloomfield Township. The club aims to build a welcoming environment where beginners, families, and seasoned competitors can play, learn, and connect through various programming options.</p><p>APC's membership model offers hassle-free flexibility through a single monthly fee that allows unlimited, all-day play on professional-grade courts. The facility features designated courts for beginners, intermediate, and advanced players, ensuring optimal playing conditions for all ages and skill levels. Members gain access to unlimited open play, clinics, tournaments, social events, and court reservations as part of their membership benefits.</p><p>The club distinguishes itself through its community-focused approach, offering unlimited paddle demos that change quarterly, allowing members to test the latest technology from various brands. For newcomers to the sport, Ace Pickleball Club Bloomfield Township will provide free introductory clinics covering the rules and fundamentals to help players feel more comfortable learning the game. Additional information about membership and programming can be found at <a href="https://www.acepickleballclub.com/bloomfield-mi" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.acepickleballclub.com/bloomfield-mi</a>.</p><p>To celebrate the grand opening, the club is offering the first month free to Bloomfield Township and Birmingham community members. This expansion reflects the broader national growth of Ace Pickleball Club, which currently operates 18 locations across the country with plans to open 25-30 additional clubs in 2025. The franchise's continued expansion underscores pickleball's rising popularity as both a competitive sport and social activity that combines physical fitness with community engagement.</p>
