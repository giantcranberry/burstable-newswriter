<p>Glidelogic Corp's Policy Intelligence Suite achieved an 83.8% weighted forecast hit rate in predicting key outcomes of China's Fourth Plenary Session of the 20th Central Committee, marking a significant advancement in AI-driven policy analysis. The artificial intelligence platform successfully anticipated priorities related to China's upcoming 15th Five-Year Plan covering 2026-2030, with the system registering 10 full matches, 4 partial matches, and 2 early or weak matches against actual policy outcomes.</p><p>The validation demonstrates the predictive strength of Glidelogic's technology in forecasting major policy directions including "AI+" integration across industries, innovation-led economic growth strategies, and gradual implementation of delayed retirement policies. Founder Fred Xue emphasized that these results show how AI-based policy foresight transforms uncertainty into actionable intelligence for decision-makers navigating complex geopolitical landscapes. The company's achievement in accurately predicting outcomes from one of China's most important political gatherings highlights the growing capability of artificial intelligence in policy analysis and forecasting.</p><p>The Policy Intelligence Suite's performance suggests AI systems can now provide reliable insights into complex governmental decision-making processes previously considered unpredictable.
