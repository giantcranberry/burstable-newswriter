According to Abe Katz, Manager of FixMold LLC, contamination typically advances to non-visible surfaces by the time most people detect mold odors. The humid Florida climate creates ideal conditions for rapid mold growth, particularly in poorly ventilated spaces or properties with water leakage issues.</p><p>Homeowners should seek professional assistance when experiencing persistent musty or earthy odors in specific rooms, unexplained respiratory irritation, headaches, or allergy symptoms that worsen indoors.
