The 29.1% market share represents the highest percentage of EV sales California has recorded in any single quarter, building on the state's established position as America's premier electric vehicle market.</p><p>The record-breaking figures provide significant encouragement to American EV industry participants, including companies like Massimo Group (NASDAQ: MAMO), indicating robust market potential in the nation's largest electric vehicle market.
