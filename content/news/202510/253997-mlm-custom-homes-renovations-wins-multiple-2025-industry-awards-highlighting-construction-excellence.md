<p>MLM Custom Homes & Renovations has been voted the Winner for Best Construction Company in My New Orleans Magazine's 2025 Best of Home Awards, as determined by the magazine's readers through an online ballot. This recognition underscores the company's dedication to outstanding craftsmanship, innovative design, and unparalleled client satisfaction in custom home building and renovations throughout the Greater New Orleans area.</p><p>Founded over 17 years ago by General Contractor and Owner Machi Medrzycki, the company specializes in custom new home construction, historic renovations, kitchen and bathroom remodels, room additions, and commercial renovations. MLM's commitment to transparency and quality is supported by advanced tools like Buildertrend software, which provides clients with real-time project updates including daily photos, notes, budgets, and timelines. Serving south Louisiana and Mississippi, the experienced team ensures every project reflects excellence in build quality while honoring clients' distinctive tastes.</p><p>We are deeply grateful to the readers of My New Orleans Magazine for voting us as the Best Construction Company, said Machi Medrzycki, General Contractor and Owner of MLM Custom Homes & Renovations. This recognition is a testament to our team's passion for creating exceptional spaces that our clients cherish. Whether we're restoring a historic New Orleans home or building a modern custom home, we approach every project with precision and care.</p><p>The company has also earned the 2025 Houzz Best Design + Build Company award and the 2025 Best Customer Service Award by Pulse of The City New Orleans, further validating their industry leadership. Their work has been featured in publications such as Our Louisiana, Millenium and New Orleans Lifestyle magazines, showcasing high-end residential projects, historic restorations, and innovative commercial renovations. For more information about MLM Custom Homes & Renovations' residential services visit their website at <a href="https://mlm-inc.com/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://mlm-inc.com/</a>, and for commercial projects visit <a href="https://www.mlm-commercial.com/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.mlm-commercial.com/</a>.</p>
