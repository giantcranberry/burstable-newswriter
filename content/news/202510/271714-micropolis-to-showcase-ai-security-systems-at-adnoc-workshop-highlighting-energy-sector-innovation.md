<p>Micropolis Holding Co. will demonstrate its artificial intelligence-driven security systems and robotics solutions at the Abu Dhabi National Oil Company's Workshop on AI Security scheduled for November 10, 2025. The UAE-based technology company specializes in unmanned ground vehicles and AI-powered security systems designed specifically for industrial and energy sector applications.</p><p>The company's presentation will feature its latest autonomous technologies, including off-road unmanned systems, autonomous mobile robots, and edge-based AI surveillance platforms. These systems are engineered to improve safety measures, increase automation capabilities, and enhance situational awareness across oil and gas operations. Founder and CEO Fareed Aljawhari stated that the invitation to participate underscores Micropolis' expanding influence in advancing robotics and AI technologies for creating smarter, safer, and more sustainable energy-sector operations.</p><p>Micropolis maintains vertically integrated capabilities that span from mechatronics and embedded systems to AI software development and high-level autonomy implementation. The company's comprehensive approach allows it to develop complete solutions for urban, security, and industrial applications. Additional information about the company is available in their <a href="https://ibn.fm/MCRP" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">newsroom</a>.</p><p>The workshop presentation represents a significant opportunity for Micropolis to showcase its technological innovations to one of the world's leading energy companies. ADNOC's focus on AI security reflects the growing importance of advanced technological solutions in protecting critical energy infrastructure and operations. The integration of autonomous systems and AI-driven surveillance platforms addresses key challenges in the energy sector, including operational safety, efficiency improvements, and security enhancement.</p><p>This participation follows the company's ongoing commitment to developing specialized solutions for industrial applications. The full press release detailing Micropolis' involvement in the ADNOC workshop can be accessed at <a href="https://ibn.fm/mrk24" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://ibn.fm/mrk24</a>. The event will take place at the Abu Dhabi Energy Center, bringing together industry leaders and technology innovators to discuss the future of AI implementation in energy security.</p><p>For energy sector leaders and technology executives, this development signals a broader industry shift toward AI-powered security solutions that can operate in challenging industrial environments. The ability to deploy unmanned systems and edge-based surveillance platforms represents a fundamental change in how critical infrastructure protection is approached, potentially reducing human risk exposure while increasing monitoring capabilities across vast operational areas.</p><p>The timing of this workshop coincides with increasing global focus on energy security and infrastructure protection. As energy companies face evolving security threats and operational challenges, AI-driven solutions like those being demonstrated by Micropolis offer promising approaches to maintaining secure and efficient operations while adapting to the complex demands of modern energy production and distribution.</p>
