<p>Datavault AI has incorporated four Delaware subsidiaries to launch independent, regulated blockchain-based marketplaces across multiple sectors. The newly formed corporations—International Elements Exchange Inc., International NIL Exchange Inc., Information Data Exchange Inc., and American Political Exchange Inc.—will create specialized exchanges for real-world assets, sports and entertainment data, corporate information, and political donations respectively.</p><p>Each exchange will undergo independent regulatory review managed by Houlihan Lokey, with technology infrastructure provided by NYIAX and Nasdaq. This regulatory oversight aims to ensure compliance and build market confidence in these emerging digital asset platforms. The company expects to launch its first exchanges before the end of 2025, marking a significant expansion of its data monetization capabilities.</p><p>The initiative is supported by a strengthened patent portfolio exceeding 70 assets and funding from Scilex's $150 million strategic investment. This financial backing and intellectual property foundation position Datavault AI to compete in the $16 trillion global tokenized asset economy. The company's IBM-partnered AI engines and patent-backed tokenization frameworks form the technological core of these new marketplaces.</p><p>Datavault AI's cloud-based platform provides comprehensive solutions through its Acoustic Science and Data Science Divisions. The Acoustic Science Division features patented technologies including WiSA, ADIO and Sumerian foundational spatial and multichannel wireless HD sound transmission technologies available at https://www.wisatechnologies.com. These innovations cover audio timing, synchronization and multi-channel interference cancellation, representing significant advancements in audio technology.</p><p>The Data Science Division leverages Web 3.0 and high-performance computing to provide solutions for experiential data perception, valuation and secure monetization. The Information Data Exchange enables Digital Twins and licensing of name, image, and likeness by securely attaching physical real-world objects to immutable metadata objects. This approach fosters responsible AI implementation with integrity across multiple industries including sports & entertainment, events & venues, biotech, education, fintech, real estate, healthcare, and energy.</p><p>The company's technology suite offers AI and Machine Learning automation, third-party integration, detailed analytics, marketing automation and advertising monitoring capabilities. This comprehensive approach allows Datavault AI to serve diverse industry needs while maintaining customization options for specific client requirements. The establishment of these four specialized exchanges represents a strategic move to capture value across multiple segments of the emerging digital asset marketplace.</p>
