<p>SuperCom Ltd. (NASDAQ: SPCB) has secured a new contract to provide alcohol monitoring technologies and services in California, valued at up to $3 million over an initial three-year term. The contract, secured through the company's wholly owned subsidiary Leaders in Community Alternatives (LCA), is already in effect and runs through 2026 with potential for extensions and recurring revenue. This latest contract win represents another milestone in SuperCom's strategic expansion into the U.S. electronic monitoring market.</p><p>SuperCom president and CEO Ordan Trabelsi emphasized that the company's reputation for providing reliable and high-quality electronic monitoring services was pivotal in securing the contract. This achievement builds on SuperCom's track record of securing numerous new electronic monitoring contracts in both the United States and Europe. The company expressed pride in being chosen once again to support California's criminal justice system, noting that long-standing relationships with county customers and law enforcement underscore their trust in SuperCom's technology and services.</p><p>The contract further solidifies SuperCom's strong position in the electronic monitoring industry and demonstrates the growing adoption of their technologies within the U.S. criminal justice system.
