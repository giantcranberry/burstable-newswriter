<p>RMX Industries, Inc. has entered into a securities purchase agreement for an up to $50 million contingent financing facility from ATW Partners, a New York-based institutional investor. The facility is designed to provide growth capital and support RMX's next phase of expansion, including product development, customer programs and go-to-market execution.</p><p>Karl Kit, Chief Executive Officer of RMX, stated that this financing is expected to provide the flexibility needed to accelerate what's working while investing in the next set of opportunities. We're focused on disciplined growth, delivering for customers, strengthening our operational foundation, and building durable value for all stakeholders, Kit said. With ATW's support, we're positioned to move faster and scale with confidence.</p><p>The proceeds are anticipated to enhance RMX's current platforms and pipeline by advancing product development, expanding customer programs and elevating commercial coverage, while providing working-capital flexibility to pursue future commercial opportunities. Additional details regarding this finance agreement will be included in the Company's upcoming Form 1-U filing available through <a href="https://www.sec.gov" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.sec.gov</a>.</p><p>The closing of the transactions contemplated by the securities purchase agreement and the availability of initial tranche of funds under the convertible note facility are subject to the satisfaction of various closing conditions. This financing comes at a critical time for the data compression industry as organizations worldwide face increasing challenges with video bandwidth, storage needs, and power consumption.</p><p>RMX specializes in advanced data compression and video optimization technology that has demonstrated exceptional performance in demanding environments. The company's platform reduces video bandwidth, storage needs, and power consumption by up to 50% without compromising quality or functionality across any network or hardware infrastructure. Originally developed for mission-critical military applications, RMX's technology is now positioned to serve the growing needs of defense, AI, cloud, and enterprise ecosystems as data becomes a foundational asset across multiple industries.</p><p>This substantial capital infusion represents a significant vote of confidence in RMX's technology and market position at a time when efficient data management solutions are becoming increasingly crucial. The funding will enable the company to capitalize on the rapidly expanding market for intelligent data compression solutions in an increasingly digitized global economy where data transmission and storage efficiency have become critical operational considerations for organizations across multiple sectors.</p>
