<p>G2 Risk Solutions has completed its transaction with EverC, significantly expanding the company's global reach and adding proven artificial intelligence-powered risk solutions to its product portfolio. The combined organization now delivers a comprehensive suite of AI- and analyst-driven risk solutions designed to help global banks, merchant acquirers and online marketplaces strengthen their defenses against fraud, scams and illegal or unsafe transactions.</p><p>The acquisition brings EverC's deep marketplace expertise and battle-tested AI technology into G2 Risk Solutions' portfolio, promising to fortify online marketplaces against escalating threats. According to Rochelle Blease, president of G2 Risk Solutions, this strengthens what has become the circulatory system of global e-commerce. The integration positions the company to better protect the rapidly expanding digital commerce ecosystem from sophisticated financial crimes and security breaches.</p><p>Among the enhanced capabilities is Smart Scan, an AI-powered risk assessment tool for marketplaces and their payment providers that was recently launched. This technology joins other G2 Risk Solutions offerings including proprietary risk intelligence, merchant onboarding, continuous monitoring, education and professional services, scam detection solutions and automated risk management. The combined entity now serves nearly every major payment provider worldwide from offices across the United States, Europe, India and Israel.</p><p>Brian Longe, chief executive officer of G2 Risk Solutions, emphasized that the unified organization will leverage proven AI and unrivaled expertise to raise the industry standard for e-commerce risk innovation. The transaction represents a strategic move to address the growing complexity of digital fraud and regulatory compliance challenges facing financial institutions and online platforms globally. More information about the company's expanded capabilities can be found at <a href="https://g2risksolutions.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://g2risksolutions.com</a>.</p><p>The mission-focused companies have agreed not to publicly disclose the financial terms of the deal. The transaction received support from several advisory firms including DLA Piper LLP acting as legal advisor to G2 Risk Solutions, PJT serving as financial advisor to G2 Risk Solutions, Deutsche Bank acting as financial advisor to EverC, and Meitar serving as legal advisor to EverC. This consolidation in the risk management sector comes at a time when digital commerce platforms and financial institutions face increasing pressure to implement more sophisticated fraud prevention measures.</p><p>For business leaders and technology executives, this merger signals a significant advancement in the fight against digital financial crime. The combination of G2 Risk Solutions' global presence with EverC's specialized marketplace AI technology creates a more robust defense system against increasingly sophisticated fraud schemes. This development is particularly relevant as e-commerce continues to expand globally, with businesses facing mounting regulatory requirements and consumer protection expectations.</p><p>The enhanced capabilities position the combined entity to address the complex challenges of modern digital commerce, where traditional fraud prevention methods often fall short against evolving threats. The integration of AI-powered solutions with human analyst expertise represents the future of risk management in financial services and online marketplaces, potentially setting new industry standards for security and compliance.</p>
