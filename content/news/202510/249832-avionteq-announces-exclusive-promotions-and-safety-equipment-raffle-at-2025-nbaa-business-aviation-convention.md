<p>AvionTEq will showcase exclusive event-only promotions and host a product raffle at the 2025 NBAA Business Aviation Convention & Exhibition from October 14-16 at the Las Vegas Convention Center. The company's booth #3528 will feature limited-time deals on several key aviation testing products alongside a live demonstration of their equipment capabilities.</p><p>Attendees can access special pricing on multiple avionics test equipment bundles, including the FDS HHMPI & Readout Bundle that comes with a free iPad and $500 rebate when combined with readout services. The promotion also includes early access to FDS SRVIVR25 Kits for convention visitors. Additional offers include 20% discount on the CCX T-RX RP+ Multifunction Test Set with complimentary accessory kit and calibration services.</p><p>The company is also promoting several other products with bundled iPad offers, including the SDR-OMNI Multifunction Test Set, Nortec UL60-L Battery Charger/Analyzer with 5% discount, and FDT uQAR2w Wi-Fi QAR also featuring 5% savings. Special pricing on selected items and flexible rental options will be available throughout the exhibition period.</p><p>All booth visitors are eligible to enter a raffle for a UTS100 ULB Tester, described as an industry-trusted solution for precise underwater locator beacon verification. The underwater locator beacon tester represents critical safety equipment for aviation maintenance operations, ensuring emergency locator devices function properly in marine environments.</p><p>Fred Bostani, CEO of AvionTEq, emphasized the importance of NBAA-BACE as a key platform for connecting with operators and maintenance, repair, and overhaul organizations. The convention serves as a primary meeting point for aviation professionals to evaluate new technologies and equipment solutions that support aircraft safety and operational efficiency.</p><p>AvionTEq's presence at the exhibition underscores the ongoing demand for reliable testing equipment in business aviation maintenance. The promotional offerings aim to make advanced avionics testing tools more accessible to aviation organizations facing budget constraints while maintaining strict safety standards. The company's global supply network supports airlines, MROs, OEMs, and military organizations with calibration and troubleshooting solutions through their website at https://www.avionteq.com.</p>
