<p>Energy Fuels Inc. will conduct a conference call on Tuesday, November 4, 2025, at 9 a.m. Mountain Time to review its third-quarter 2025 financial performance. The company plans to release detailed financial results and operational highlights prior to the conference call, which will feature a management presentation followed by a question-and-answer session for participants.</p><p>The earnings call represents a significant opportunity for investors and stakeholders to gain insight into Energy Fuels' progress in supplying critical minerals essential for clean energy and advanced technologies. As the leading U.S. producer of natural uranium concentrate for several consecutive years, the company plays a crucial role in supporting nuclear utilities that generate carbon-free nuclear energy. The quarterly results will provide important indicators about the company's operational efficiency and market position in the rapidly evolving energy sector.</p><p>Energy Fuels' diversified portfolio extends beyond uranium production to include rare earth elements, heavy mineral sands, vanadium, and medical isotopes. The company operates the White Mesa Mill in Utah, the only fully licensed and operational conventional uranium processing facility in the United States. This facility also produces advanced rare earth element products and vanadium oxide when market conditions support production, while evaluating potential recovery of medical isotopes from existing uranium process streams for emerging Targeted Alpha Therapy cancer treatments.</p><p>The company's international expansion includes developing three heavy mineral sands projects: the Toliara Project in Madagascar, the Bahia Project in Brazil, and the Donald Project in Australia, where Energy Fuels has the right to earn up to a 49% interest in a joint venture with Astron Corporation Limited. These developments position Energy Fuels as a key player in the global supply chain for critical minerals necessary for renewable energy technologies, electronics, and medical applications.</p><p>Investors seeking additional information about Energy Fuels can access the company's latest news and updates through its newsroom at <a href="http://ibn.fm/UUUU" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">http://ibn.fm/UUUU</a>. The upcoming earnings conference call will provide crucial insights into how the company is navigating market dynamics while contributing to the transition toward cleaner energy solutions and advanced medical technologies.</p>
