<p>RChilli, a leading provider of AI-driven recruitment solutions and Oracle partner, will exhibit its latest innovations at Oracle AI World from October 13-16, 2025, at The Venetian Convention & Expo Center in Las Vegas. The company's advanced technology enhances recruitment through automation, enriched data quality, and bias-free evaluation, all integrated with Oracle Fusion Cloud Human Capital Management (HCM).</p><p>The company's product suite offers significant value through integration with Oracle Cloud HCM solutions, providing organizations with tools including Candidate Profile Import, Essential Data Enhancer, Full Database Reprocessing, List Of Values, Customizable Taxonomy, Redact & Design, Browser Assistant, Email Importer, and Bulk Data Import. Josh Adams, Director of ERP Operations at RChilli, announced the introduction of RChilli's AI Agents for Recruitment at the event, describing them as advanced virtual assistants designed to automate crucial hiring tasks such as profile analysis, pre-screening, data validation, and interview scheduling.</p><p>These AI agents focus on reducing bias and supporting inclusive hiring practices while streamlining recruitment processes to ensure fair and efficient candidate evaluation. RChilli has built its solutions on responsible AI principles, ensuring innovations enhance trust, transparency, and business value according to CEO Vinay Johar. The collaboration with Oracle allows RChilli to deliver intelligent recruitment automation at scale, helping organizations achieve cleaner data, reduce manual work, and accelerate smarter hiring decisions.</p><p>RChilli's long history of engineering recruitment solutions includes automated extraction and standardization of data, helping organizations maintain well-structured candidate databases that contribute to improved decision-making and reduced time-to-hire. The company's products are available on multiple platforms including Oracle Cloud Marketplace, SAP SuccessFactors, and Salesforce AppExchange, ensuring global accessibility and trusted performance. Oracle's partner program, detailed at <a href="https://www.oracle.com/partner/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.oracle.com/partner/</a>, provides partners with choice and flexibility through several program pathways and benefits spanning training, go-to-market collaboration, technical accelerators, and success support.</p><p>Attendees at Oracle AI World can visit RChilli's booth #6029 to experience live demos, explore real-world success stories, and meet with product experts. The company's participation highlights the growing importance of AI-powered recruitment automation in addressing modern hiring challenges while maintaining compliance with various international standards including ISO 27001:2022, SOC 2 Type II, NYC AEDT LAW, HIPAA, FedRAMP Ready, and GDPR.</p>
