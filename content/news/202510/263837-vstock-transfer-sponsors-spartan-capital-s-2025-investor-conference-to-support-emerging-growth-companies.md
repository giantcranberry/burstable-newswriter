<p>Vstock Transfer will serve as a Bronze sponsor of the Spartan Capital Securities' Second Annual Investor Conference, scheduled for November 3, 2025, at the Marriott Marquis Hotel in New York City. The sponsorship demonstrates Vstock Transfer's ongoing commitment to supporting innovative companies and facilitating meaningful engagement between issuers and the investment community. This conference has established itself as one of the premier gatherings for growth companies, institutional investors, and industry leaders seeking to identify emerging opportunities in the market.</p><p>Seth Farbman, Co-Founder of Vstock Transfer, emphasized the strategic importance of their participation, stating that sponsoring the Spartan Capital Conference reflects their commitment to the ecosystem of CEOs and investors driving the next generation of IPOs and SPACs. As a trusted stock transfer agent that supports public and private companies through every stage of the capital markets process, Vstock Transfer's role involves supporting companies through every stage of going public with precision, transparency, and deep market understanding.</p><p>John D. Lowry, Founder and Chief Executive Officer of Spartan Capital Securities, LLC, expressed pride in having Vstock Transfer join as a Bronze sponsor for their second annual investor conference. Lowry noted that Vstock's support underscores the caliber of this event and the shared commitment to fostering meaningful connections in the investment community. The conference, organized in partnership with B2i Digital, will feature presentations from more than 60 carefully selected companies across technology, healthcare, consumer, mining, precious metals, and other high-opportunity sectors.</p><p>David Shapiro, CEO of B2i Digital, highlighted the critical role sponsors play in making conferences like this possible, expressing gratitude for Vstock Transfer's involvement. Shapiro explained that Vstock Transfer's participation not only strengthens their ability to create meaningful connections between investors and presenting companies but also helps advance B2i Digital's core mission of combining the reach of digital marketing with the impact of face-to-face engagement. The program will include main-stage presentations, panel discussions, curated one-on-one meetings, and extensive networking sessions designed to maximize interactions among investors, presenting companies, and sponsors.</p><p>With over 500 institutional and high-net-worth investors expected to attend, the conference represents a significant opportunity for growth companies to access capital and for investors to identify promising investment opportunities. The event's timing coincides with increased activity in public offerings and special purpose acquisition companies, making Vstock Transfer's expertise in stock transfer services particularly relevant for participating companies navigating the complexities of public markets. For more information about the conference and registration details, please visit <a href="https://spartancapital.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://spartancapital.com</a>.</p>
