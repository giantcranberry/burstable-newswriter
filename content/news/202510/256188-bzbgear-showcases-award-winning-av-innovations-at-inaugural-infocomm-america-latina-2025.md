<p>BZBGEAR will showcase its award-winning professional audiovisual and broadcasting equipment at the inaugural InfoComm América Latina 2025 event in Mexico City from October 22-24. The exhibition marks the first time the prominent industry event has expanded to Latin America, transforming the World Trade Center into a hub for AV innovation featuring extensive exhibition floors, Spanish-language conferences, and immersive demonstration rooms.</p><p>The company will display its refreshed brand identity alongside new Pro AV and broadcasting solutions at Booth 75, where visitors can register for free passes. Eugene Bocharov, CEO and President of BZBGEAR, emphasized the significance of participating in the region's first major industry event, stating the company looks forward to learning about local markets and demonstrating partnership capabilities.</p><p>Among the featured innovations is the BG-8K-1616MA, recognized as the market's first 8K 16x16 matrix switcher and an InfoComm 2025 award winner. Another highlight includes the BG-IPGEAR-XTREME-PRO, an 8K AV over IP multicast transceiver operating over 1 Gb networks that earned recognition at IBC2025. The company will also present the BG-STREAM-DE, a 4K video streaming encoder/decoder/recorder compatible with Dante AV-H standards and another IBC2025 award recipient.</p><p>The exhibition will feature the COMMANDER G2, a second-generation IP/Serial joystick controller with a 5-inch touchscreen designed for precision control applications. BZBGEAR's 4K-VP and VPPRO series matrix switchers will demonstrate capabilities for video walls, multiview applications, and signal extension, described as versatile tools for AV distribution. The ADAMO series PTZ cameras, recognized as the most awarded in the market, will be shown with enhanced 40x optical zoom lenses and AI auto-tracking features in both FHD and UHD resolutions.</p><p>InfoComm América Latina 2025 represents a significant milestone for the professional AV industry's expansion into Latin American markets, featuring comprehensive educational programming through Congreso AVIXA in Spanish, focusing on artificial intelligence, sustainability, marketing, and industry trends. The event also includes AVIXA Xchange LIVE networking opportunities connecting industry leaders and innovators across the region. More information about BZBGEAR's products and solutions can be found at <a href="https://bzbgear.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://bzbgear.com</a>.</p>
