<p>inGroup International announced that its flagship brand inCruises has been honored with Gold and Silver Travel Weekly Magellan Awards, continuing a four-year streak of industry recognition. The company received these prestigious awards for excellence in both digital innovation and partner development programs within the travel industry.</p><p>The Gold Magellan Award was awarded in the Marketing category for inCruises' newly launched public website, which features a modern, intuitive design that better communicates the brand's mission to make luxury travel experiences more accessible and rewarding globally. The enhanced website improves user experience while delivering clearer, more robust messaging for both Members and Partners, reflecting the company's commitment to evolving digital outreach.</p><p>Additionally, the AMP UP global initiative earned a Silver Magellan Award in the Marketing category for its significant impact on community engagement and growth of the company's unique referral marketing model. This program challenges and inspires Partners to expand their teams, strengthening the company's position in the travel industry.</p><p>Michael Hutchison, inGroup's Chief Executive Officer and Co-Founder, stated that these awards highlight the company's continued commitment to evolving both digital outreach and empowering Partners worldwide. He expressed satisfaction in seeing the technology and marketing team's efforts recognized alongside other leading innovators in the travel industry.</p><p>Doug Corrigan, Chief Marketing Officer, added that the company is honored to be recognized again by Travel Weekly and emphasized their constant effort to deliver messaging that matches their unparalleled offering. The Travel Weekly Magellan Awards honor the best in travel services, and this latest recognition reinforces inCruises' position as a leader in global travel innovation.</p><p>The awards further strengthen inCruises' reputation for excellence in travel technology, marketing, and Partner support. As the world's largest subscription-based travel club, inCruises has booked more than 600,000 guests since launching in 2016 and provides Members access to nearly 200,000 cruises, hotels, and resort offers through its platform available at <a href="https://inCruises.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://inCruises.com</a>.</p><p>inGroup International maintains its commitment to corporate citizenship through support of humanitarian efforts including Mercy Ships. The company's broader corporate information and additional details about its operations can be found at <a href="https://in.Group" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://in.Group</a>, reflecting the organization's global reach and corporate philosophy.</p>
