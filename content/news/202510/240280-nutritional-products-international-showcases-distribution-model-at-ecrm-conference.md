<p>Mitch Gould, founder and CEO of Nutritional Products International and InHealth Media, presented his Evolution of Distribution model at the 2025 ECRM Vitamin, Weight Management & Sports Nutrition Session held at the PGA National Resort & Spa in Palm Beach Gardens, Florida. The conference served as a critical platform for connecting retailers and suppliers across the health and wellness industry, with ECRM events establishing themselves as the standard for building relationships between retail buyers and industry decision makers.</p><p>Gould's participation emphasized his company's ongoing commitment to accelerating brand growth in the highly competitive U.S. marketplace. The session provided an optimized environment for both established and emerging brands to meet directly with key category buyers from across multiple retail channels. ECRM continues to be one of the most effective venues for building relationships with retail buyers and industry decision makers, said Gould. The opportunity to engage directly with these professionals allows us to showcase how NPI and IHM help brands navigate the complexities of entering and expanding within the U.S. market.</p><p>During the conference, Gould made presentations to major retailers including Walmart, GNC, Vitamin Shoppe, Vitamin World, CVS, and Walgreens. These presentations reinforced NPI's position as a trusted partner for connecting brands with some of the most influential names in U.S. retail. The company's decades of experience span sports nutrition, dietary supplements, health and wellness, and consumer packaged goods, providing valuable insights for businesses looking to penetrate the competitive American market.</p><p>The Evolution of Distribution platform simplifies the entire process for brands seeking U.S. market entry, offering comprehensive services including importing, warehousing, regulatory compliance, retail distribution, and strategic marketing. This turnkey approach provides international and domestic brands with an efficient, cost-effective strategy for success in the challenging U.S. marketplace. From day one, our mission has been to create an efficient, cost-effective strategy for international and domestic brands looking to succeed in the U.S., Gould added.</p><p>InHealth Media team members bring more than 30 years of combined experience with health, skin care, fitness and nutrition brands to the partnership. The company's distribution to thousands of outlets throughout the U.S. has been accomplished through knowledge, experience and relationships with key executives. This comprehensive understanding of modern consumer preferences, combined with extensive resources and tools, positions both companies as valuable partners for brands seeking retail success in the evolving health and wellness landscape.</p>
