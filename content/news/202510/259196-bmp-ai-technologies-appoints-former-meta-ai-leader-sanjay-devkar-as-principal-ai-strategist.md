<p>BMP AI Technologies Inc. has appointed Sanjay Devkar as Principal AI Strategist, bringing over 25 years of global experience in artificial intelligence and machine learning to the company. Devkar's background includes leadership roles at Meta Platforms Inc., where he led a 300-member organization focused on perception and personalization models for VR/AR devices, and previous positions at Amazon Web Services, IBM, and Infosys.</p><p>During his tenure at Meta's Reality Labs, Devkar's team standardized MLOps practices across multiple product surfaces, reduced inference latency by 18 percent through runtime optimization, and advanced privacy-by-design frameworks in coordination with global regulators. His experience spans AI systems architecture, governance, and scalable deployment, making him particularly valuable for BMP AI's strategic direction in enterprise AI adoption.</p><p>Vighnesh Dobale, Chief Executive Officer of BMP AI Technologies Inc., emphasized the strategic importance of this appointment, stating that Devkar's leadership in AI and VR at Meta, combined with his deep understanding of MLOps and compliance, will strengthen the company's capability to deliver robust, trustworthy AI solutions for global clients. The appointment signals BMP AI's commitment to advancing AI infrastructure for regulated industries including fintech, healthcare, and enterprise automation.</p><p>Devkar will focus on developing reliable and cost-efficient AI models for real-time decision support and predictive insight, establishing AI governance frameworks that balance innovation with privacy and safety requirements, and expanding R&D partnerships across the MENA region. He will also guide scalable deployment of BMP AI's flagship chatbot and blockchain platforms for enterprise clients.</p><p>In his statement, Devkar highlighted the evolving nature of AI adoption, noting that reliability and accountability now matter as much as accuracy in AI systems. He expressed his commitment to working with BMP AI to build systems that are both impactful and responsible, reflecting the growing industry emphasis on ethical AI deployment.
