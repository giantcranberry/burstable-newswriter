<p>Tompkins Financial Corporation reported diluted earnings per share of $1.65 for the third quarter of 2025, representing a 26.9% increase from the $1.30 reported for the same period in 2024. Net income for the quarter reached $23.7 million, up $5.0 million compared to the third quarter of 2024, demonstrating substantial improvement in the company's financial performance.</p><p>President and CEO Stephen Romaine attributed the strong results to the company's team strength and balance sheet quality. The company's improved earnings reflected strategic investments in the business, with expenses for the 2025 year-to-date period increasing over 4% compared to the prior year period. The net interest margin improved significantly to 3.20% in the third quarter of 2025, up 41 basis points from the third quarter of 2024.</p><p>This expansion contributed to net interest income of $63.9 million for the quarter, representing a $10.7 million or 20.1% increase compared to the same period last year. The improvement in net interest margin resulted from increased yields on average interest-earning assets and higher average loan balances, combined with lower funding costs from an improved funding mix. Loan growth remained robust, with total loans at September 30, 2025 increasing $406.8 million, or 6.9%, from September 30, 2024.</p><p>The growth was primarily concentrated in commercial real estate and commercial and industrial portfolios. Deposit growth also showed strength, with total deposits reaching $7.1 billion at September 30, 2025, up $475.2 million or 7.2% from the same period in 2024. Asset quality metrics showed improvement, with nonperforming assets declining to $53.0 million, representing 0.63% of total assets at September 30, 2025, down from $62.7 million or 0.78% at September 30, 2024.</p><p>The allowance for credit losses coverage ratio strengthened to 113.06% at September 30, 2025, compared to 88.51% at September 30, 2024, reflecting improved protection against potential loan losses. Capital ratios remained strong, with the ratio of total capital to risk-weighted assets at 13.27% at September 30, 2025, well above regulatory minimums for well-capitalized institutions. The company maintained a stable liquidity position with ready access to $1.5 billion in liquidity, representing 17.8% of total assets.</p><p>For more detailed financial information, investors can visit <a href="https://www.tompkinsfinancial.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.tompkinsfinancial.com</a>. Noninterest income showed modest growth, increasing 0.8% to $23.6 million for the third quarter of 2025 compared to the same period in 2024. Year-to-date noninterest income of $71.1 million was up $3.8 million or 5.7% compared to the same period in 2024, driven by increases in other income, insurance commissions and fees, and wealth management fees.</p><p>The company's performance through the first nine months of 2025 showed consistent improvement, with diluted earnings per share of $4.52 for the period ended September 30, 2025, up 25.9% from the $3.59 reported for the same period in 2024. Year-to-date net income reached $64.8 million, representing a $13.6 million or 26.6% increase compared to the prior year period. These results indicate strong operational efficiency and strategic positioning within the competitive financial services landscape.</p>
