This proven performance record suggests significant potential for enhancing security and military operations where explosive threat detection is critical. The collaboration represents a meaningful advancement in drone capabilities, particularly for applications requiring rapid threat assessment and response.</p><p>Red Cat's Black Widow drones will also integrate with Safe Pro's new SPOTD Navigation, Observation and Detection Engine, described as a powerful, edge-based solution that processes data locally rather than relying on cloud connectivity.
