<p>Strawberry Fields REIT, Inc. (NYSE AMERICAN: STRW) reported $18.4 million in Adjusted Funds From Operations for the second quarter of 2025, representing a significant increase from the $14.3 million recorded during the same period in 2024. The self-administered real estate investment trust, which focuses on skilled nursing and healthcare-related properties, described this as its strongest quarter since founding. The company's expansion trajectory is evident in its portfolio growth from 33 skilled nursing facilities in 2015 to 142 healthcare facilities currently, encompassing over 15,500 licensed beds across 10 states.</p><p>Moishe Gubin, Founder, CEO, and Chairman of Strawberry Fields, attributed the company's success to its unique approach combining healthcare industry knowledge with real estate expertise. Gubin emphasized the benefits of the company's master lease structure during the earnings announcement, noting that this operational model has been instrumental in driving consistent performance. The company's strategic positioning has enabled it to carve out growing market share in the healthcare real estate sector while maintaining focus on the real estate aspects of skilled nursing facility operations.</p><p>The company's growth story reflects broader trends in healthcare real estate investment, particularly in the skilled nursing sector where demographic shifts and aging populations continue to drive demand. Strawberry Fields' expansion from a modest portfolio to operating 142 facilities demonstrates the scalability of its business model and the increasing institutional interest in healthcare-related real estate assets. Investors can access the latest news and updates relating to STRW through the company's <a href="https://ibn.fm/STRW" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">newsroom</a>.</p><p>Looking ahead, Strawberry Fields REIT is scheduled to announce its third quarter financial results on November 6 after market close, followed by its quarterly earnings call on November 7. These upcoming announcements will provide further insight into whether the company can maintain its current growth momentum and continue its expansion in the competitive healthcare real estate market. The consistent performance and strategic growth position Strawberry Fields as a significant player in an industry that continues to attract substantial investment interest due to demographic tailwinds and healthcare sector stability.</p>
