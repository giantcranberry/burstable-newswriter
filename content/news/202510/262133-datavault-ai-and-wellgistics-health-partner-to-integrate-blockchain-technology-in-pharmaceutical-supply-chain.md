<p>Datavault AI Inc. (NASDAQ: DVLT) and Wellgistics Health Inc. (NASDAQ: WGRX) have signed a non-binding letter of intent to integrate DataVault's blockchain-enabled PharmacyChain smart contract technology into Wellgistics' proprietary infrastructure. This partnership aims to digitize prescription tracking from script to fulfillment, ensuring accurate, transparent and efficient delivery while supporting patient safety and optimized outcomes.</p><p>The collaboration represents a significant advancement in pharmaceutical supply chain management by leveraging blockchain technology to create an immutable record of prescription drug movement. This integration could potentially reduce medication errors, prevent counterfeit drugs from entering the supply chain, and provide real-time visibility into prescription status for healthcare providers and patients alike. The companies are exploring a revenue-sharing model based on PharmacyChain usage fees, with definitive terms to be determined following further negotiation.</p><p>Datavault AI, headquartered in Beaverton, Oregon, specializes in AI-driven data experience, valuation and monetization. The company's cloud-based platform provides comprehensive solutions with a collaborative focus in its Acoustic Science and Data Science Divisions. More information about Datavault AI's technology offerings can be found at <a href="https://www.datavaultsite.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.datavaultsite.com</a>. The company's Data Science Division leverages the power of Web 3.0 and high-performance computing to provide solutions for experiential data perception, valuation and secure monetization.</p><p>Wellgistics Health operates as a next-generation pharmaceutical distribution and digital prescription routing company. The integration of blockchain technology into their existing infrastructure represents a forward-looking approach to addressing longstanding challenges in pharmaceutical supply chain management. This partnership could set new standards for transparency and efficiency in an industry where accurate tracking and verification are critical for patient safety.</p><p>The latest news and updates relating to DVLT are available in the company's newsroom at <a href="https://ibn.fm/DVLT" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://ibn.fm/DVLT</a>. The collaboration between these two NASDAQ-listed companies demonstrates how emerging technologies like blockchain and artificial intelligence are being applied to solve real-world problems in healthcare and pharmaceutical distribution. As the partnership develops, it could potentially influence how prescription drugs are tracked and managed across the entire healthcare ecosystem, creating new benchmarks for security and efficiency in medication delivery systems.</p>
