<p>Dr. Krystylle Richardson's INNOVALIGY® philosophy has achieved international bestseller status across multiple Amazon categories including Knowledge Capital, Organizational Change, and Leadership Strategy shortly after its release. The framework, detailed in her book INNOVALIGY®: The New Manual of Innovation – 10 Precepts to Empower the RISE of the Everyday Innovator, represents a lifestyle approach to innovation designed for everyone from students and startups to executives and policy leaders.</p><p>Dr. Richardson describes innovation as being within reach of everyone every day, emphasizing that this book aims to awaken the innovator in all people and build cultures where ideas can transform communities. The global launch comes at a critical time when businesses and individuals are seeking new pathways for growth and resilience in a rapidly changing world. Dr. Richardson brings over 45 years of industry experience from leadership roles at companies including Ford, General Motors, GE HealthCare, PepsiCo, and Motorola, along with expertise as an ISO, FDA, Six Sigma, CE Mark, and risk assessment auditor and consultant.</p><p>Her background provides proven insight into how innovation has always been essential rather than optional, addressing the common leadership realization of innovation's value only after projects collapse or revenue repercussions occur. Following the successful virtual global launch event hosted by Dr. Akinyemi O. Samuel with special guest HRH Dr. Clyde Rivers, Dr. Richardson has taken INNOVALIGY® beyond the book into practical application.</p><p>She is currently in Nairobi, Kenya, beginning a series of engagements where she will introduce Innovaligy® as a teaching framework in academic institutions and entrepreneurial hubs. This Africa debut marks a significant expansion of the philosophy from its origins in the USA and Europe, demonstrating the global momentum behind making innovation a daily discipline that empowers people everywhere to create solutions.</p><p>The book provides practical value across multiple audiences. For entrepreneurs, it addresses the tension between bold ideas and limited resources through the 10 Precepts of Innovation, offering strategies to transform challenges into opportunities and scale solutions in resource-constrained environments. Corporate leaders gain insights on cultivating innovation cultures that avoid bureaucratic suffocation, while students and aspiring changemakers receive frameworks to overcome self-doubt and become everyday innovators.</p><p>Policy makers and community leaders learn how innovation can address systemic challenges in education, healthcare, and economic development by shifting mindsets and empowering ownership of change. INNOVALIGY® is available in multiple formats including Kindle, paperback, and hardcover editions through Amazon.
