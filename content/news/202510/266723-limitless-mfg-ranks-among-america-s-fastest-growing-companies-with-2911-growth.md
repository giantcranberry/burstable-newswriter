<p>Limitless MFG, a Los Angeles-based full-service manufacturing and branding agency, has achieved remarkable recognition as one of America's fastest-growing private companies. The company secured the #129 position nationwide on the prestigious 2025 Inc. 5000 list, while also ranking #6 in U.S. manufacturing, #22 in California, and #8 in Los Angeles. This recognition highlights the company's extraordinary 2,911 percent growth over three years, positioning it as a national leader in creative manufacturing services.</p><p>Founded by Miller McCoy, Stephen Mangini, and Brett Schwers, Limitless MFG has evolved from an in-house operation into a vertically integrated agency that brings creative vision to life. The company offers comprehensive services including product design, manufacturing, fulfillment, e-commerce, and tour merchandising, enabling brands and creators to move efficiently from concept to market. This integrated approach combines creative direction, technical expertise, and operational precision to provide clients with a seamless production experience from start to finish.</p><p>Miller McCoy, CEO of Limitless MFG, emphasized the company's mission, stating, "From day one, our mission has been to redefine how brands in entertainment, fashion, and lifestyle industries bring products to market. This recognition by Inc. is proof of what's possible when you combine a talented team, loyal clients, and relentless focus on quality and execution." The company's growth reflects both operational excellence and deep understanding of modern brand needs, serving clients ranging from global touring artists and viral influencers to established consumer brands seeking comprehensive creative, production, and logistics solutions under one roof.</p><p>Limitless MFG's notable partnerships include collaborations with major retailers and brands such as Nordstrom Rack, B.B. Simon, Stevenson Ranch, and Gin & Juice by Dre and Snoop. These diverse collaborations demonstrate the company's ability to operate effectively across retail, fashion, and entertainment sectors while maintaining consistent quality and innovation standards. The company's success is driven by its three co-founders' complementary expertise: McCoy leads strategic vision and growth, Mangini focuses on creative development and brand strategy, and Schwers manages production systems, logistics, and fulfillment.</p><p>The company's Los Angeles headquarters keeps it connected to the heart of entertainment and fashion while serving clients globally. As Limitless MFG continues to expand its global production network and client base, it sets new standards for how brands create and distribute products. McCoy summarized the company's philosophy, stating, "Limitless isn't just our name. It's the mindset that drives everything we do." This recognition from Inc. Magazine represents more than a milestone; it signifies the emergence of a new agency model that understands both the artistic and infrastructural elements essential for brand success in today's competitive market.</p>
