<p>Hidden Brains has been honored with the Bronze Stevie Award for Company of the Year in Computer Services, representing the company's 19th international business award. The recognition ceremony occurred in Portugal on October 10 after an extensive two-month judging process involving more than 250 professionals globally. This accolade underscores Hidden Brains' 22-year history of providing superior computer-related services, including consulting, support, integration, operations management, and technology-enabled solutions.</p><p>The International Business Awards selection process integrates expert jury assessment and public voting, with organizations submitting nominations that detail their accomplishments. Panels of industry professionals evaluate and score entries based on innovation, impact, service excellence, and measurable results. Entries surpassing specific score thresholds earn Gold, Silver, or Bronze awards. This esteemed acknowledgment positions Hidden Brains among top-tier technology firms, building on previous honors such as being named 'best software development company' by GESIA and receiving the 'Emerging AI product of the year' for datums.</p><p>Chirag Patel, VP President at Hidden Brains, highlighted the company's innovation philosophy, noting that innovation extends beyond discovery to the value and impact it generates. The company employs a comprehensive framework focused on collective aspects that meet benchmarks, whether through implementation, strategy, or optimizing client objectives. With a team of over 700 developers, strategists, and innovators, Hidden Brains has established itself as a creator and driver of new technology, serving clients in more than 107 countries.</p><p>Hidden Brains' recent international expansion into markets like the UAE, USA, and UK has enabled it to address diverse client challenges while unlocking new opportunities for technological transformation. Its client portfolio ranges from startups to Fortune 500 companies, including well-known brands such as Caterpillar, Honeywell, and Best Buy. For additional details on the Stevie Awards and other recipients, visit <a href="https://www.stevieawards.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.stevieawards.com</a>. The Stevie Awards program annually receives over 12,000 entries from organizations in more than 70 nations, celebrating exceptional workplace performances across nine award programs.</p>
