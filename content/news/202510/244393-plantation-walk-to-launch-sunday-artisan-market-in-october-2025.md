<p>Plantation Walk, Broward County's premier entertainment district, will debut the Sunday Morning Artisan Market on October 12, 2025, establishing a weekly showcase for South Florida's best local artisans, craft makers, and specialty vendors. The market will operate every Sunday from 10 AM to 2 PM in the Plantation Walk Plaza, creating a new weekend tradition that combines commerce with community engagement.</p><p>Shoppers will discover handcrafted goods, art, jewelry, home decor, baked treats, gourmet foods, and specialty items while enjoying live music throughout the Plaza. This initiative represents a significant investment in local creative talent while providing residents with a unique Sunday destination that combines shopping, entertainment, and community engagement.</p><p>Visitors can also explore Plantation Walk's two dozen restaurants, shops, services, and experiences just steps away from the Plaza, making the Artisan Market a complete weekend destination. The market's strategic location within an established entertainment district creates natural synergies between vendors and existing businesses, potentially driving additional foot traffic to all establishments in the area. Parking and admission are free, removing barriers to participation and encouraging broad community involvement.</p><p>The Sunday Morning Artisan Market represents an important development for South Florida's creative economy, providing a dedicated platform for local artisans to reach customers directly.
