<p>Global Tax Network (GTN), a leading mobility tax services firm, has been named a Best of the Best Firm by INSIDE Public Accounting (IPA) for 2025, marking another year of recognition in this prestigious industry distinction that has been awarded for nearly three decades. The Best of the Best designation honors 75 firms annually that demonstrate excellence in management, growth, and forward-looking strategy, selected from more than 600 US and Canadian firms participating in IPA's 35th Annual Practice Management Survey.</p><p>Eric Loff, President of GTN, expressed pride in the achievement, stating that the recognition reflects not only the firm's success but, more importantly, the exceptional commitment and talent of the GTN team. He emphasized that their dedication to delivering exceptional experiences for clients and colleagues makes such achievements possible. The 2025 IPA Best of the Best Firms represent a diverse range of organizational sizes, with net revenues spanning from $4.2 million to $2.2 billion.</p><p>The selection process for Best of the Best Firms involves rigorous evaluation across more than 35 key performance metrics that identify organizations delivering outstanding financial and operational results while fostering sustainable growth and creating long-term value for both clients and employees. For detailed information about the award criteria and selection process, visit <a href="https://insidepublicaccounting.com/top-firms/best-of-the-best/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://insidepublicaccounting.com/top-firms/best-of-the-best/</a>.</p><p>INSIDE Public Accounting has established itself as a respected independent publication known for comprehensive benchmarking reports within the accounting industry, with annual surveys providing in-depth analysis and insights into best practices that drive success across hundreds of accounting firms nationwide. Organizations throughout the country rely on IPA's benchmarking data to refine their business strategies and elevate performance.</p><p>This recognition carries significant weight in the competitive accounting services landscape, where firms must continuously demonstrate excellence in both client service and operational management. The Best of the Best designation serves as an important industry benchmark, helping corporate clients identify top-performing firms that can deliver consistent, high-quality services. For business leaders evaluating accounting partners, this distinction provides valuable third-party validation of operational excellence and strategic positioning.</p><p>GTN's specialization in mobility tax services positions the firm at the intersection of global workforce trends and complex tax regulations, a critical area as businesses increasingly operate across borders and employees work in multiple jurisdictions. The growing demand for sophisticated mobility tax expertise reflects broader trends in global business operations and remote work arrangements, making GTN's recognition particularly relevant for multinational corporations and organizations with distributed workforces. The firm's validation by IPA underscores its capability to navigate complex cross-border tax challenges while maintaining the operational standards required in today's competitive professional services environment.</p>
