<p>The Hong Kong International Lighting Fair (Autumn Edition) and Hong Kong International Outdoor and Tech Light Expo will open next week, bringing together leading global brands to showcase innovative lighting technologies under the theme "Illuminated Designs for a Smarter Future." Organized by the Hong Kong Trade Development Council, the twin events will feature approximately 3,000 exhibitors from 20 countries and regions, including representation from Asia-Pacific, Europe, and the Americas.</p><p>The Autumn Lighting Fair, running from October 27-30 at the Hong Kong Convention and Exhibition Centre, will highlight the Hall of Connected Lighting, featuring about 70 leading brands showcasing cutting-edge smart lighting and IoT applications. Notable participants include local brand GRE Alpha, Foshan Electrical and Lighting, and TUYA Smart from the Chinese Mainland, along with international companies such as Casambi and LEDiL from Finland, Moorgen from Germany, Koizumi from Japan, and Signify from the Netherlands. The zone will present award-winning designs and immersive lighting experiences, with Signify unveiling its latest product series during the event.</p><p>Both fairs will showcase internationally recognized lighting designs featured in prominent architectural and cultural projects worldwide, including Gardens by the Bay in Singapore, the BBC Headquarters in London, the Sanxingdui Museum in Sichuan province, and Hong Kong's M+ visual art museum. This highlights Hong Kong's position as Asia's premier hub for smart lighting innovation. The Autumn Lighting Fair's Hall of Aurora will present approximately 540 renowned global brands featuring creative, high-quality lighting products.</p><p>The Hong Kong International Outdoor and Tech Light Expo, taking place from October 28-31 at AsiaWorld-Expo, focuses on outdoor, industrial, and public lighting technologies that support smart city development and sustainable solutions. The event features specialized zones including Outdoor and Public Lighting, Smart Pole and Solution, Horticultural Lighting, and Technical & Professional Lighting. The Smart Pole and Solution zone will showcase products integrating IoT sensors, data collection, communications, and energy management to enable more efficient urban lighting infrastructure.</p><p>Global buyers can register for free admission at <a href="https://tinyurl.com/4xrh93ek" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://tinyurl.com/4xrh93ek</a>, while additional event information is available through the official websites at <a href="https://hklightingfairae.hktdc.com/en" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">hklightingfairae.hktdc.com/en</a> and <a href="https://hkotlexpo.hktdc.com/en" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">hkotlexpo.hktdc.com/en</a>. Eco Expo Asia 2025 will run concurrently from October 28-31 at AsiaWorld-Expo, with complimentary shuttle bus service provided between the two exhibition venues throughout the event period.</p>
