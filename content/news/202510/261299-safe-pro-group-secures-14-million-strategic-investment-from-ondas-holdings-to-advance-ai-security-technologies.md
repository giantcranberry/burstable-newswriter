<p>Safe Pro Group Inc. has entered into definitive agreements for a $14 million strategic investment led by Ondas Holdings Inc., marking a significant expansion of their partnership in autonomous security technologies. The private placement involves two million shares of Safe Pro common stock and is expected to close on or about October 22, 2025, with proceeds designated to accelerate development and integration of the company's AI-driven computer vision and threat detection systems.</p><p>This investment builds upon Ondas Holdings' prior investment in August, demonstrating growing collaboration between the two companies focused on expanding Safe Pro's operational reach and enhancing mission safety through artificial intelligence and autonomous technologies. The partnership combines Safe Pro's expertise in AI-powered defense solutions with Ondas' specialization in autonomous drone technologies, creating synergies that could transform security operations across multiple sectors.</p><p>Safe Pro Group delivers AI-enabled security and defense solutions through cutting-edge platforms like SPOTD, providing advanced situational awareness tools for defense, humanitarian, and homeland security applications globally. The company specializes in artificial intelligence solutions for drone imagery processing, leveraging commercially available drones with proprietary machine learning and computer vision technology to enable rapid identification of explosives threats. This approach provides a safer and more efficient alternative to traditional human-based analysis methods.</p><p>Built on a cloud-based ecosystem powered by <a href="https://aws.amazon.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">Amazon Web Services (AWS)</a>, Safe Pro Group's scalable platform targets multiple markets including commercial, government, law enforcement and humanitarian sectors. The company's integrated approach combines Safe Pro AI software, Safe-Pro USA protective gear and Airborne Response drone-based services to deliver comprehensive safety and operational efficiency solutions. The latest news and updates relating to SPAI are available in the company's newsroom at <a href="http://ibn.fm/SPAI" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">http://ibn.fm/SPAI</a>.</p><p>The strategic investment represents a significant validation of Safe Pro's technology roadmap and market positioning in the rapidly evolving defense technology sector. As global security challenges continue to evolve, the integration of AI and autonomous systems becomes increasingly critical for effective threat detection and response capabilities. This funding enables Safe Pro to accelerate the deployment of its AI-driven solutions across multiple applications, potentially transforming how security operations are conducted in both military and civilian contexts.</p>
