<p>OptimumBank Holdings, Inc. will participate in the ceremonial ringing of the Opening Bell at the New York Stock Exchange on Monday, October 27, 2025, commemorating the bank's 25th anniversary. The event represents a significant milestone for the financial institution that began operations in 2000 in Ft. Lauderdale, Florida, and has since grown into a billion-dollar organization.</p><p>Chairman Moishe Gubin will lead the ceremony from the NYSE Trading Floor, accompanied by executives, board members, and team leaders. Gubin described the anniversary as a defining moment for the organization, reflecting on OptimumBank's transformation from a small community lender in Broward County to an institution with expanded reach across Florida and beyond. The celebration acknowledges 25 years of growth, resilience, and community-focused service that has characterized the bank's development.</p><p>The Opening Bell ceremony is scheduled to begin at 9:30 a.m. Eastern Time and will be streamed live on the NYSE's official YouTube channel, providing public access to the commemorative event. This public recognition at one of the world's most prominent financial exchanges underscores the bank's journey and achievements over the past quarter-century, serving as both a celebration of past accomplishments and a statement of continued ambition in the financial services sector.</p><p>OptimumBank has built its reputation on providing strongly service-oriented banking with reasonable fees, positioning itself as an alternative to larger financial institutions. The bank remains committed to supporting economic development through responsible banking practices and community partnerships. Its comprehensive financial solutions include Business Banking, Business Lending, SBA Lending Solutions, Treasury Management, and Personal Banking services designed to meet diverse customer needs.</p><p>For investors seeking additional information about OptimumBank Holdings, the company maintains updated resources through its newsroom at <a href="https://ibn.fm/OPHC" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://ibn.fm/OPHC</a>. The anniversary celebration comes as the bank continues to emphasize its community-focused approach while maintaining its growth trajectory in the competitive financial services landscape, demonstrating how regional institutions can achieve significant scale while preserving their original mission and values.</p><p>The NYSE bell ringing ceremony serves as more than symbolic recognition for OptimumBank. It represents validation of the community banking model's viability in an era dominated by financial giants, showing that personalized service and local focus can drive substantial growth. For business leaders and financial industry observers, this milestone highlights the potential for regional banks to compete effectively while maintaining their distinctive character and customer relationships.</p><p>As financial services continue to consolidate, OptimumBank's 25-year journey from local lender to NYSE-recognized institution offers important lessons about sustainable growth strategies. The bank's ability to expand its footprint while staying true to its community banking origins provides a case study in balancing scale with service quality, a challenge facing many financial institutions in today's competitive environment.</p>
