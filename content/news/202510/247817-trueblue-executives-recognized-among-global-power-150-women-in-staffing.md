<p>TrueBlue President and CEO Taryn Owen and PeopleReady President Kristy Willis have been named to the Staffing Industry Analysts' Global Power 150 Women in Staffing list, recognizing the most influential women shaping the global staffing industry. This acknowledgment comes as TrueBlue implements strategic initiatives to address persistent workforce challenges across multiple sectors.</p><p>Jeff Sakaguchi, Board Chair of TrueBlue, stated that this recognition reflects the exceptional leadership both executives bring to the company and the industry. He emphasized their strategic approach to advancing TrueBlue's mission of connecting people with work while delivering lasting value for clients and workers. Under their leadership, TrueBlue has implemented several strategic initiatives to create greater value in a dynamic labor market.</p><p>The company has advanced its proprietary digitally enabled platforms to improve efficiency, enhance customer loyalty, and support long-term value creation. These technological advancements have been crucial in addressing the evolving needs of both employers and job seekers. TrueBlue has also expanded its sales capacity through strategic investments in operational structure, technology, partnerships, and enterprise alignment to support scalable growth.</p><p>This expansion comes at a critical time when businesses across multiple sectors face persistent labor shortages and workforce challenges. TrueBlue has strengthened its presence in high-value verticals such as healthcare and professional staffing while addressing labor shortages in skilled trades. This strategic focus allows the company to serve critical sectors where workforce needs are most acute, particularly as industries continue to recover and adapt to post-pandemic economic conditions.</p><p>Taryn Owen expressed that the honor reflects the collective efforts of TrueBlue's teams, who work daily to connect people with work opportunities while supporting businesses and strengthening communities. Both Owen and Willis acknowledged their teams' steadfast support as fundamental to their recognition. As The People Company, TrueBlue continues to build talent solutions for the changing world of work, connecting people with opportunity through precision, speed, and scale. The company's approach combines decades of industry expertise with innovative digital platforms to address complex workforce challenges. More information about TrueBlue's workforce solutions can be found at <a href="https://www.trueblue.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.trueblue.com</a>.</p>
