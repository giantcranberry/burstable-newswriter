<p>tZERO Group, Inc., a blockchain-powered multi-asset infrastructure company, has appointed Shobhit Khandelwal to its Board of Directors, bringing significant expertise in technology strategy, distributed systems, and applied artificial intelligence. This appointment comes as tZERO executes its strategic reset following Alan Konevsky's recent appointment as Chief Executive Officer, positioning the company to enhance its technological capabilities and market position.</p><p>Khandelwal's background as founder and CEO of ShyftLabs, a global data and AI product company, and Carter, a privacy-first retail media platform, provides tZERO with proven leadership in scaling advanced analytics solutions across retail, government, and healthcare sectors. His existing relationships with tZERO's investors and partners, including Bed Bath & Beyond, Inc., create immediate strategic alignment as the company accelerates its development timeline.</p><p>CEO Alan Konevsky emphasized the strategic importance of this appointment, stating that Khandelwal's fluency with disciplines central to building compliant, high-performance marketplaces and experience integrating AI and data-driven automation into regulated environments will help transform, scale and deepen collaboration with shareholders and strategic partners. This expertise becomes increasingly critical as tZERO evolves its technology stack and expands its multi-asset digital infrastructure both in the US and globally.</p><p>The board addition reinforces tZERO's commitment to bridging blockchain innovation with regulatory integrity and technical excellence. Khandelwal will provide guidance on how emerging technologies—including AI, data interoperability, and tokenization frameworks—can enhance tZERO's product roadmap and global reach. The company operates through its broker-dealer subsidiaries, including tZERO Digital Asset Securities, LLC, which is registered with the SEC and a member of FINRA and SIPC. More information about tZERO Digital Asset Securities may be found on <a href="https://brokercheck.finra.org" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">FINRA's BrokerCheck</a>.</p><p>Board Chairman Matt Mosman highlighted the timing of this appointment, noting that having someone with Khandelwal's technical depth and product vision adds tremendous value at this inflection point for tZERO. His experience scaling data-driven organizations will guide tZERO as it continues building institutional infrastructure to support tokenized assets and other on-chain financial products.</p><p>Marcus Lemonis, Executive Chairman and Principal Executive Officer of Bed Bath & Beyond, Inc., tZERO's largest investor, pointed to Khandelwal's previous collaborative experience and expertise in scalable architectures and emerging technologies as bringing a valuable new dimension to the board. Khandelwal's educational background from Columbia University and leadership roles at Citigroup, Walmart, and Jet.com, where he specialized in embedding intelligence and automation into enterprise operations, further strengthens tZERO's governance and strategic direction.</p><p>For institutional investors and market participants, this board appointment signals tZERO's intensified focus on integrating artificial intelligence and advanced data analytics into its regulated marketplace for digital securities and real-world assets. As blockchain infrastructure becomes increasingly sophisticated, the combination of Khandelwal's AI expertise with tZERO's existing regulatory compliance framework positions the company to lead in developing next-generation financial products and services. More information about tZERO's institutional-grade technology can be found at their <a href="https://www.tzero.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">website</a>.</p>
