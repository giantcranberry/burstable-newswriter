<p>Safe & Green Holdings Corp. (NASDAQ: SGBX) and its subsidiary Olenox Corp. have reaffirmed their Open Collaborative Framework partnership with global technology firm OneQode in response to a major Amazon Web Services outage that disrupted millions of users worldwide. The collaboration aims to merge OneQode's high-performance digital infrastructure with Olenox's field operations to improve automation, communication reliability, and environmental safety across digital networks.</p><p>Michael McLaren, CEO of Safe & Green, emphasized the critical nature of reliable data collection and two-way communication in field operations. It is critical to Olenox that data collection and two-way communication in the field is reliable, McLaren stated, highlighting the partnership's focus on addressing vulnerabilities exposed by recent infrastructure failures. This emphasis on reliability comes as businesses increasingly depend on uninterrupted digital operations for critical functions.</p><p>OneQode CEO Matt Shearing pointed to fundamental flaws in current internet infrastructure, noting that much of today's systems remain outdated and vulnerable to widespread disruptions. Shearing explained that OneQode's platform was built fundamentally different to prevent such failures and support next-generation operational performance, positioning the partnership as a proactive solution to systemic infrastructure weaknesses. This approach addresses growing concerns about the fragility of traditional cloud services that have demonstrated susceptibility to cascading failures.</p><p>The reaffirmed partnership comes as companies increasingly seek resilient alternatives to traditional cloud services following high-profile outages. The collaboration leverages Olenox's position as a vertically integrated energy company with Safe & Green Holdings' expertise in modular solutions development. Additional information about Safe & Green Holdings is available in the company's newsroom at <a href="https://ibn.fm/SGBX" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://ibn.fm/SGBX</a>.</p><p>Safe & Green Holdings operates as a leading modular solutions company with core capabilities including the development, design, and fabrication of modular structures. The company supports third-party and in-house developers, architects, builders, and owners in achieving faster execution, greener construction, and higher-value buildings through its innovative approaches to infrastructure development. This partnership represents a strategic move toward creating more resilient digital ecosystems that can withstand the increasing demands of modern business operations while maintaining environmental sustainability standards.</p>
