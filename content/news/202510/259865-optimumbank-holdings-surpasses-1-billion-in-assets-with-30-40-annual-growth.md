<p>OptimumBank Holdings, Inc. Chairman Moishe Gubin recently discussed the community bank's significant growth milestones and strategic direction during an appearance on The GotStocks Podcast by IBN. Gubin revealed that OptimumBank has surpassed $1 billion in assets while achieving consistent annual growth rates of 30-40% over recent years, underscoring the bank's successful community banking model in an era dominated by large financial institutions.</p><p>The chairman emphasized OptimumBank's commitment to traditional, in-person banking services combined with modern digital conveniences, describing this hybrid approach as the optimum way to bank. This balanced strategy represents a significant development for business leaders evaluating banking partnerships, as it offers the personal service of community banking with the technological convenience that modern businesses require. Gubin highlighted the institution's strong balance sheet, noting the absence of debt as a key financial strength that positions the bank for continued expansion through either equity or debt opportunities.</p><p>Founded in 2000 by local Broward County businessmen, OptimumBank was established with the specific mission of bringing back true community banking amid the rise of out-of-state mega-banks. From its inception, the bank invited local investors of all sizes to participate in its growth and mission, fostering a sense of community ownership and engagement. The bank's focus on personal service, reasonable fees, and deep expertise in real estate and commercial lending has resonated with customers seeking alternatives to larger financial institutions.</p><p>OptimumBank's financial solutions include comprehensive Business Banking, Business Lending, SBA Lending Solutions, Treasury Management, and Personal Banking services. The company's commitment to fostering economic development while maintaining community-first values has been instrumental in its rapid growth.
