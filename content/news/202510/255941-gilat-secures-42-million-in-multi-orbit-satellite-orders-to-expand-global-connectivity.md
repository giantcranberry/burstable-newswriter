<p>Gilat Satellite Networks Ltd. has secured $42 million in orders from a leading satellite operator for its multi-orbit SkyEdge IV platform, with systems scheduled for delivery over the next 12 months. The substantial investment will significantly expand global deployment of Gilat's technology as satellite operators worldwide seek to enable next-generation connectivity across multiple orbits to meet escalating demand for broadband services.</p><p>The SkyEdge IV platform supports critical applications including In-Flight Connectivity, maritime mobility, enterprise broadband, and cellular backhaul. The growing adoption of in-flight connectivity services and rapid rollout of free Wi-Fi on commercial flights are driving substantial global investment in satellite infrastructure. The platform's scalability, flexibility, and operational efficiency allow operators to meet increasing passenger and enterprise demand for seamless, high-speed broadband connectivity worldwide.</p><p>These orders demonstrate the strong confidence satellite operators have in Gilat's ground segment technology as they expand their capabilities to address the growing demand for global connectivity solutions. SkyEdge IV continues to power the expansion of In-Flight Connectivity and other high-growth mobility applications, driving the evolution of the connected world across multiple industries and geographic regions.</p><p>Gilat's comprehensive portfolio includes cloud-based platforms and modems, high-performance satellite terminals, advanced Satellite On-the-Move antennas, and integrated ground systems serving both commercial and defense markets. The company's products support multiple critical applications including government and defense operations, mobility services, broadband access, cellular backhaul, and enterprise clients while meeting stringent service level requirements. For additional details about Gilat's technology offerings, visit <a href="http://www.gilat.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">http://www.gilat.com</a>.</p><p>The $42 million investment represents a significant milestone in the satellite communications industry, reflecting the accelerating transition toward multi-orbit connectivity solutions. This expansion comes at a crucial time when global demand for reliable, high-speed connectivity is surging across aviation, maritime, and enterprise sectors. The deployment of SkyEdge IV technology over the next year will enable satellite operators to deliver enhanced services to customers worldwide, potentially transforming how businesses and consumers access connectivity in remote locations and during transit.</p><p>For business and technology leaders, this development signals continued growth in satellite infrastructure investment and highlights the strategic importance of multi-orbit capabilities in meeting future connectivity demands. The orders position Gilat as a key enabler of next-generation satellite services that will support digital transformation across multiple industries, from aviation to telecommunications to enterprise operations.</p>
