<p>GlobalTech Corp. (OTC: GLTK), a diversified technology holding company, has secured $1.4 million through a convertible note private placement to advance its artificial intelligence and data solutions across multiple industries. The funding will support the company's expansion into high-growth international markets and development of AI-powered solutions while exploring additional acquisition opportunities.</p><p>The company's diverse portfolio spans finance, compliance, retail, sports, recruitment, and eLearning sectors, with several standout platforms driving its growth strategy. Among these platforms is Cadnz, a lending lifecycle management system designed specifically for banks, which helps financial institutions streamline their lending operations. Another key platform, ThrivoAI, serves as a business intelligence and performance optimization tool for various industries seeking data-driven insights.</p><p>GlobalTech's sports technology offering includes Baseball Blitz, a digital hub that supports baseball operations while enhancing fan engagement through data analytics and digital experiences. The company's strategic approach combines targeted acquisitions with regional alliances to build its market presence across different technology sectors. According to the company's <a href="https://ibn.fm/GLTK" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">newsroom</a>, GlobalTech continues to focus on empowering visionary companies and innovation-led enterprises to drive transformation across global markets.</p><p>The $1.4 million capital infusion represents a significant step in the company's broader strategy to strengthen its position in competitive technology markets while developing next-generation AI solutions for diverse industry applications. As a U.S.-based technology holding company, GlobalTech operates through strategic partnerships and capital investment to expand its influence in the rapidly evolving technology landscape.</p><p>The company's diversified approach allows it to leverage cross-industry insights and technological innovations, potentially creating synergies between its various platform offerings. This multi-sector strategy positions GlobalTech to capitalize on emerging opportunities in both established and developing technology markets while addressing the growing demand for AI and data-driven solutions across business sectors. The funding announcement follows the company's ongoing commitment to enabling growth in artificial intelligence, big data, and frontier technologies.</p>
