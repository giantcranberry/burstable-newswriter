Learning needs to go beyond content delivery to deliver experiences that truly change behavior, Hynes stated. By integrating ARQ's learning engineering and immersive storytelling expertise into our platform, we're enabling our clients to deliver smarter, more engaging learning that scales and performs.</p><p>The partnership enables organizations to access immersive digital learning experiences developed using ARQ's learning engineering approach and authored with tools like Intellum Evolve, a key component of ARQ's immersive content technology suite. We're excited to collaborate with Intellum to showcase how immersive design, AI, and data-driven learning can coexist across modern ecosystems, they said. This partnership underscores our shared commitment to advancing the science of learning while maintaining openness, interoperability, and cross-platform collaboration at the heart of our technology approach.</p><p>The partnership represents a significant advancement in corporate learning technology by combining Intellum's AI-first platform available at <a href="https://www.intellum.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.intellum.com</a> with ARQ Innovation's specialized expertise in learning engineering and immersive design accessible through <a href="https://www.arq-innovation.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.arq-innovation.com</a>.
