<p>BMP AI Technologies Inc. has confirmed it is addressing a Caveat Emptor designation applied by OTC Markets Group on October 22, 2025. The company stated it has been in active communication with OTC Markets to understand the basis for the designation, which relates to the review of the company's publicly available financial information and recent market activity in its securities.</p><p>OTC Markets has provided BMP AI Technologies with a detailed list of supplemental information requested from the company. BMP AI Technologies intends to furnish all such information promptly and expects to complete its response within the week. The company reiterated that it has not authorized or engaged any third parties to conduct promotional, marketing, or investor relations campaigns on its behalf.</p><p>The company remains fully committed to transparency, regulatory compliance, and accurate disclosure, and is cooperating fully with OTC Markets to expedite the removal of the caveat emptor designation. OTC Markets has informed the company that such designations are generally maintained for a 30-day period; however, under the circumstances, the company and its legal counsel will be requesting that the 30-day policy be waived and that the designation be removed upon verification of the requested supplemental information.</p><p>A spokesperson for BMPA stated, "We are aware of the designation and are taking this matter very seriously. Our team is fully committed to cooperating with OTC Markets and ensuring our compliance. We will continue to keep our shareholders informed as we work through this process." The company confirms that its operations and SEC filings remain current. Additional information about BMP AI Technologies Inc. is available at <a href="https://www.bmpaitech.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.bmpaitech.com</a> or by visiting <a href="https://www.sec.gov" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.sec.gov</a>.</p><p>This development comes as BMP AI Technologies positions itself as a next-generation artificial intelligence and blockchain development company specializing in enterprise automation, data integrity, and decentralized business solutions. The company's technology alliances deliver transformative products including HeartEase (AI-powered health diagnostics), BMP AI (enterprise chatbots), and proprietary blockchain platforms designed for secure information distribution and internal enterprise payments.</p><p>The Caveat Emptor designation represents a significant regulatory consideration for investors and the company alike, potentially affecting market perception and trading activity. The company's prompt response and commitment to providing requested information demonstrate its focus on maintaining regulatory compliance and market confidence during this period of review. For technology companies operating in regulated markets, such designations can impact investor confidence and market liquidity, making swift resolution critical for maintaining business momentum and stakeholder trust.</p>
