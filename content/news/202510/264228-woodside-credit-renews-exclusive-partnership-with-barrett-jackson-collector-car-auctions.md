<p>Woodside Credit has renewed its exclusive partnership with Barrett-Jackson, maintaining its position as the sole financing provider for The World's Greatest Collector Car Auctions. Now entering its 14th year, this extended collaboration builds upon a longstanding relationship between the two industry leaders, making vehicle financing more accessible for Barrett-Jackson bidders through Woodside Credit's streamlined processes.</p><p>The partnership's success was recently demonstrated at Barrett-Jackson's Fall Auction at WestWorld of Scottsdale, where the event drew tens of thousands of attendees and bidders, resulting in over one million dollars in collector car loans financed through Woodside Credit. The company maintains a full team of underwriters, loan experts, and support staff at every Barrett-Jackson auction location, providing comprehensive onsite services including vehicle verifications, loan processing, and coordination of gate releases.</p><p>Mitch Shatzen, President and Chief Operating Officer of Woodside Credit, emphasized the strategic importance of the partnership, stating that their industry-leading loan program performs optimally when paired with events representing the heart of the enthusiast community. The collaboration ensures financing remains straightforward and provides an exceptional experience for clients throughout the purchasing process.</p><p>Woodside Credit will return to WestWorld of Scottsdale for Barrett-Jackson's flagship auction scheduled for January 17-25, 2026, continuing support for both new and returning bidders. A significant enhancement to the partnership allows bidders to initiate Woodside Credit pre-approval directly within the Barrett-Jackson bidder registration process at <a href="https://Barrett-Jackson.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">Barrett-Jackson.com</a>, with early registration pricing available through December 1, 2025.</p><p>With over $4 billion in loans since its establishment in 2003, Woodside Credit has solidified its position as America's premier classic and collector car financing provider. The company offers terms up to 15 years and loans reaching $1,000,000, providing unparalleled quality through commonsense lending practices detailed at <a href="https://WoodsideCredit.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">WoodsideCredit.com</a>. This exclusive endorsement by Barrett-Jackson underscores Woodside Credit's dedication to supporting dealerships, enthusiasts, and collectors nationwide while driving growth in the specialty finance sector of the collector and exotic car marketplace.</p>
