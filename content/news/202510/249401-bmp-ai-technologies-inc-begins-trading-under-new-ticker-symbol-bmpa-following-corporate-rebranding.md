<p>BMP AI Technologies Inc. (OTC: BMPA), formerly known as NeuralBase AI Ltd., announced that its common stock began trading under the new ticker symbol "BMPA" on October 9, 2025. The change follows the company's corporate name change from NeuralBase AI Ltd. to BMP AI Technologies Inc., with both changes becoming effective on October 8, 2025, as announced by the Financial Industry Regulatory Authority (FINRA). The company's CUSIP number remains unchanged despite these corporate identity updates.</p><p>Vighnesh Dobale, Chief Executive Officer of BMP AI Technologies Inc., described the ticker change as an "exciting and important step" that aligns the company's market identity with its corporate brand. "We look forward to the future as we continue to build out our company and deliver innovative AI and blockchain solutions to the enterprise market," Dobale stated. He emphasized that this represents a significant milestone in the company's development and reflects its commitment to driving long-term value for shareholders under the new symbol.</p><p>The rebranding coincides with what Dobale characterized as an "incredibly dynamic phase of growth and innovation" for the company. He noted that the company's new website at <a href="https://www.bmpaitech.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.bmpaitech.com</a>, while still under development, is expected to be completed soon and will showcase the cutting-edge technology being developed. The website will highlight the transformative impact of the company's AI and blockchain solutions across multiple industries, positioning BMP AI at the forefront of the enterprise technology revolution.</p><p>Additional information about BMP AI Technologies Inc. is available through the SEC's EDGAR database at <a href="https://www.sec.gov" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.sec.gov</a>, where the company files required disclosures as a fully reporting entity. The company specializes in enterprise automation, data integrity, and decentralized business solutions through its technology alliances. Its product portfolio includes HeartEase, an AI-powered health diagnostics platform, BMP AI enterprise chatbots, and proprietary blockchain platforms designed for secure information distribution and internal enterprise payments.</p><p>The corporate rebranding signals BMP AI Technologies' strategic positioning in the rapidly evolving enterprise technology landscape. For business leaders and investors, this move represents the company's commitment to establishing a distinct identity in the competitive AI and blockchain markets. The timing of this rebranding during what the CEO describes as a "dynamic phase of growth" suggests potential expansion and innovation initiatives that could impact enterprise technology adoption across industries. The company's focus on enterprise automation and decentralized solutions addresses growing business needs for operational efficiency and secure data management, positioning it to capitalize on the increasing demand for integrated AI and blockchain technologies in corporate environments.</p><p>The company continues to trade on the OTC Markets, where investing involves significant risk including potential loss of principal, low liquidity, high volatility, and limited publicly available information. Investors are advised to conduct thorough due diligence and consult qualified investment advisors before making any investment decisions.</p>
