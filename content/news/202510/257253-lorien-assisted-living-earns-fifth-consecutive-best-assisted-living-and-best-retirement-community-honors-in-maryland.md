<p>Lorien Assisted Living has secured the 'Top Winner' designation in two categories of the Daily Record's 2025 Reader Rankings Survey, marking the fifth straight year the family-owned company has received this distinction. The Maryland-based organization earned honors as 'Best Assisted Living' and 'Best Retirement Community' in the state, underscoring its ongoing dedication to quality care and community engagement.</p><p>'The results of the Daily Record's Reader Rankings survey highlight our steadfast commitment to our residents, their families, and to the positive growth of Lorien Assisted Living,' stated Lou Grimmel Sr., Chief Executive Officer of Lorien Health Services. This recognition aligns with the launch of Harmony Park, an innovative memory care neighborhood in Howard County that integrates outdoor elements indoors to establish a soothing, park-like atmosphere for residents with dementia.</p><p>John Mangione Jr., Chief Operating Officer of Lorien Health Services, emphasized the award's importance, noting, 'We are proud to be recognized by Marylanders as a leader in delivering high-quality healthcare services. Our focus on personalized care and innovation motivates us to continually improve the well-being of those we serve.' The Daily Record Reader Rankings process involves nomination and voting phases, culminating in an annual celebration where top winners are announced, with this year's event set for October 20.</p><p>In addition to memory care, Lorien Assisted Living communities throughout Maryland offer specialized services such as on-site dialysis, Parkinson's care, Korean senior care, and residential home care services via <a href="https://www.lorienhealth.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">Lorien at Home</a>, with more services planned for 2026. The consistent accolades over five years demonstrate Lorien's sustained excellence in a competitive healthcare environment, which is increasingly vital as Maryland's aging population expands and requires tailored care options.</p><p>The Daily Record, with 134 years of editorial excellence, is a component of <a href="https://www.bridgetowermedia.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">BridgeTower Media</a>, one of the nation's foremost business-to-business media companies. Their Reader Rankings program honors Maryland's premier businesses and services through community nominations and voting, making Lorien's repeated success especially significant in reflecting public trust and satisfaction with their care offerings.</p>
