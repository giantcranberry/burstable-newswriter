<p>Whirlpool Corporation has been recognized as one of the Best Companies to Work For in Mexico and achieved the top ranking in the category of Best Companies to Work For Women. This recognition underscores the company's ongoing commitment to creating an inclusive workplace environment where employees feel valued and empowered to contribute to the organization's mission of improving life at home.</p><p>The designation reflects Whirlpool's dedication to building a workplace culture that supports all employees, particularly highlighting the company's success in creating opportunities and support systems for women in the workforce. As a leading home appliance company, Whirlpool Corporation maintains a significant presence in the Americas, with approximately 90% of its $17 billion in annual sales coming from the region. The company employs 44,000 people worldwide and operates 40 manufacturing and technology research centers globally.</p><p>Whirlpool's recognition in Mexico aligns with the company's broader purpose of Improving Life at Home, extending this philosophy to how it treats and develops its workforce. The company's iconic brand portfolio includes Whirlpool, KitchenAid, JennAir, Maytag, Amana, Brastemp, Consul, and InSinkErator, all benefiting from the innovation driven by its diverse workforce. Additional information about the company's operations and corporate initiatives can be found at <a href="https://WhirlpoolCorp.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://WhirlpoolCorp.com</a>.</p><p>This workplace recognition carries significant implications for both the company and the broader manufacturing sector in Mexico. As the only major U.S.-based manufacturer of kitchen and laundry appliances, Whirlpool's success in creating an inclusive workplace sets an important precedent for other multinational corporations operating in the region. The #1 ranking for women employees demonstrates concrete progress in gender equality within traditionally male-dominated manufacturing industries.</p><p>The recognition also highlights the connection between workplace culture and business performance. By fostering an environment where employees feel valued and empowered, Whirlpool positions itself to attract and retain top talent, driving innovation across its product portfolio. This approach supports the company's ability to meet evolving consumer needs while maintaining its competitive edge in the global home appliance market.</p>
