<p>Duos Technologies Group, Inc. President Doug Recker and Chief Financial Officer Adrian Goldfarb will present at the 19th Annual LD Micro Main Event on Tuesday, October 21st at 2:30 PM PT at the Hotel del Coronado. The joint presentation will focus on the company's continued operational progress, growth trajectory, and expansion across Edge Data Centers and digital infrastructure sectors, providing investors with insight into Duos Technologies' strategic direction and performance metrics.</p><p>Management will be available for one-on-one sessions following the presentation, offering investors direct access to discuss the company's financial outlook, capital strategy, and long-term value creation initiatives. These private meetings represent a significant opportunity for detailed financial analysis and strategic understanding of the company's position in the technology marketplace. The LD Micro Main Event XIX, running from October 19th to the 21st at the Hotel del Coronado in San Diego, California, features approximately 120 companies presenting in half-hour increments. For more information about LD Micro, visit <a href="https://www.ldmicro.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.ldmicro.com</a>.</p><p>Duos Technologies operates through its subsidiaries including Duos Technologies, Inc., Duos Edge AI, Inc., and Duos Energy Corporation. The company specializes in designing, developing, deploying and operating intelligent technology solutions for Machine Vision and Artificial Intelligence applications, including real-time analysis of fast-moving vehicles, Edge Data Centers and power consulting. Additional company information is available at <a href="https://www.duostech.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.duostech.com</a>, <a href="https://www.duosedge.ai" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.duosedge.ai</a>, and <a href="https://www.duosenergycorp.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.duosenergycorp.com</a>.</p><p>The company's participation in the LD Micro Main Event represents a strategic effort to communicate directly with the investment community during a period of significant technological transformation in edge computing and AI infrastructure. The Edge Data Center market represents a growing segment within digital infrastructure, with increasing demand driven by expansion of IoT devices, 5G networks, and real-time data processing requirements across multiple industries. This positioning matters for business leaders as edge computing becomes increasingly critical for supporting low-latency applications, autonomous systems, and distributed AI workloads that require processing closer to data sources.</p><p>Investor conferences like the LD Micro Main Event serve as important platforms for publicly traded companies to articulate their growth strategies and operational achievements to potential investors and financial analysts. The ability to conduct one-on-one meetings allows for deeper discussions about specific financial metrics, market positioning, and competitive advantages that may not be fully addressed in public presentations alone. For technology executives and investors, understanding companies like Duos Technologies provides insight into how traditional infrastructure companies are adapting to the demands of AI-driven automation and edge computing requirements that are reshaping multiple industries from transportation to energy management.</p>
