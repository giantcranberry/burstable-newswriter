<p>Stevens-Leinweber Construction has appointed DJ Peterson as Business Development Lead to strengthen the company's position in Phoenix's rapidly evolving commercial real estate market. Peterson will focus on building client relationships and identifying growth opportunities by leveraging the company's four decades of construction expertise in ground-up and tenant improvement projects.</p><p>Phoenix-based Stevens-Leinweber Construction President and CEO Jamie Godwin emphasized the strategic importance of this appointment, noting that Phoenix has changed dramatically over the decades, and the company has succeeded by growing in step with the market. Godwin described the current market as now including advanced manufacturing, ultra-sophisticated logistics and highly creative office environments. He characterized Peterson as a seasoned relationship manager, collaborator and continuous learner who is equally committed to honoring the company's past while embracing the future.</p><p>Peterson brings a unique background to the construction industry, having previously worked as a baseball agent and spending nearly a decade as a minor league player with organizations including the Mariners, Reds, White Sox and Rockies. His professional network includes membership in the Arizona Chapter of NAIOP, Greater Phoenix Economic Council and ULI, positioning him to effectively navigate the complex commercial real estate landscape. Peterson stated that strong relationships start with understanding and then evolve into strategies that drive meaningful results, expressing excitement about applying the company's core values and expertise for the benefit of clients and communities.</p><p>The appointment comes as Stevens-Leinweber Construction continues to expand its footprint in the Valley's commercial construction sector. The company has completed more than 9,500 tenant improvement projects in the Phoenix metro area over its 40-year history and currently has more than 17 million square feet of ground-up Class A office and industrial space either completed or in its pipeline. Additional information about the company's services can be found at <a href="https://stevensleinweber.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://stevensleinweber.com</a>.</p><p>This strategic hire signals Stevens-Leinweber Construction's commitment to maintaining its competitive edge in a market experiencing significant transformation. The company provides both general contracting and design-build services across office, industrial, retail and mixed-use sectors, earning repeated selection for prominent Valley projects through its established reputation among architects, designers, owners and developers. For business leaders and industry observers, this appointment represents how established construction firms are adapting their leadership and business development strategies to capitalize on Phoenix's economic diversification beyond traditional commercial real estate into advanced manufacturing and logistics sectors that require specialized expertise and relationship management capabilities.</p>
