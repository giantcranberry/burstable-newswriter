<p>WAYS1, a South Korea-based smart mobility technology company, will participate in the Thailand Smart City Expo 2025, scheduled for November 5-7 at QSNCC Bangkok. The company plans to unveil an on-site demonstration of LDM TWIN, an AI-powered digital twin traffic management solution currently being piloted in Thailand. The exhibition booth will feature a live demonstration showcasing a realistic 3D digital twin model of intersections in Thailand's Nonthaburi region, allowing visitors to experience how real-time data from vehicles, motorcycles, and pedestrians is collected, analyzed, and visualized within the system.</p><p>The demonstration will enable participants to witness next-generation data-driven traffic control and visualization technologies, offering insights into the future of smart city mobility. WAYS1's booth is expected to attract representatives from municipal governments, transportation authorities, and system integration partners across Thailand and other countries. Discussions during the event will focus on LDM TWIN's scalability, data quality validation, and international collaboration opportunities for future smart mobility applications. The company's participation in the Thailand Smart City Expo 2025 represents a significant milestone in its expansion across the ASEAN region.</p><p>Seungho Hwang, CEO of WAYS1, emphasized the system's core functionality, stating that at the core of LDM TWIN is real-time monitoring powered by on-site data. He explained that by integrating high-definition maps, real-time perception, and signal coordination, the system enables seamless visualization and analysis of traffic conditions in real time. The company plans to demonstrate a model specifically optimized for ASEAN urban environments during the exhibition, highlighting the technology's regional applicability.</p><p>By introducing the LDM TWIN system to Thailand, WAYS1 aims to foster cross-border collaboration in AI-driven traffic management, smart infrastructure, and sustainable urban planning. The initiative underscores the company's commitment to advancing digital transformation for smart cities, supporting governments and industry partners in building safer, more efficient, and environmentally conscious transportation ecosystems. Throughout the expo, WAYS1 will operate at Booth H14, hosting live demonstrations, business meetings, and partner consultations. Visitors will experience interactive simulations and explore potential applications for smart city infrastructure across ASEAN regions, demonstrating how LDM TWIN integrates digital twin technology with real-time data to enhance traffic management and urban mobility. More information about the company's technology offerings can be found at <a href="https://www.ways1.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.ways1.com</a>.</p>
