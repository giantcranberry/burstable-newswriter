<p>Sono-Tek Corporation will present at Spartan Capital Securities' Second Annual Investor Conference on November 3, 2025, at the Marriott Marquis Hotel in New York City. The company's participation in this prestigious event signals its position as an innovative company positioned for long-term growth in the specialized coating technology sector.</p><p>CEO Steve Harshbarger emphasized the significance of this opportunity, stating that the company is honored to share its growth story with a distinguished audience of investors and peers. The conference provides a strategic platform to highlight Sono-Tek's progress and strengthen relationships with both existing and potential new investors.</p><p>Spartan Capital Securities Founder and CEO John D. Lowry expressed delight in featuring Sono-Tek among the conference's carefully selected companies. The event will showcase more than 60 companies across technology, healthcare, consumer, mining, precious metals, and other high-growth sectors, with over 500 institutional and high-net-worth investors expected to attend. For conference details, visit <a href="https://spartancapital.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://spartancapital.com</a>.</p><p>Sono-Tek's ultrasonic coating systems represent a critical technology for applying thin films across multiple high-growth industries. The company maintains a strong position in medical device manufacturing, where its precision coating solutions enable life-saving technologies including stents, balloons, diagnostic devices, and various drug delivery platforms. This established medical technology foundation provides stable revenue streams while the company expands into new markets.</p><p>The semiconductor and microelectronics sectors represent significant growth opportunities for Sono-Tek as customers increasingly adopt the company's solutions for next-generation chips, displays, and sensors. This expansion comes at a crucial time when global semiconductor manufacturing requires more sophisticated coating technologies to support advancing computational and connectivity demands.</p><p>Clean energy applications present another substantial growth vector, with Sono-Tek's technologies leading development in fuel cells, carbon capture systems, advanced solar cells, and various other industrial applications. The company's evolution from research and development tools to high-volume production machines with significantly higher average selling prices demonstrates market validation and scalability.</p><p>Sono-Tek's strategic market approach utilizes a multi-channel distribution network including direct sales personnel, independent distributors, and experienced sales representatives. This diversified approach ensures efficient market penetration across diverse sectors globally while maintaining flexibility to adapt to regional market conditions.</p><p>The Spartan Capital Investor Conference will feature main-stage presentations, expert panels, curated one-on-one meetings, and extensive networking sessions designed to facilitate meaningful dialogue between growth companies and the investment community. For Sono-Tek, this represents an opportunity to showcase its technological advancements and strategic positioning across multiple high-value industrial sectors.</p><p>Industry observers note that Sono-Tek's presence at such conferences typically signals companies approaching significant growth phases or preparing for expanded market presence. The company's diversified application portfolio across medical technology, semiconductors, and clean energy positions it to benefit from multiple technological megatrends simultaneously, potentially reducing sector-specific risk while maximizing growth opportunities.</p>
