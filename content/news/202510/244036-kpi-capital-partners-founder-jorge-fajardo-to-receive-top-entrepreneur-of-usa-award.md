<p>Jorge Fajardo, founder and president of KPI Capital Partners, will receive the Top Entrepreneur of USA award during a ceremony in Miami on October 9, 2025. This recognition celebrates visionary leaders who have promoted economic growth, innovation and business development in the country, highlighting Fajardo's trajectory as a promoter of strategic investment projects and creator of opportunities across various sectors.</p><p>The award ceremony, organized by Negocios and Ejecutiva Magazine, will take place at the EB Hotel in Miami during Hispanic Heritage Month. These publications maintain active online presence through their websites at <a href="https://www.negociosmagazine.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.negociosmagazine.com</a> and <a href="https://www.ejecutivamagazine.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.ejecutivamagazine.com</a>. The magazines are leaders in information and interviews with executives in southern Florida, featuring daily news updates and extensive social media content distribution.</p><p>Fajardo's recognition extends beyond his business success to his commitment to the Latin community. As a Colombian immigrant who grew up in the American education system, he has combined personal experience with professional knowledge to empower others. Through educational programs, he has trained aspiring investors in real estate, sharing his experience and knowledge to help others achieve success.</p><p>The Top Entrepreneur of USA awards honor business leaders who have demonstrated exceptional performance and leadership in their respective fields. These awards highlight the dedication, innovation and positive impact that entrepreneurs have generated in their communities and beyond. Recognition as one of the best entrepreneurs not only celebrates professional success but also inspires others who aspire to excel on their own paths.</p><p>The event will feature a cocktail reception followed by a formal dinner, creating an environment for networking and celebrating achievements. Journalist and businesswoman Sabina Covo, founder of Covo Communications, will serve as master of ceremonies. Fajardo will be honored alongside other distinguished entrepreneurs including Claudia Marquez of Hyundai Motor America, German Pugliese-Bassi of Persea Capital Ventures, and Rafael Marrero of Rafael Marrero & Company.</p><p>Negocios Magazine is also distributed through Pressreader, a digital platform with more than 7,000 publications worldwide, accessible through hotels, companies, airplanes and cruises. The magazines are additionally published on the ISSUU.com platform where complete online publications can be visualized. The publication is part of the media group Diurna Urbis Group Corp. based in Miami, Florida, which also publishes several other Hispanic-focused publications.</p><p>The Top Entrepreneur of USA ceremony serves as both a platform to honor success and an opportunity to promote new alliances and collaborations. This annual event promises to be a memorable celebration of achievements, innovation and leadership within the United States entrepreneurial community, particularly highlighting the contributions of Hispanic business leaders during Hispanic Heritage Month.</p>
