<p>Lee's Famous Recipe Chicken, a venerable name in the fast-food industry known for its home-style fried chicken, is once again in the running for USA Today's 10Best Readers' Choice Awards for the Best Fast Food Fried Chicken. This nomination for the 2025 awards marks the brand's third consecutive year in the competition, following its impressive top 10 finishes in both 2023 and 2024. The voting period is now open and will conclude on July 7, offering fans a chance to support their favorite fried chicken brand.</p><p>Ryan Weaver, CEO of Lee's Famous Recipe Chicken, has expressed immense pride in the nomination, seeing it as a reflection of the brand's unwavering commitment to quality and consistency. 'Recognitions like the USA Today's Top 10 Fast Food Fried Chicken reflect the pride and consistency our operators deliver every day,' Weaver remarked. The brand is rallying its customer base to participate in the voting, hoping to secure a historic third consecutive win.</p><p>With a rich history spanning nearly 60 years, Lee's Famous Recipe Chicken has carved out a niche for itself with its fresh, never frozen, and hand-breaded chicken offerings. The menu boasts a wide array of choices, from bone-in chicken and breast strips to sandwiches and classic Southern-style sides. Operating over 130 locations across 12 U.S. states and Canada, Lee's has established itself as a go-to destination for fried chicken enthusiasts.</p><p>The nomination comes at an opportune moment, aligning with National Fried Chicken Day on July 6. This celebration of one of America's favorite dishes, which enjoys an 83% popularity rating according to a YouGov survey, provides the perfect backdrop for Lee's to potentially add another accolade to its name. Customers are encouraged to celebrate by visiting Lee's locations and casting their votes online.</p><p>This year's nomination not only reaffirms Lee's Famous Recipe Chicken's reputation for delivering high-quality fast food but also underscores the fiercely competitive nature of the industry. A third consecutive win would not only bolster the brand's standing but also serve as a testament to its enduring appeal and operational excellence, setting a benchmark for others in the fast-food fried chicken category.</p>
